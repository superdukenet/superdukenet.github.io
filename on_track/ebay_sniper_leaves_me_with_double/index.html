Colonel_Klinck

2012-05-10 17:47:00 UTC

I decided it was time I bought some one piece leathers for the track. I spot some RST and Wolf leathers going. The RST have apparently only been worn twice, sliders are virgin. They are at the cheap end of RST range at only £300 new but get good reviews and the price was £100 with 3 hours left so I setup a snipe for them. The Wolf ones are spanking new, still with labels in them. Seller was given them as part of a deal on a new Suzuki according to the ad. They are nearer £600 new. With 12 hours left they are at £150 so a snipe is setup on those as well. I think I can always sell which ever pair I prefer on again if I do actually win both pairs.

I win the RST with last sec bid of £180
