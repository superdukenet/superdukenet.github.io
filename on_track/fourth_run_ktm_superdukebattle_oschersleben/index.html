Fourth run KTM Superdukebattle - Oschersleben

Tead

2010-06-14 15:00:00 UTC

This weekend it was time to open the throttle in Oschersleben

Saturday
1 training
2 qualifications

The training was in the morning. Weather was dry but a little cold. But thats fine
1 hour before the first qualification it started to rain... oh what a wonderful world
Only a few drivers have got a second set of wheels with raintyres and most of them hoped for dry weather in the second qualy so the track was pretty empty. I drove out just to make sure I've get a time to be able to start. Without raintyres

The second qualification was dry, everbody happy and it was time for some good laps.
I drove out with my old tyres, both soft but very used. I was able to drive a 1.36,3 which was a bad time for me but well...

Dirk set the pole with a 1.34,3min - very fast

On sunday I started from the second place, Dirk next to me.

We talked some bullshit in the morning - we always make fun about each other
I sticked a
And I sticked another sad smiley in his cockpit and told him not to remove it. He didnt :>

I told Dirk that I will enter the first corner in front of him and he just laughed. This dick
"You are a sucky starter"
Oh I will show him how bad I start

The first race was on sunday 12:15




I did it. The first corner was mine ;D Wonderful



The first 8 or 9 laps I lead the race
Suddenly at the end of the start/finish line Dirk showed me his front tyre and overtook me
In the next right corner he pointed at the back of his bike. I was like "What in the world is he showing to me? Does he want me to follow him? I was in front
of him for the whole race till now so what the..." - wait what is this. The cokpit smiley... oh this dick
I showed him my smiley on my bike the evening before and explained him that this smiley will demoralise him
Laughing in my helm I drove one lap behind him and was able to overtake him again. Crazy guy

I finished the race first. 0.181 sec in front of him. I was happy

Training and qualification



Race



The second race was at 3 o'clock

I had a good start again and we drove next to each other down the start/finish line - but he braked a little later and so he had the first corner
I had to laugh again It's so much fun to drive and fight with him

The second race was harder, he was in front of me a lot of laps but in the end I was able to finish 1.6sec in front of him again
