KTM Ride Orange Motocross Tryout Days

KTM UK is pleased to annouce the dates for its Ride Orange Motocross Tryout events. Giving you the chance to test the latest range of KTM SX models from the 2009 line up.

The Ride Orange Motocross Tryouts will tour the country from Septembe r to November and in conjunction with Authorised KTM dealers, offer you the chance to test any of the following models at some of the UK's most exclusive tracks.

125 SX - 150 SX - 250 SX - 250 SX-F - 450 SX-F

For your chance to take part or for more information, contact your local dealer from the list below. Contact details can be found on the dealer locator link below.

Date Venue Dealer
6th September Cwmythig Hill, Rhayader ET James
13th September Abram Motor Park, Wigan Robinsons of Rochdale
21st September Leuchars, Fife Drysdale Motorcycles
4th October East Trax, Chippenham Gear4 Motorcycles
