2011-08-27 11:33:00 UTC
I did Oulton Park with Focussed Events on Tuesday.
The circuit is fantastic on the SD - I loved it but Christ was I knackered afterwards.
I was a little concerned by the poor reputation of FE and things didn't start off too well - I had printed off the disclaimer form they email and filled that out beforehand, got to the front of the queue only to be told that I had to also fill another form for the circuit.
Then after the briefing which was OK, everything seemed to go contrary to what we had been told ie where to go, the order in which to get noise tested etc.
However, once we got on track all went smoothly, all sessions in our group went the full 20 minutes and everyone behaved well!
The sun even came out and it was a lovely day but I'm not giving FE credit for that.
They hire out cameras so are probably trying to protect their interests but I saw a few people with their own cams. Could you not secure yours with cable ties if safety/bits flying off is the issue?
