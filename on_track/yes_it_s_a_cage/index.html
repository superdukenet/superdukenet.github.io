Very nice, Mr. T, very nice. I'd have a supercharged RX8 for track days if I could figure out why the damn thing won't run!

I have to admit, this gave me a completely new appreciation for the BMW marque. I had only driven an M3 about 15 years ago, but got to do major seat time in both the M3 (sequential twin clutch manual/paddles) and the M6. Both were absolutely stellar. I never knew how good they are. Considering these were bone stock showroom tune cars with DOT tyres that carried a bunch of rookies around VIR all day long, I can't say enough about them.

Got two more dates at VIR coming up, one in Mercedes (AMG, AMG, please, AMG) and another in 'Vettes. As you can see, VIR is a real driver's track. I just watched the F1 race from Monaco; the AMG was the pace car; the in-car footage from the pace car was almost as stirring as the F1 footage.


Viva Internal Combustion!
