2014-04-20 21:34:00 UTC
Klinky, can you change the title of this to Spartan Racing please and ill keep all updates in here far the 3 1/2 guys that follow it


Not had time to do a proper update of my website but a quick rundown here.

Donington was going ok. Had a big ole change in everything over the winter. New job, sponsorship from a coach who has helped my training and mental prep stuff. Everything going super dooper. Went into this feeling bulletproof. Still finding my feet with the 600 having only ridden it a handfull of times. As usual tested on shagged rubber. If you can get them times out well used rubber when you put new boots on its far easier to get athe mosr of them. Especially with the SC's only having about 10 laps at 100% before starting to drop a bit. Got to 1.41's fannying about in trackday traffic.

New rear on for qualifying. Hard to get a clear lap in he Elite 600 field with 41 bikes with spotty young heros all going absolutley batshit crazy. Did 3 laps, couldnt get space, came in and got a mate on the pit wall to try and get me out in a space. So had 1 flying lap and got stuffed into turn 1 by Curtis (who is the Triumph test rider). Qualified 12th out of 41 with a 1.39.1. Went into race confident of low 38's which would run me top 10. Got away ok but was abit excited and trying too hard in first few laps and made some mistakes (see vid ). Arm pump set in, which ive got cured now as it was down to new leathers too tight on forearms and pegs too high so i couldn't use my legs enough.

Bad day overall though. Dropped to 18th in race. A felow rider was killed in the 500 race. Loads of big ones with a few mates injured. Dislocated collar bone, broken hand, broken arm, knocked out. Called a few sponsors and my girl on the sat night saying it didnt feel right and i had a bad vibe. Wanted to come home fresh and reset for next round. Got talked into staying by them all and a few felllow riders.

Sunday warmup made some changes to bike and felt better. Put better rear scrub on for race. Warmer was only on for 45min before we were out. Got a decent enough start, flicked left for craner and tried to drive round the outside to block pass a few of them into old hairpin and lost the rear. Caught it but when it came in it half highsided my out the seat and off the pegs then went out again and i came off the left side of the it. Just missed by another bike who hit my feet and left rubber on my boots! Pure luck i'm not spanked up bad. Bike stayed on its wheels and went left into the tyre wall at easily 100mph. Its not pretty.

This might be racing over for me. Will try and chip way at it to get it back. But its had a big hit.

TV coverage will be on Motrs TV on thursday evening at 8pm, then we will see what actuall happened to the bike coz i've got no idea what gymnastics it actually did.

Thanks to the guys who text me etc with well wishes Appreciate it.
