BASH69

The D209 was the previous tyre to the the SportMAX D211 GP, the SportSMART which you are running now is the `Do-it-all` road and track tyre and a very capable tyre.

Personally, unless running at the sharp end of the fast group id stick with you Sportsmarts unless they are down to the wear bars!

The track pressures for the Sportsmarts are 30psi front, 21psi rear - both cold pressures
