My first race ever...

Leonidas

2009-06-24 10:02:00 UTC

Well, maybe that's not the right place as it's not about the SDR, but then again maybe it is...

Last wk, i had my first ever race, not on the SDR though.
I raced the RC8

In the first place, i wanted to try the SDR, but i wanted to service my shock first and i did not have the time.

So, i went for the RC that was race ready

I 've only rode the RC on track a month ago and was really impressed by how easy it is to ride. The power difference is significant compared to the SDR, but still it is really easy and comfy to ride on track.

Best personal lap time on our local track (on the SDR) was 1:32,05.
After 2 days on the RC, where i have to say i was a bit conservative, best lap was 1:33,1. Not bad actually considering i 've never rode a Superbike before on track.

To cut the long story short, on Saturday, i had some problems getting the correct temperature on my tyres (Superbike slicks), but hopefully by the evening, i had it worked out.

On Sunday morning we had the qualifying session. From a Saturday low of 1:33,2, wearing a fresh pair of slicks, i got a flying 1:29,7 on the RC.

I 've always thought that the RC would be a difficult bike to ride compared to the SDR, but that is sooo untrue. The bike is awesome and it's easily compared to the rideability of the SuperDuke.

3 corners before the end i close my eyes, say all my prayers and dive into one of the quickest corners of the track carrying around 165 km/h and feeling the bike down to its limits in terms of lean angle (far away from the riders limits... ). Overtaking a gixxer 750 round the outside and managing to keep the rubber side up, i chase the last guy, the R1 seen above...

Hitting the apex of the last turn i was just behind him and opened the throttle as quickly as i could... Helmet was touching the tank and i was just peeping to see if the R1 is going to open up... Hopefully the RC worked perfectly and embarrassed the R1 overtaking it just before the end of teh race. It must have been 30cm or smt like that

I was sooooo happy... Leaving the start out of the picture, my first race was quite a blast and i enjoyed it to the max...

It was only when i returned to the pits, that i realised i finished second

So that was the first of the RC and it got a podium finish !!!
