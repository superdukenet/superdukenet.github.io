Festival Internacional Acidente Portugal 2014

tripoddave

2013-10-12 08:44:00 UTC
You are cordially invited to the 3rd running of the International Crashfest.
This time it is to be run at Portimao in Portugal hosted by Hottrax from Friday 26th to Monday 29th September.
4 days on track with an Endurance 'race' being run on the afternoon of the 3rd day for those who want to take part.
There will be a choice of at least two hotels: a 4 Star hotel and a 3 star with the possibility of a B&B option.
Price (all inclusive) for the 4 star is £725 and £649 for the 3 star which is not bad when you consider it is full board, includes bike transport and 4 days at Portimao.
Even at it's most expensive it works out at £141 per day for track, hotel and food.

Hottrax are not putting this up on their website for a good few weeks yet so there is the possibility of getting a group together and getting a discount if we can get sufficient numbers to commit early.

I think everyone who has been on these trips would recommend it and Portimao has to be on every rider's bucket list so:
Who is up for this?
