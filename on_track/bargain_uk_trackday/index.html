Ever wondered what the draw of the circuit was?

I run a trackday/circuit experience for southwestbikers.com every year at a small track in Wales called Llandow. It's a short circuit(approx 1.2 miles) and a lot of fun.

I have spaces left in the FAST group(about the same pace as the top of an inters group on a normal day) and the NOVICE group.

It's a forum day so most of the people know each other and are going for the social giggle - it's not a place that we get issues with egos or sharks.

Each group runs 17 bikes - 16 riders and one on track marshal who is there to help you if needed
Each session is 15 minutes
You get 6 sessions

The day is run by the Llandow staff under the normal trackday rules/regs.

Riders briefing at the start of the day will explain everything for first timers.


This is a great way of dipping your toe into circuit playtime without the pressure of big track users.
