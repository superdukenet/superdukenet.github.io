2012-04-27 12:52:00 UTC
Good few days. Had a great laugh at Cadwell. Was good to meet up the the SD boys. All top blokes, nice and chilled and up for a laugh.

Waiting for vids to upload so few pics 1st.



Oulton was totally pissing it down. Lots of stoppages form guys going down and cracking engine casings and covering the track in oil. Had 1 session, got absolutley soaked, then some dude in the novice group threw his Blade on it's ribs on the 1st fooking sighting lap!!!! Track closed for 2 hours to clean up the oil.

Only got about 4 sessions then packed up. Bike was working ok. Slipper is better with the plate change. 1st time on wets, was still finding my feet with them but overall pretty impressive. Had a good run in the last session with a guy from the TDR forum who races. He set off 10-15 secs before me so it gave me something for focus on and got me head down and tried to get a rythym going and reel him in, really enjoyed it. Caught and passed him on the last lap. Got it on vid, taking aaaaages to buffer though.

Few Oulton pics.

1st part of hissy's chicaine




Little bit lively over Hilltop early on in the day




Cadwell was the better of the two days. 1st time i've been there on a bike so all new to me. It's loads steeper than it looks in the vids!!

2 wet sessions then a 1/2 dry sesh that i only did two laps in then came off coz i'm too tight to shag a set of wets on a part drying track. Changed to Interacts and took a session to run them in and try and get a feel for the place. Then had 1 dry session to try and get a run round and pick gears, braking markers etc. Was taking it too steady, not running it in hard enough on the front etc but better that then launch it on the 1st time there!

Goal was to crack a 1min 50. Just managed it and no more. GPS fooked up on dry session so not accurate but got a time off the vid. 1min 53's on running in tyres session. Then next session traffic on the lap, generally being a lazy and not going hard enough, wrong gears, comfort braking for no reason, completelty missing nearly every apex on the entire freakin circuit , but got a 1min 49.9. Happy enough, but at the same time a bit dissappointed as i can see where shit loads of time can come off that. Overall got be be happy though, only my 4th full trackday on the bike so baby steps, chip away with no more big offs and try and get better on it.



I'll let the other guys post their pics. Sabre did a cracking job of snapping a few shots. They turned out bettter than the Official Photographer!!
