Not got a great Internet connection here.

Thanks for the support. Really helps. I'm 470miles from home on a track i've never seen before on a bike that's took a big punt on setup.

About 1.5 sec off the fastest lap. Streetfighter times are very quick this year. My times would put me top 5 in the Sportsman Elite 600 class and top 15 in GP1 Superbike class. I'll have to re check the sheets but last time I looked I was only 2 seconds off Jenny Tinmouths best lap.

Not had the carbon wheels on yet. My wets are on std rims. Not sure if I should swap the tyres over and run the carbons tomorrow.

Track is ok. Nice and flowing but still trying different lines and gears so can go faster still. Learning alot, it's spinning the wheel all the way round the last corner. (I think Steve liked it!) but seems controllable and progressive. Really destroying tyres though. My rear wet is almost down the to smooth. Bike is setup ok. Changed some things and found time. Feels like a decent base. Not great, but in the ball park to start with. Most importantly is it's not snapping anymore and spins up under control. Seems like the bike has to move to get a good time round here. Only been out the seat once so far.

Got pipped over the line by the current champ after holding second all race. Beat me by 2 hundreds of a second!
Got 2nd in race two but tyre was fit for the bin before we even started. Very hard race, but learned alot about throttle control.

Just have to see what tomorrow brings. Really looking forward to finally getting my grid girl on the front row on tv!

Best lap in wet /damp /cold so far is 55.7. Supposed to be 3-4deg and sleet tomorrow.
