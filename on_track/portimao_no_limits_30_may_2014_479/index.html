I wasn't keen on the crates, but I've looked at how I can adapt my long term packing regime.
Swindon as a drop off is pretty crap for me, despite NL saying "there may be a south East drop point" on numerous events, it never appears.

I did Parc Algar on FE's first trip there, and despite having a massive crash in the wet (120 MPH as I left the bike) I've always wanted to go back.
It really is a great track, with some features that are really unique.
The only draw back is the main straight. On a 990 you will get well and truly dusted. Even a 600 will pull a decent gap if they have the correct gearing.
Don't fit lower than 16/38, you'll hit the limiter in top way early.

Even the back straight is fairly long, but nowhere near the issue the main one is.

By the time I'd added a bag, my flights came to nearly £400.
