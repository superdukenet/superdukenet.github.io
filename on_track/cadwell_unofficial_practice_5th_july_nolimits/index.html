Superdan

2011-07-05 19:06:00 UTC
Well woke up at about 5am today and could not for the hell of it get back to sleep, dont know why I was not really that exited as no one I knew was there, but got to the track at 7am and park up in usual spot, there was about 10 Polish bikers next to me who were camping over and it felt like I was a work.

Saw a Kawaski and a triumph BSS race team setting up so there was going to be some quick riders out there, did the first session and got Colin to alter the front end (new springs) making an improvement in the feel of the front end however still dont like the riding position or the flickablity, just cant get comfy, find the seat too flat, going to jack up the Ohlins rear I think and maybe drop another ring at the front through the yokes.

Back brake still wont work, tried every method from every internet search and every professional Aprillia mechanics methods and Aprilia bulitins, nothing makes this POS brake work unless you fit aftermarket rearsets which changes the placement of the MC (id be tempted if I was keeping it).

Was getting my Mojo on and was overtaking alot of bikes and getting spanked by the BSS boys (1:35 laps ) takes a lot to shit me up on track, but was just about to tip into the right left for Hall Bends by the clubhouse when one of the Kawa's goes on the inside of me, had a little wobble as it shat me up but carried on. Tw@t.

Had a monster slide from the rear on Charlies 1 promptly came in to change my underpants and spoke to the tyre guy. Been running K3 Ractecs front and rear he told me to drop the rear from 31 to 28 hot, never had a problem after that, was sunny all day.

Lots of crashes from the Polish boys, they were having their own crashfest......and tried tyre warmers for first time today. Awesome!

Went out a bit after lunch but had done over 100miles and 2 hours on track by 2pm and I was fooked, got a false nuetral and a nuetral upchanging from 3-4th almost grass tracking so called it a day, wanted to be in one piece for the crashfest!!.

The dash showed my top speed of the day as 141mph and I never got out of 4th so im going to try a 44 or 45t rear next time, its on 15-42 ATM.

My mate was timing me and said my fasted lap was a 1:52 which I found a bit dissapointing
