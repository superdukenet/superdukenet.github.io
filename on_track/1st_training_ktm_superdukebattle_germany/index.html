This weekend the KTM Superdukebattle had its first training in Oschersleben
Weather was wonderful, Oschersleben is great
For those who dont know the track, a little vid from me, but with my old R6 back in the year 2008

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IF2UbtdL5sY

This year 32 guys and girls take place in the KTM Superdukebattle and it will be a lot of fun

For me it was a very good weekend, too. When I start to drive my Superduke R I told everybody that this bike will be as fast as my R6 - everybody thought Im crazy

My fastest lap with my R6 in oschersleben is a 1:33,84min
My fastest lap with my KTM is now a 1:34,00min
Driven with the 2010 Continental Race Attack, front soft and rear endurance. Im sure with a medium rear tyre I can go faster

I love my KTM





Next weekend is our 1st race - at the Nuerburgring
Maybe with some more photos and hopefully a vid - I totally forgot to record some laps this weekend
