Had a great time at Brands yesterday!

Not the best weather obviously but i was surprised by the grip given by my standard corsa 3's.

I met Pete briefly and saw his slightly bent SD, poor thing. Different group, garage and trying to stay warm and dry kept me busy a lot.

Had two small incidents. Where Pete mentioned the fluids on the track after Graham Hill bend i went through it and spun up the rear which was a bit of a ass eating pants moment!

Also got target fixated on the back of one guy on his 990 SM going into graham hill and totally missed my braking point, locked the front wheel which tucked and then had to go straight onto the grass cos i was scared to turn it late!

Best lesson of the day was get some wets! Saw Pete enjoying his.

Anyhow thats definately the last trackday this year, can't wait to go to Brands when its dry.
