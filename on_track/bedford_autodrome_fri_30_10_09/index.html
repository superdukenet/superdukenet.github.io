2009-10-31 12:12:00 UTC
A good day indeed at Bedford.
It was my first time at this venue, and I wasn't disappointed.
I opted to take the ZX6r trackbike, it's only been out once this year in the spring, as the SD has taken preference. It took a while to get back into the IL4 riding technique, but it was good to get back out on the old greenie.

As it turned out the SD's seemed to be the weapon of choice as they carried more speed out of the corners. I think if I'd have taken mine I would have been struggling to meet the noise regs, as I had 106db at Brands with baffles and 102db at Cadwell with the same set up?

It was good to catch up again with Dan, Jules and Craig and to meet Motoronin for the first time.
Looking forward to seeing the track shots.
