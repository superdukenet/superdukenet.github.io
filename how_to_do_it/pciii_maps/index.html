2008-08-06 19:56:00 UTC
I hope over the coming weeks/months to put together a decent library of custom maps which people can download to their PCIII to help make their bike run better but without the expense of custom mapping and dyno time.

The maps will not be a perfect fit for all bikes but should help.

Here goes....

Edited to say....

These maps were written specifically for the bikes they were copied from and may not be suitable for your bike. Make sure the base map and bike setup are the same as yours before use. If there isn't a suitable map please don't risk it. There are no guarantees and all maps are downloaded and used at the users' risk.
