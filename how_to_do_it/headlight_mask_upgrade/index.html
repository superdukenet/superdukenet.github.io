Hi kids, a few of you might have read in a few threads that me and samba were wanting to upgrade our old shape headlight masks form the 05/06 to the latest one.

Over at superMOTO we tend to do things the other way around! I missed not having a tacho, so I did this to an '06 SM 950:



It's an '09 SD dash mounted on the 950SM mask. The wiring was a bit involved, but not really an issue once I got my hands on the wiring schematics of both bikes... and the coolant temperature sensor from the SM. Neat and a huge improvement over the original. Bought the parts in the US where (at the time) they were MUCH cheaper than in Europe, shipped to a friend's address, then picked them up on the next business trip.

The footprint of the SM clocks is not too dissimilar to that of the original speedo that you've mounted, so this will almost certainly fit.

AFTER I'd done the job I found a whole "how to" on ktmtalk!!! If you're interested let me know and I'll fish-out the thread for you.

Interestingly, I have the original speedo and mount that looks just like in the picture from Kinge. Ebay would get you a speedo like this one for not much (100USD typically), then an original mount from KTM should see the bill way way lower than the 1000USD quoted! Or make me an offer on mine...
