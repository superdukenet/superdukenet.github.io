2011-08-19 19:57:00 UTC

If you unbolt your throttle housing you'll see just a couple of thin plastic tabs going across the width of the housing, you just need to chop these out, I imagine there might be other ways of doing it but if it was me I'd get it done now while you're waiting for the throttle to arive and then you'll only have to adjust the cables while you're in almeria if you don't have time to do it between the throttle arriving and you dropping the bike off for shipping.

If you ordered it direct from G2 then I usually allow at least 3 weeks for shipping from the US.
