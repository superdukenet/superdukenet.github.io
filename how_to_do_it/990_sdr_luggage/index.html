I go away camping for a week or so on a single seat 990. Use a KTM tank bag, a Kriega tail pack and about four bungee cords to hold on a tent, mattress and sleeping bag roll. It all holds together quite well but you wouldn't want to be unpacking anything quickly or frequently from the rear end as once it's all attached it's hard to access. I sometimes also use a Kriega backpack for additional stuff.
