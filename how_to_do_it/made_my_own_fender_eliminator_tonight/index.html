Made my own fender eliminator tonight...

BobbyG990

2010-03-20 01:45:00 UTC
I didnt really like the looks or the prices of the fender eliminators Ive seen on the market so I decided to make my own. I spent less than $10 at home depot, and used a piece of aluminium I cut off of a street sign. My only concern is that I dont have a tag light now, Im considering buying the LED tag light bolts. Also might end up getting soaked if the roads are wet. I have a LED tail light integrator on the way, should be here in 5 or 6 days so that will take care of my turn signals. All I really did was cut the plastic with a utility knife, made a tag bracket and bolted things together, double nutted the bolts to fight off the vibes (regular nut 1st, lock nut 2nd). I think it came out pretty good, although not everyone will be able to do it. Im sure different cans and larger license plates would make this impossible, but I got away with it
