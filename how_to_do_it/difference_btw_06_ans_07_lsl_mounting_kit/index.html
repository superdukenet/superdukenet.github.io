Here's the link to the thread which picks up on the same problem I had with the earlier fitting kit. Like TJ says we swapped kits but I was going to make my own. The fork mounting tube needs to be 20mm longer than the '06 one - it's a simple aluminium tube and the bolt needs to be a corresponding length as well. And the riser on the tank needs to be increased by 10mm. At least that's what I seemed to be saying in the post.

If you've got it to fit onto an '07 then it's an '07 kit. There's no way that the '06 lengths would allow you to mount it on the taller '07 tank. But it does fit with nothing level or 90 degrees to the bike. It had me a bit concerned at first but like you say - as long as the travel is free and nothing touches at lock to lock and there is no distortion on the brackets at full lock then all will be OK.
