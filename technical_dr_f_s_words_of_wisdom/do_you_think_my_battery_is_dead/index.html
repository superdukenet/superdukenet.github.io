Shade, all you got to do to check your alternator is take a battery reading as normal with your multimeter and then take another one with the bike running, it should be higher voltage while the bike is running, I can't remember the exact amount but it might be something like 14.5 volts you're supposed to see, whatever it is, if you see an increase then your alternator is probably working ok.
