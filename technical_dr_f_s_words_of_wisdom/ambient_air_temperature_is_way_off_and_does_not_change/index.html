I need to have a look at this but since I'm at work at the moment I can't.
I'm pretty sure on my bike that works fine. I'll have a look and post later.
I know from experince that some people tamper with the temperature sensor to trick it in to thinking it is cold to get more fuel injected. Make sure that is not the case. You can probably spot that by checking if someone soldered a resistor over it or so.

EDIT:
Just got home and had a look. On my bike it works fine. I'm pretty sure it is just corrosion on a connector or so. Open the head light and look for the air temp sensor. There are two temp sensors on the SD, one on the airbox for the ECU and the other one must be in the head light cowl for the dash. hope this helps.
