Hey Buster, No... ye can forget about the rear air filter hose for now, the one I am referring to is the one that you would normally attach to the top front.

(which you need the block-off plates to have access to the needed 90 degree elbow).

(the one in question is actually is a crankcase breather hose that goes to the LH engine side cover),

It is very easy to remove all of the SAS, it is of no use at all with the employment of Mars box,

but the crank case breather hose must remain vented, never plug or kink the hose.

the ad-on universal filter is 1-2" tall and has a fitting to fit the described hose. (which you will not need if you do the plates).

the alloy plate does not need to be anything special, ktm sells nice fancy ones if you like, use the elbow as a template if your making your own.

the front engine plate takes an end wrench and a 1/4" X 8mm universal socket to gain access without removing stuff.

Have patience ready. DO NOT CROSS THREAD!
