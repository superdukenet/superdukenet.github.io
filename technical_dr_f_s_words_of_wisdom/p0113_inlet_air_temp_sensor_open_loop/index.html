I had a FI error 9: rear cyl MAP sensor.

swapped the front to rear and vise versa. Still got error 9 so I know it wasn't the sensor.

looked at the area infront and below the ECU (behind steering head). the hundred + wires there are not wrapped or protected. alot of the strands are rubbing against the frame where it turns 90 deg and up into the ECU. I saw at least 2 wires with insulation chaffed away where I can see bare strands. I cleaned and wrapped with electical tape, etc.

error gone for 100 miles so far.....


else where close to the engine the wiring harness is protected by a loom/wrap.
