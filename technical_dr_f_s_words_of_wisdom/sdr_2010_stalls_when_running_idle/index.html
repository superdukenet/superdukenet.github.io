Since recently my SDR 990 from 2010 is stalling when it's running idle.

15k service was done 3000km ago (incl sparkplugs), bike ran smoothly ever since. untill all of a sudden it started to stall when the bike runs idle at e.g. a traffic light or crossing. While you have tune ECU connected up try throttle cable idle reset. It's an option in the test page. If you select this it will fully close the throttle butterfly's to 0 (if you have the airbox open or the left hand triangle access panel off, you can see the linkage move to the mechanical stop from it's predetermined idle position). Once it's fully closed from the test, switch the ignition off then adjust the throttle cable to get correct slack at twist grip. Then do 15 minute reset (start from cold without touching throttle, switch off after 15 mins but before 16 mins).

This should stabilise and reset the idle. I had the issue of stalling when cold once throttle was touched and then high idle when hot. During the 15 minute reset mine idled at +2k. Next start it went straight to 1600 and was steady.
