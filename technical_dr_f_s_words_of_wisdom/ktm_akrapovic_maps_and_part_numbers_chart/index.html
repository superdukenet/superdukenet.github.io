Thule

I went in to the dealer today to get my first service done and also had the Akra map loaded onto the bike. While I was there I was also able to get a chart of which maps were supposed to go with which pipes. I brought it home and made a scan of it figuring that this may help other solve the mystery of which maps existed and what pipes they go with. It’s especially nice since it has the part numbers on it as well.

The sheet I got has all of the fuel injected bikes on it (690’s, RC8, Adventures, etc) but I cut it down just to show the SD applications for the sake of size. If anyone is interested in the rest of them let me know.

(The one with the star next to it is what I got loaded today)
