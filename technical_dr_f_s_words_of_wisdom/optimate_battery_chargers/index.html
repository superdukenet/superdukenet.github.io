Optimate & Battery Chargers

Dr F

2007-11-18 19:51:00 UTC

The GSXRs I used to have all had alarms. These always seemed to kill the battery over winter and during lengthy periods of non-use. It's not quite the same problem with my superduke though as a) it doesn't have an alarm and b) I just wanna be out riding it all the time.

Anyway.....the owners manual says the battery should be disconnected from the bike - or at least the earth taken off - when putting it on charge. I was thinking of cabling the bike up so I could simply just plug it in to keep the battery nice and cosy.

Anyone else already got their bike hooked up to a Optimate or the like and anyone experienced any problems. THE last thing I want t do is fry the electrics on it.

Thanks,

Simon.
