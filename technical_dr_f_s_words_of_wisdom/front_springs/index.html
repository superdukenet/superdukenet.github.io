88kg is 194 lbs which is what I weight. I'm not having any issues with the stock springs on an 08SDR, which are .97kg/mm or 95n. They should be close unless there is another problem.
To get an accurate read you need to get two friends for help. Get in your gear. Get in a tuck with one friend balancing the bike. The other takes two measurements. One after compressing the front; The other after lifting up on the forks and letting them settle. Average the two.
If the readings are more than 10mm apart things are in a bind. Loosen the axle pinch bolts. Loosen the lower trees. Back off the triple a half turn.
Compress and lift the forks. Fast-as in bounce.
Tighten things back up in reverse order.
If this doesn't help or if there is not excessive sticktion, I know it's not really a word, I'd take 'em to a suspension shop and have 'em checked out.
