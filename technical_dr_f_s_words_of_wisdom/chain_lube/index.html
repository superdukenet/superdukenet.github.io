I just pop the rear up using a swing arm stand and wipe the chian down with an old gym sock soaked with WD-40.

I then wipe the chain with a dry sock and then spray on some Hondaline Pro-Chain lube. This stuff is great...no fling at all. If chain needs adjusting then now is the time as its nice and clean and will give you correct feel during adjustment.
