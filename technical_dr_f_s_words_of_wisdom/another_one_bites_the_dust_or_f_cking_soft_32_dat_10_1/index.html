Can almost guarantee it's not the thermoswitch.

Have had the Soft 32 Dat 101 thing more times than I care to remember - once with a definitely failing battery (easy to check), once due to a failing reg/rec and/or battery (both checked out OK but problem solved by replacing both - reg/rec can crack and intermittently fail only when in use and hot) but it's most often been a loose/broken cable/connector - early warning of a failing reg/rec is often blown bulbs, of loose/broken cable/connector is often clocks resetting similar to start up but given your lack of other symptoms, it may just be a tired battery.
