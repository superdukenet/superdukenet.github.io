BT016's - So good they deserve their own thread

Lowrance

2008-11-10 00:12:00 UTC
Just mounted up the 016 Stones. Fantastic tyres! In fact..the best street tyres I've ever used. They warm up quickly and stick like glue.

The best part about these tyres is the profile and construction. The OEM Pirelli CorsaIII's stuck well when finally warmed up but the feedback was vauge when heeled over due to the overly stiff sidewall. The tirangulated front was also an issue, especially on this bike (the SD doesn't need any help in the turning catagory). The Stones have a rounder profile which provide way more neutral steering charactersitics.

Wet weather traction seems good. Didn't push it as the tyres were new and it was the first rain we've had here is So Cal this year. Slippery roads and mold release agent don't mix all that well.

Not sure how they'll do on the track. Probably like most street tyres...overheat and get greasy. For the road they're perfect.

Wear wise, I've got 300 miles so far and they seem to be wearing like any other tyre. I typically get 1000 miles out of a set.

Great grip, compiant ride, excellent steering charactersitics and inexpensive. I paid $220 for the set. What's not to love?


Get them! You'll be impressed.

John
