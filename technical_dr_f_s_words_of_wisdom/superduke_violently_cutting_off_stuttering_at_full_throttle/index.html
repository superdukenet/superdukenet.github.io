Hey Guys,

Two different stories, one identical issue.

About 2 months ago I take the SDuke for a day ride. The bike had been sitting for most of the time for about 2 months (By "sitting" i mean that I could not ride it on a daily basis like I usually do, only once every other week or so...). The whole ride goes well, but at the end as I was doing a steady 80Mph it cut off on me, like, a micro second... but it was VERY strong! ... Stopped on the side of the road, checked everything ... revved it up a bit in neutral ... nothing... Get back on the bike, nail it, does it again so bad that I almost fell off!!
: (

Today I took the bike for the first time in 3 weeks (was gone overseas for a while...). Same thing happened but worse, longer "stuttering" "cutting off" periods... Horrible... every single time I would take off at a stop sign it would start doing it, whereas I would go strong or not on the throttle...
: (

After my first time I took it to the dealership where they checked everything but could not reproduce the symptoms. Conclusion was that maybe I had some nasty particles in the fuel that might have clogged the fuel filter which got replaced...
I took it again today but, as I told them, it felt more "electronic" related, like a dying fuel pump or ECU, or or...

Anybody had that kind of issue?

Thanks for sharing.
Cheers.

I had a similar problem which turned out to be a broken connection to one of the ignition coils. Check the the condition of the wires and make sure the insulation is sound. One of the wires on my bike was worn through by the clamp used by in a dyno session to measure the ignition pulses. The problem only manifested itself at high revs. Just needed re-soldering and has been fine ever since.
