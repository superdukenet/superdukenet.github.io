"Hey Bud... First off you should join us for a ride!!!!! Second.... Their are two fuel filters in the tank:1 screen filter---1 paper cone filter. The paper filter has clogged on mine and has done the same things your taling about. I suggest removing the pump and cleaning the screen filter and completely removing the cone filter. Reasons are the cone filter can easily start to fall apart (like mine did) causing all types of crap in the tank. I suggest putting an inline filter like the one I have from Jegs(like $40 ish). Search my post and youll find a thread i started with a posted part number for the filter. If you need some help I'm in Chatsworth. Shoot me a PM and Ill get you my phone#."
