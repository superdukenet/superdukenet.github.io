Ah yes... many moons ago... www.autospeed.com did some excellent reviews of electric superchargers. The bottom line is that they are useless, and this can be proven with physics as well as tests.

The gains are prob between a stock air-intake and a hi-performance airfilter and no tract and airbox - and run without heatsoak in the enginebay. LOL

I must say - it does sound like a good idea... but trust me don't bother.

Good suggestion though... I would certainly be interested if something was released that actaully worked. But size and power constraints with the technology we have is the issue.

Cheers!

Keep the idea's coming!
