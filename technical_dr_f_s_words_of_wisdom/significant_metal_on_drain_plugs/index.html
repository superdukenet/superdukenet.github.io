Hi all,

i have a 07 SD which now has 16,000km. i bought it with 9500km on it and changed the oil at around 11,000km. no metals to be seen.
was changing the oil today and noticed alot of metal shavings on the oil tank drain bolt, and on the engine drain bolt. enough to make me worried anyway.
bike had done 5,000km since previous oil change, only street useage (but hard riding, went through 4 sets of rubber to 1 oil change

i have now filled the bike back up with shell ax7 15w/50 and will run it for 1000km and change the oil again to look for metal. half will be hard riding in the hills and half will be highway cruising to motogp next weekend.

how much metal do these bikes move around? i thought they were normally fairly clean. bike seems to be running fine otherwise, no strange noises, making good HP. starts easy as.

not sure where to start, i think changing the oil after a good run will give me a good indication whats going on but what goes wrong with these things? i might check the valves in 1000km when i drop the oil again.

help??
