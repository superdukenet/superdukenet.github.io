ktmguy

2015-02-23 20:48:00 UTC
Possibly remove the secondary butterfly valves in your throttle bodies.
Make sure your throttle cable isn't getting stuck/lube your throttle housing (takes 5 mins)
Check spark plugs

If this is a new symptom I doubt your PC3 is at fault. Tunes don't just rearrange themselves on the fly. If the bike was like this when you got it then you might want to consider a retune.
