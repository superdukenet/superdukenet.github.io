2012-08-04 05:23:00 UTC

I think Klink has an 06 and they only have 7or 8 bars not the 13 that are on the 07+ models. Hence 4 bars compared to 6. Having said that mine will sit around 4 to 5 if it's cold enough ie 0-10 DegC. Seeing Klinks in the UK and it's summer its entirely possible!

I fitted the thermostat and it was one of the best moves I made to reduce the temperature. The other key steps are to flush the radiator really well, the one other part I found is that the rubber flaps on the radiator shroud were blocked by cables thus they wouldnt open when on the move. I just went and removed them all entirley and havent looked back. The fan moves more than enough air on its own when stationary.

Since these mods I've only had one time where it went to 10 bars briefly and it was 42DegC in stop start traffic and lotsa slipping of the clutch.

Oh and dont worry about the fan being on more often. As long as you dont leave the bike on but not running the battery wont be a problem. It'll die sooner just from being in high temps.
