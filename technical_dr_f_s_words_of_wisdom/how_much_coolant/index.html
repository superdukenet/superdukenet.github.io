jack front wheel / end up 50mm, fill radiator, slowly and fill reseviour 3/4 up or half, lower bike and take out bleed screw from other side of rad (opposite to rad cap, run engine until no bubbles/air put screw back in and cap on, run bike until 4 bars then run for 5 mins, let cool top up coolant if needed, then just keep an eye on level for a ride or 2. then done (this is on an 06)
