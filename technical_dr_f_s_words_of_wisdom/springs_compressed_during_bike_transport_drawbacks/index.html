Springs compressed during bike transport - drawbacks?

41KS

2013-02-01 11:50:00 UTC
Hi, just to ask - my bike is in the sea container belted to the pallet and springs on suspension are strongly compressed to 25% (100%=no weight, 0%=compressed completely to the end). Transport time were planned to be 2 week, but may well extend to 4 weeks... Could it be any adverse influence to suspension after 4 weeks suspension been continuously compressed? Any experiences?

Hotbrakes

2013-02-01 13:11:00 UTC
Fork seals. If they're in good shape chances are you'll be fine. But if there are any major temperature changes within the sea container the air in the forks will expand and push. Did you happen to bleed the forks once compressed?
