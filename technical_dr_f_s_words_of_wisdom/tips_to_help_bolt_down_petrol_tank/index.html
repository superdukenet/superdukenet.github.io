SDNerd

2012-07-17 03:49:00 UTC

I just barely thread the tank bolt into the frame, then barely start the two rear tank bolts down next to the seat without threading them. Wedge a BFS (big f***ing screwdriver) between the rear tank mounts and the rear sub frame to get everything aligned perfectly and than tighten everything down. Works like a charm!

41KS

2012-07-25 10:53:00 UTC

Ratchet strap (tie down strap) around the front of the tank and through the rear footrests.

Only needs a reasonable amount of tension and the bolt lines up better, not perfect but enough to get it done up.

Sounds scary but it is a straight pull from footrest brackets to tank front.
