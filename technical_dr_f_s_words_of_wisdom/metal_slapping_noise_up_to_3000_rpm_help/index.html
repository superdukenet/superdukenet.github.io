pretty sure not a cam chain - it would rattle most when warmed up and in neutral... could be a shift-fork or shift-cam maybe? (since you get kicked out of 1st...) but then again you have no trouble shifting... hard to say, but if it is only happening in gear, i'd suspect clutch/tranny.
