These crash protectors are jus that crash protectors
In the event of a big crash with these i would still expect the bike to be written off due to frame damage and general cosmetics BUT if you were to say slide of on diesel at a roundabout doing about 15mph i would expect that they would save major damage and allow you to ride away on the bike with a slightly red face
Or maybe if you were to pull away from a petrol station and forget to put your bike in gear and the bike fell 0ver you would also save yourself a lot of money
Hence if you have a big off nobody is going to be able to substantiate that the crash bungs made the damage worse or better
In fact i was renewing my insurance recently and one of the companies who quoted even offered a discount if r+g bungs were fitted

I understand that but it doesnt matter if its a slow speed off or not (unless its stationary!) those narrow fixings will bend and the bike could end up scraping along the deck anyway.

Having said all that (and I'm NOT slagging anyone off for trying to make alternatives to r&g) even the R&G ones have narrower fixings (although they don't look as long or narrow) and look a bit vunerable.

It would be intersting to get posts from anyone unfortunate to have had an off with any crash bungs on just to see how good/bad they are.

It's like a lot of safety kit, we spend our dosh but hope they are never used, however if anyone has had the unfortunate experience of coming off it would be interesing to see their comments.
