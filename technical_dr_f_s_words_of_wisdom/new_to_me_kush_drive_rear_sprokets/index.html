Those have been around for some time. Haven't seen any in person or known anyone that runs one. I remember hearing that there weren't a long term solution... Internet info, take with a grain of salt.

I don't think that you have to worry about your XR as long as you keep it shod with knobbies. Throw on some street rubber with more grip then rear hub or transmission will take a beating.

I had been looking for a good cheap Cush hub like the Kosman unit for my Norton. Already destroyed one tranny. Next wheel, will have a better setup, looking for a better transmision come to think about it.

Building a wheel around a true cushhub like the 640s gives you a real cushion, changeable rubbers, and gearing options that you might want for that BRP of yours, especially if you want to go back and forth between knobbies and street rubber.

Take a chance and report back after a few thousand miles. I'm curious to see how you make out.
