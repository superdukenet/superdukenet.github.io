I can't get on to that page but I can take an educated guess at what the answer is.

The newer bikes have to have catalytic converters with the exhausts due to emissions regulations and so Akrapovic started including removable ones with their systems.

The original Akra systems stated as being for the older models don't come with catalytic converters as they were pre regulation

So the only difference between the exhausts is that one comes with catalytic converters and the other doesn't, they state the earlier one is not suitable because you are "supposed" to have cats but most I know have bought the system designated as for the older model to save some money as they have no intention of fitting the cats anyway.
