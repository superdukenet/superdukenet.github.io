Dr F

2008-03-02 20:23:00 UTC
Unfortunatly, the '07 bikes only seem to make around 108 - 110bhp stock, with a full system it will make about 115bhp if your lucky according to members who've had theirs dyno'd, the '05/'06 bikes make between 120 and 125 bhp. The DNA filter improvements depend on who you talk to. Mine only made .5bhp more with a DNA whereas others are claiming 2-3 bhp gains. One thing that seems to be true though is that the Akrapovic fuel map downloaded by your dealer works a lot better on the '07 bike than the '05/'06 bikes, so much so that a power commander doesn't seem to give as good gains as it does on the earlier bike, if at all, just smoother pick up at low revs.

You will need a photo hosting website such as Photobucket to include pics in your threads.

Hope this helps.
