I run the Shell Rotella synthetic in all my bikes. Never a problem. That used to be what KTM called for. It's a matter of business who they recommend, not necessarily a better oil. The Shell is cheap and works great. Very heavy duty. About $17/gallon at Wal-Mart for full synthetic. It's not that important, I think. Nor is the frequency of the oil change. These things aren't that fragile. It's marketing. Take care of it, but no need to be paranoid.

My take after years and years, and miles and miles.
