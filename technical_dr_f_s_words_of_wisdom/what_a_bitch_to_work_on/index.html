What a bitch to work on!

jcs895

2012-12-04 21:23:00 UTC
I'm new to the Superduke and love to tinker, but whyyyy is this bike such a bitch to work on!

Took out the tank bolt on Sunday to have a look underneath and then spent the rest of the day wrestling it back on. I ended up having to take the steering damper off cos I just couldn't get enough leverage to poke the bolt back in, I'll save putting it back on for another time - when I have a few spare days! One day off gone, no progress.

Now today I took off the headlight to fix some dodgy looking wiring, all fixed no problem but then it wouldn't start! Soft 3.1 dat 10.1 wtf does that mean!!! Thank god for all those that have gone before me on this forum as the ktm manuals are shite, found the answer-ish so had to locate the battery. It's in the most fooked up place! Why put it there? Wrestled around for most of another day to find it was only putting out 11.8V, that's clearly not enough to start it so why does the battery fault light only come on when voltage drops below 10?! Hooked up a permanent charge lead and my heated suit connector and finally got enough charge to start it - problem is its now too late and antisocial to run it in the garage and pissing it down outside! Two day offs gone, minimal progress.
I just want to ride the fooker!

Thanks everyone to a very thorough forum though, all your past posts helped me no end. Are there any other ridiculous designs to be aware of before I waste more days off? I still have an improved rear shock from Viking to put on and a few stickers....what's gonna go wrong from installing them? Wheel rim strips fook up the wheel alignment?....Ha!

Rant over, lovely bike, bitch to work on.
