On my battery, there are two wires connected to the postive and four (battery tender, Power Commander, and the two regular) wires connected to the negative.

You all know that because of the battery location and the electrical harnesses in the way, it is often very difficult to even see, much less get access to the negative and...yes...I neglected to attach one of the wires to the neg pole...so the battery wasn't charging....

so...I reconnected all four wires properly and everything is now apparently fine. Burned around aimlessly on the bike for an hour this morning...went home, restarted, everything seems okay.

I could just say..."oops, sorry...all my own stupid fault", and "I shouldn't have bothered anybody about this" (incl. the dealer, you guys, etc.) BUT I choose instead to rightly or wrongly blame the unknown designer of the battery box (and its location)...an individual, or committee, that has already been threatened with death (and worse) by various member of this forum.

or (just thought of this) I could blame winter, the long lay off, having to reinstall the battery and forgetting about the two main connections on the negative side....or I could blame my age (memory)....

..anyway..sorry...my fault..sort of.
