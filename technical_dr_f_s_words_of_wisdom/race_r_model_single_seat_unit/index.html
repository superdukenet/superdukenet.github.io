2012-05-05 20:35:00 UTC

I'm away till the 14th but here are a couple of pics.
If you go this option, it mounts on the forward passenger mounts, I scored some wide head allen bolts fron the wreckers.
You will also need two arced supports under it, If you look at and SDR tail Micrfiche you will see what I mean.
One is up front near the tank and another is just at the end of where the undertray is.
I made my own for my R6 with some 3mm Aluminium flat bar and rubber stoppers.
The tail off Perfect Fairings was made to fit an 06 I think and so I had to Jerry and cut the underside a little but it works well.
My taill fits snug so needs nothing on the rear, I'm sure you could do something similar.
Also don't put the R seat on as it makes it too high, looks good but feels shit.
