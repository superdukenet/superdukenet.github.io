shadowman

2011-02-27 08:11:00 UTC

I finally got around to running my bike on a dyno. Bike: 2008 SD-R with about 18,500kms. I just had the valve clearance checked, installed new chain and sprockets (16F-40R), and changed the oil & filter.
Other Mods:
BMC Air Filter w/stock air box.
Akrapovic slip-ons with baffles installed.
MotoHooligan Secondary butterflies.
SAS By-Passed.
Dynojet O2 Sensor Plugs.
Installed Otto Leirer TuneECU map for SD-R w/Akrapovic w/o catalyst w/baffles. I have the SAS and O2 Sensor un-checked (off).

The first chart shows 126.1 HP at 9,759 RPM with 10.4 m.kg at 8206 RPM.



The second chart shows the air/fuel ratio to be between 13.3 to 13.6. Not sure if that's good. On the computer screen the whole A/F line was in the green zone so I assume its good.



Can someone with Dyno knowledge and experience please comment on the charts. I may get the Motohooligan Airbox soon. The power is good now but I could always use more POWER!
