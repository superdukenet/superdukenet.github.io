De-catting the standard pipes

DukeofCornwall

2006-09-06 10:19:00 UTC
Whilst I appreciate the style of Akro's etc on the SD, I have to admit I like the style of the standard pipes in as much that they follow the lines of the bike well, plus (Evo's aside) they don't look as dated as aftermarket cans.

Does anyone know if it's possible to 'de-cat' the standard pipes - ie open & gut the standard pipes? A friend of mine had this done a few years ago on an MV Augusta, also one of the writers in Bike magazine has had his standard pipes 'modified' on his MT01(? - Big Yam V-twin thing).

In time I would like to retain my pipes but have the cat removed, presumably this will give a little power increase plus loosing weight & heat from the cat.
