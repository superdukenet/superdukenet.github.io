2008-05-18 12:27:00 UTC

mine is placed same as dr.f's. on the earlier bikes it can also fit behind the headlight cowling but not sure about the 07 models.

i fitted a pc on a standard bike and it definitely helped. i got it for future add ons and to help with the low down snatchiness and cutting out.
then i added leo's and remapped the pc. a power improvement, although it ran a little rougher and had a flat spot until i took out the circular plates between y-piece and end cans. baffles still in.
then i added a dna filter, keeping the same pc map, and its great. crisp, responsive and fun. still cuts out now and then (very rarely) but most just as i take off for the first time after warming up. it hasn't cut out properly on the move for a long long time now (fingers crossed)

petrol consumption seems pretty much the same to me, though i haven't been keeping a close eye on it.
