Quick oil question:
Castrol Power 1 Racing and Repsol Moto Racing oils are the cheapest ones in my country, but they are not ester based..
FUCHS SILKOLENE COMP 4 ELECTROSYNTEC SAE 10W-40 is ester based and a little bit more expensive, but I don't know their products. Most expensive oils are the Motul 300V and Motorex ester-based ones (22-25 USD/L)..
I bought Motorex oil in the past (for the price of the cheap oils), but my stealer can not sell Motorex oils "from his big 60L Motorex barrel", so what oil to use next time?

cat's comments about ester oils from the forum:
motorex and motul (and some others)...PAO/ester is pretty much the best,... KTM specifies because high performance engine etc. 50 works better at higher temps than 40, the 10 (motorex) or 15 (motul) is so that it gets moving to valve gear etc quicker when cold, at start-up.

MOTUL do 15W50 not 10W50. full synthetic - ester, same as Motorex. it's expensive but as bad as Motorex. and you're more likely to find the 4l containers.

AMSOIL, Rock Oil, etc., aren't available in Aus/NZ/SA. [or not in SA, anyway. correct me if i'm wrong.]

CASTROL synth more likely to be avaiable, but CASTROL synth is not the real ester synthetic like MOTOREX and MOTUL...and i know this is a big argument and boring old forum thing, but KTM says full synth ester for all their engines, high performance engines, and i'm not going to do anything different that's ok but not as good.
