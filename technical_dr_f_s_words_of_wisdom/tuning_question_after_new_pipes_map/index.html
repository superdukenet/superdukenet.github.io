Tuning Question after New Pipes/Map

Zaq

2007-07-11 22:28:00 UTC

Got my Leo Vince's on last weekend. Had the dealer load the Akro slip-on map. I am using only the rear baffles in the Leos. I have a 2007 US spec SD.

My experience thus far is mixed. The sound is much improved - about the right level of noise for me. Performance while on the gas is maybe a bit torque-ier through the range.

The odd thing is it tends to continue revving when I let off the gas. Kind of like the anti-skid system is overcompensating and dumping too much gas in. Even after coming to a complete stop it idles at like 2300 for 5-10 secs before settling in to 1400 or so. Kinda throws a loop in my timing when I expect some engine drag and get none.

Any suggestions would be greatly appreciated!

Thanks,

2007-08-08 22:58:00 UTC

Well, I can confirm with certainty that the reason for the over-rev condition on stops was due to disconnecting the 2nd and 3rd gear sensor wires.

I double checked to make sure I had the correct wires disconnected - I did. Besides, the nuetral light worked so it had to be correct.

Re-connected the 2nd & 3rd gear sensors and took it out for a ride - absolutely no extra revs on slow downs. Absolutely perfect.

I looked through the shop manual and here is an excerpt on the N-2nd-3rd gear sensors, from page 7-36:

"NOTE: This function is especially important for the idle speed control".

<shrug>

So for now I am leaving the wires connected. I suspect the only right answer is to either have the Akra map loaded into the ECU (which disables 2nd/3rd gear restriction) and use the PCIII for fine tuning, or maybe invest in Tuneboy which if I recall gives you the ability to disable the 2nd/3rd gear limitation.

Anyway thanks for all the help and suggestions.

Dr. F - I did disconnect the clutch micro and checked it with a meter - it was fine.

Getting it dyno'd this Saturday so it should be good to go after that.

Thanks!

Zaq
