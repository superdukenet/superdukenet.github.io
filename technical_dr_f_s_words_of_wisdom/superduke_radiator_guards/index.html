2007-11-19 10:33:00 UTC

LOL! You're worse than me...nearly.

Piece of cardboard to use as a template...lay cardboard of the mesh and trace around it. Cut to shape.
I utilise a soft double sided foam to cushion the mesh from the radiator to eliminate and chances of ribbing which could lead to a leak.
I attach the guard with cable/zip ties.

Job takes 20mins start to finish....30mins if you spray paint the mesh black like I do.
