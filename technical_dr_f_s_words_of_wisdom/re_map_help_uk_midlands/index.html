Why do you think that an Akra map has been installed if the bike has standard exhausts and airbox?

If it's because the previous owner sold the aftermarket silencers and replaced the airbag before they passed it on then they really should have reverted to stock map too. The Akra map is notoriously rich even with a free flowing airbag and exhaust so with the your standard set up It most be really, really rich which explains your fume issue. But if you are keeping standard I'd have thought a good KTM dealer would refresh your ECU for you back to standard.

If you have a PC you need to get a cable and a map from this forum or like me get a Power Commander and get a custom map made by a good dyno place. It will make a huge difference to the bike if you change anything from standard. To be honest, you should consider junking the standard exhaust and airbox. You wouldn't regret the difference in feeling they make to the riding experience. And when you get the exhaust off you will see how heavy it all is. Like seriously a lot of weight.

As mentioned Projex comes with a very good reputation, there's a lot of members on here who have used them in the past. I have always used HMRacing but they are down South but very knowledgable about setting up LC8 engines.
