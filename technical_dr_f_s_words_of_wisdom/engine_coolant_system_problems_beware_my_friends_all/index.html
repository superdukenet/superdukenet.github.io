Good forum members all, joe has been thinking deeply on this LC8 malfunction and have concluded there is a problem with the coolant

reservoir overflow hose being misrouted from the factory on my 07 SD. I have came to this conclusion from repeated trips into my under tank

cavity for air box replacement. As many may recall, joe was the first to have the mar box after Jeff and Heather, also the first to have the MOTO box.

(Aaron you are a master with the CNC!) During these repeated trips into this area. I have discovered many things, One of which can pose a serious

threat to the longevity of your engine. The space in between the frame and other devises especially the stock air box is extremely tight.

During these repeated trips into this area I have noticed that my overflow tube/hose was pinched off due to the KTM hose mis-routing, this can and will

cause premature engine failure. Here is why.... Expansion in the engine cooling system is a natural occurrence, the pressure has no whare to escape to,

in turn causing the water pump seal and or head gasket to fail, this is only the tip of the problem, now that there is a natural route for the coolant to

escape because it has no where left to go but any weakness it can find to escape, when the undetected internal engine corruption has started

silently beyond the normal realm of sight and other senses then when cooling off now does the opposite, it becomes a tiny vacuum siphon

that will slowly but surely kill your LC8 engine from the inside out, It will now not only have lost coolant unexplainabley and beyond your

knowledge, now it is in a natural self destruct mode. No matter how many repairs are made if this described hose is kinked or pinched this will kill

your engine over and over again. Factory routing, this routing is a farse, it winds its way through a maze of parts in no particular organized fashion,

which OMHO is only an invitation for disaster. May the force be with you all. Check this by taking off the hose on the radiator that goes to the

coolant reservoir, blow into this hose if you can not or you feel resistance you have the problem, if not then carry on.

Peace,
joe
