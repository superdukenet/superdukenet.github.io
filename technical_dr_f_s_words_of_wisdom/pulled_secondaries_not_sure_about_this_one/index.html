Pulled secondaries...not sure about this one.

Lowrance

2011-05-13 02:28:00 UTC

Just pulled the secondary flies (Motohooligan's)...not so sure about this modification. Fired bike up, started just like normal, 4 bars, blip throttle and huge hesitation/bog. Hmmm. Was a little concerned about this before I pulled them. Got better as it warmed up further but still very very prevelant. Took it for a quick ride around the block and noticed an increase in power through mid (couldn't really open it up to test top end) The bog is not as noticeable while riding but still a little disconcerting.

Headed to dyno tomorrow for a touch-up of my custom dual cylinder map so hopefully the tech (Mickey Cohen) can tune this new feature out. If not, I think the flies will make a return appearance on my throttle bodies.

I know the PCIII has an accelerator pump feature (very similar to a mechanical accelerator pump found on Kehin carbs gracing most of the latest 4 stroke dirt bikes) for this very reason. I'm wondering if it needs to be activated to sort this issue. Will talk it over with Mickey and let you all know what the outcome is.

Talked to him about pulling the flies prior to actually doing it and he said he pulls them on just about every bike he tunes, from Triumph twins to R1s. Hasn't pulled them on an SD yet. Fingers crossed he can sort it. Will ask him about advancing the ignition as well (how far is safe, +2, +4?).

Does anyone know if Tune ECU has the accelerator pump feature that the PCIII does?
