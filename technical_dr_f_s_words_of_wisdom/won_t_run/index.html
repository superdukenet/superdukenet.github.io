Have you run the engine with TuneECU hooked up?

Is the problem with one cylinder or both?

If only one, could be plug, coil pack and/or associated wiring,

If, more likely, both, check pulse generator - could also be failing TPS or, possibly, ECU.

Presumably, electrical power to clocks, lights, etc isn't failing also.
