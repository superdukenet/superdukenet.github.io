No. 47

2017-07-23 19:42:00 UTC

Hello!

I recently purchased a used 2007 SD990, 33k miles, and am working on getting it to tip top shape.

I noticed there's blow back oil coming out of my rottweiler intake and dripping down the whole motor. Its not a whole lot daily but I've had to top it off weekly to keep it above the "min" line.

My preliminary research tells me that its possibly the balancer shaft seal. I've noticed a small amount of oil drop from near the front sprocket, (not counting the clutch oil from the slave that I'm in the works of replacing now). If this is a possible solution, id like to replace it. I just don't know what part and location it is. I'd love some help with that.

Also I've noticed a clicking like noise from the engine and don't know if this is normal. The noise gets a little lower when I pull the clutch in. Increases in repetition rate with engine RPMs but not in volume. I was lead to believe that if indeed my balancer shaft seal needs replacing, the oil pressure can be affected and not fully circulating into the cam chain area. Or its possible that the cam chain tensioner is failing. If its an easy part to replace, I dont mind doing it asap as well. Id love to know what parts I need.
