Robin, the quick connect coupler is a trick in itself, try unhooking the coupler and then hook it back up, this is a pressure sensitive valve and is very touchy.

a fuel pressure test would be the next step or maybe introducing a small amount of fuel into the throttle bodies, then push the starter button,

this will tell you if it is lack of fire/spark or fuel, if it starts then dies after the introduction of fuel then it is fuel related,

if not go to test electrical spark at the spark plug.
