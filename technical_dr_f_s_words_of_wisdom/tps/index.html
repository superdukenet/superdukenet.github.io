Just had to replace TPS for first time - for others future reference, symptoms were initially high tickover at cold start (cured by off/on two or three times), then plus engine cutting out/in for first half mile or so, then plus engine dying at rapidly closed/blipped throttle particularly in heavy traffic/hot engine and finally plus refusing to restart after stall without opening throttle to 50-75% but even then ran very roughly and stalled on closed throttle - all developed over circa one week's daily use and initially thought that early symptoms just needed TPS adjustment and 15 min reset, and latter symptoms were due to failing fuel pump - just needed new TPS.
