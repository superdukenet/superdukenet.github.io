2011-04-29 04:39:00 UTC
had that problem just recently when i did mine.

Do you have diffrent sized phillips head screwdrivers..? I first started with one I thought was right on but it bit and stripped bits of the screw head, I then used one a little larger and was careful but got it off.

Other than that maybe use some multi-grips and lock onto the head of the screw.

Or take the TB's off and work on a bench where you are safe not to let any debris into the cylinder.

I have the MH flies installed but I thought I would try the bike without flies and came up with an idea.
So I turned flies off with Tuneecu and manually pushed the the control arm on the flies forward and put a spring in palace to hold the flies fully open.
Went for a ride and it seems to pick up quicker from low throttle. No hard work if you have the moto box as you can get to the control arm easy.
