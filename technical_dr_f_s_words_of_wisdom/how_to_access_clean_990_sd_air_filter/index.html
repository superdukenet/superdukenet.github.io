How to access / clean 990 SD air filter?

AGRO!

2014-10-18 08:10:00 UTC

I was told to check my air filter in another thread, because the fuel consumption has gone crazy on my 990 SD (2011).

How do I access and clean the air filter? On my previous KTM it was just under the seat. I tried to look in the owners manual, but for air filter cleaning it says to go to an authorised dealer.

Once open, are there any other tips you could give me, e.g. how to clean the filter if it's dirty?

Thanks!

The air filter is located under the fuel tank.
You will have to take fuel tank spoilers off first pull small hose off fuel tank that is located on left hand side just above radiator then unscrew bolt at front of fuel tank then you will need something to prop up tank I use a push bike pump.
You will see air box then the rest is straightforward once you remove air filter use compressed air to blow crap from air filter.

It's easy.

You need to lift the tank off, so remove the seat, side panels etc and remove the front tank bolt from the top. You can then lift the tank from the front if you loosen the rear mount through bolt to undo the electrical connectors and fuel pipe. The tank will just lift off now if you take the rear through bolt out and the air box is there. Remove all the screws from the top and lift the top half of the airbox off.

Put yourself a nice K&N filter on or even better seeing as you've got this far put a Rottweiler filter system on and remove the SAS followed by a dyno run..

I do have pics of my SD without the tank on that you may find useful so drop me a message if you need them and I'll send across.

Only takes 10 mins tops to get the tank off. Be careful with the side panels as there is a securing bolt on the underside so three per panel per side if I recall correctly..
