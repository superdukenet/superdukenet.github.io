Hi Spyder,

To me it seems that there could be a mix up between the imperial gallon and the US gallon. Just was checking the different websites:

Info on the
UK Web Site: Fuel capacity approx. 18.5 liters (4.89 gal)
US Web Site: Fuel capacity approx. 15 liters (3.96 gal)

1 Imperial gallon = 4.54609188 liters
1 US gallon = 3.7854118 liters

UK Website
Using the Imperial measure: 4.89 x 4.546 = 22.2ltrs
Using the US measure: 4.89 x 3.785 = 18.5ltrs
Looks like they have used the US gallon to convert liters to gallons on the UK site.

US Website
Using the Imperial measure: 3.96 x 4.546 = 18trs
Using the US measure: 3.96 x 3.785 = 15ltrs
So it seems to me that they have used the imperial measure on the US site. I.e. 18ltrs/4.546 = 3.96 (as quoted on the site. Where as they should have used the US measure (18ltrs/3.785 = 4.75 US gallons).

Make sense?
