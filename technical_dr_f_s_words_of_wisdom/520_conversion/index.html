I bought KTM PP counter sprockets from Ron at KTMTwins. They are all listed as RC8 parts, not SD/SDR.
The rear I had made by Sprocket Specialties in the US and also had a PP one. The drive sprockets are only $75 US or so but the rears are a ridiculous $130 US or so. That is why I had one made.
I used the very expensive DID 530ERV(I think) chain and it lasted the whole season plus almost two years of track days.

I was looking into these guys. I have posted this link before. Might be worth a shot. There are a couple of guys on here that have bought them I think?
