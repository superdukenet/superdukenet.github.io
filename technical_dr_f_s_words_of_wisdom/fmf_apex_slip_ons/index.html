Don't have the FMF Apex but I can comment on having loud pipes. My Leos are true "off road only" cans, no baffles. And they sound incredible but are loud loud loud. It's fine at my tracks because they don't have decible limits. They get a lot of positive comments. But for the streets its starting to suck. I set off car alarms regularly (I think they are paranoid and their alarms are to sensitive but that's just my opinion). But worst is it's hard to be a hooligan because every head within two blocks is turning to see what the noise is about. I use to be able to pull little steatlh wheelies around town but now as soon as the wheel is up heads are turning. Cops are looking for you in situations where they normally wouldn't even know you were there. They sound great but its hard to cut up on the bike with the attention they attract.
