Ive been running 1 now for about 18 months and it stood from September last year till earlier this month in a cold damp unheated garage. Tested it with a volt meter before re fitting it expecting it to have dropped voltage slightly but it was still showing a healthy 12.3V. (and when refitted cranked the bike over with zero problems).

The only negative with Lithiums is that they may need balancing, but this only really becomes a problem if your running a total loss system (the bike does not recharge the battery whilst running, such as a race bike).

I had a Shorai 14 amp battery delivered from AMI Direct and after a full charge overnight (they come 90% charged anyway) it wasn't enough to turn over an '05 SD.

The guy then sent me a 21 amp model and that was massive so wouldn't fit, i would go for the 18 amp model in future (i said fook it i'll get another yuasa in the end and sent both back), just don't trust Shorai's recommendations.

From reading the instructions you need to have it on a charger/tender without the desulfating mode as it'll confuse the diagnostics in the charger.
