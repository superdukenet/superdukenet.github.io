Instead of buying new copper crush washers, use this process for rejuvenating the old ones. Used this process for years on copper
exhaust gaskets on a 450hp turbo air cooled VW drag race engines. The heat swells the copper and the water sets it.

1) take old copper washers and wipe clean
2) place washers on the tip of an old screwdriver or similar wire
3) hold washers over a high temp flame, like a propane torch or stove burner
4) heat until they turn red
5) drop into a dish of cold water
6) reinstall
