2011-03-02 21:55:00 UTC
Did you do the 15 minute reset of the ecu after loading the akra map (start the engine and let it run for at least 15 minutes without touching the throttle)?

Are you willing to use tuneECU? If yes, read your current map from the bike, disable O2 sensors en put it back on the bike (and again, do the 15 min reset).
If you are not going to use tuneECU, disconnect the O2 sensors and go for a ride to see if it helps. Just disconnecting the sensors will make the F1 light come on but the bike will run just fine. If it fixes the problem you can get eliminators.
