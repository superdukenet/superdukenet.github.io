2CTs vs Metzeler M3s.......

.....I was running M3s, great tyres, very confidence inspiring, great in the wet and highly recommend them.

I had to switch to 2CTs as I destroyed a rear on tour in France and they didn't have Metzelers in the shop. So far the 2CTs are as confidence inspiring as the M3s, in fact the profile makes the bike a little keener to tip in if anything. However, they are NOT as sticky on the centreband as the M3s, the symptom being the rear locks up that bit easier under downshifts, specially on track. Tonnes of grip on the sides though and was more than enough tyre for me on the bends at Brands Hatch.

When I destroy this rear 2CT I'll stick another on it, then when the front is done in aswell I'll think again. If the 2CTs last significantly longer than the M3s then I'll keep with the 2CTs, but if they wear out at the same speed I'll go back to M3s without any hesitation.
