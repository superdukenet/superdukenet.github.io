It's not a part for the Lc8, certainly not for the superduke, or it would be listed in the parts manual.

I read something the other (probably on advrider, I suppose) that pointed to it not being a good idea. Sorry, I don't remember quite what, I just made a "mental note" to forget it.

The one option that can't have a negative effect, an undesirable side-effect, is to add a second fan, so that when the LC8 system does turn on the fan, it turns on two fans. Wire them in series, take the wires off the existing fan connection. Fan from the RFS /450/525/whatever, or a Spal fan that's a suitable size.
