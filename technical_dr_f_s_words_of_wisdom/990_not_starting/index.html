Hi all, I have an 07 990R 18k miles with full ti Akrapovic, motohooligan intake, SAS rmeoved etc. no issues at all for 6k miles since bought, mostly used on track and Sunday rides. I have an electrical issue with it now I cant figure out, hoping you can help.

Current issue is lights and dash turn on, fuel pump does not prime and starter does not turn.

Some back story, I was on a trackday and bike slowly stalled with FI light on constant and would not rev properly. Back at the pits the bike started fine and idled fine, giving it throttle and keeping it at steady rpm it would bounce up and down the revs. Called it a day and went home, next day cleaned connections near the battery and the BGC with servisol isopropyl alcohol, let it dry and sprayed servisol silicone grease. Bike started fine, did 15 minute idle, went for a ride and after 150 miles bike did the same as on track, slowly lost power, would not rev with a constant FI light, then would not start at all. Back home again, checked fuses with millimeter all fine, cleaned BGC again, unplugged TPS and put back in, and it started then reved it it dies with solid FI light and would not start again.

I also tried to connect to TuneECU with 3 different computers with different operating systems and could not connect, either I cant setup TuneECU properly or it cant read the 990 ECU, could the BGC be causing all this?

What are your guys thoughts on this?
