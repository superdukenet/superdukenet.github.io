larryf8

2012-09-19 22:37:00 UTC

keep the intank paper and screen filter.

40 micron (.004mm) external filter is about the same as the intank screen sock?

you take filter paper that's been submerged for a long period in gasoline and ethanol (10% here in the USA) then dry it. it will crumble.

get fuel from stations with high traffic if possible.

ethanol mixed fuels... PLEASE use ethanol treatment like startron, stabil or lucas if your bike sits more than a fortnight (english!) every carb bike I've see has been affected by this. it will clog your needles/jets so imagine what it'll do to your 10 micron filter.
