SDNerd

2014-07-23 17:57:00 UTC
Probably obvious, most of the more popular brands are all good ... It's just a battery after all.

My personal bias is toward Ballistic because I really like their somewhat eccentric-to-use charger (because it has nerd-appeal features), and more importantly (to me) - they have great service. In speaking with vendors on this side of the pond that carry multiple brands of Li technology batteries, they seem to prefer dealing Ballistic for just this reason.

I have them in 4 of my bikes right now (including the SD), and will likely purchase a 5th one fairly soon.

I have friends who have Shorai and other brands - no issues really.

I've had a Shorai fitted for nearly two years and no problems. Like Nerd says you have to get used to the counterintuitive way that the battery works in the cold. If at first it won't turn the engine over this is not, (as with conventional batteries), bad news. Just switch the lights on for a few minutes on high beam and then try again. It will then work. Seems mad but it's true!
