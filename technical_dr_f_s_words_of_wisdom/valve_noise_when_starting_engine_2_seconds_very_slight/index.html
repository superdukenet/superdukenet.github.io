Mr_Trecolareco

2012-09-18 18:38:00 UTC
Both of mine along with every other four stroke I've had do it. It's probably the cam lobes beating on the buckets til they get a good coating of oil. Sometimes if a bike hasn't been run in a while it can be a good idea to crank it over for a few seconds with the ignition off to prime the system with oil. Otherwise it's not a problem.

Make sure you warm it up before taking off though. I usually start it, let it run for 1-2 minutes, then shut it down to let the heat dissipate through conduction throughout the motor. Then after a few minutes give it a couple more minutes of idling before setting off.
