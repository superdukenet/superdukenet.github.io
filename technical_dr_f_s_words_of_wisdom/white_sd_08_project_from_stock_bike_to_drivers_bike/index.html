1. Yep, good cans those. Nice sound and they sit in tight to the tail so will crash well.

2. CPR Rottweiler air box instead of MH. Remove 2ndary flies and shafts.RC8 stacks. Basic breather mod with filter and catch tube.

3. Custom Tune Ecu map with flies and O2 sensors switched off. 9950 rev limit.

That should be a good motor after that. Mid to high 120's at the wheel. Maybe even 130bhp if you've got an optimistic dyno.

Yes internal height is 65mm as much as I remember. There are one point to keep in mind: *Sausage filter probably does not fit Rottweiler base plate (round edges). I have never tried but I have my doubts. My setup was different - filter was in exactly central position against throttle bodies. Rottweiler is moved forward because of limited space under the tank...

Tech specs of filter you need, are shown here (open electronic catalogue, page 13th): http://www.itgairfilters.com/itgcatalogue.htm JC30-65 is the one you need. They have matching aluminium base plate as well, but you need to do the holes yourself if you go this way.

Seller is different, you can buy it from Merlin Motorsport: http://www.merlinmotorsport.co.uk/ITG-A ... index.html Warm suggestion, perfect client service and short shipping times, they seem to have all in stock. I even ordered custom made foam-over-sock from ITG through them, additional few days waiting time and fluent!

One option is you can buy my setup as well, as I removed the lower air-box for cooler look, and have different filter now on the bike. Its oiled and have been used for about 4-5 tanks of fuel maybe so tested and all perfect. If you think lower air box should be on place (because of dirty roads, looks etc etc), its the best option. My bike is in Spain now and it is possibly cleanest place in Europe for riding the bike or car...
