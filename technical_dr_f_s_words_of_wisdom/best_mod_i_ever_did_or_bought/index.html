Hmmm, lets see, first and foremost people should ditch the std exhaust but as mine was already off when I bought it I've left it out.

The rest in no particular order really but all things I wouldn't want to live without now

4. LSL Flat Bars
5. Running track tyres on track (racetecs)
6. Replacing the std footpegs
7. HM Quickshifter
8. Reverse Gear Change

And I haven't really used it to do anything proper yet so it can't really go in my list as top improvements as such but TuneECU for everything it is going to enable me to do once i get it working and for what it has done for other users so far

bic_bicknell

2011-03-28 16:03:00 UTC
1. Full Ti. Akra system - never rode the bike without Akras. But the full system made it rev to the red-line in every gear. It pulls and pulls forever.
2. Suter slipper clutch - you won't know how good a slipper is till you try it - then never go back. It doesn't effect engine braking or rob you of anything. Honest.
3. Motobox - what can I say? More power, better sound, ease of maintenance, smooth throttle, no backfire on the overrun.
4. PC111 + map - to make it all work together better.
5. HMRacing quickshifter - same as the slipper clutch. Plus it's fun to just pin the throttle and play racer boy.
6. Brembo Monoblocks - just fitted these and they are the best brakes I ever rode with. Bar none. Didn't think the standard SD brakes could be beaten. But they just have been.
7. SLS steering damper - takes the shake down a little when out with Klinky and Shade on those bumpy Essex roads. You don't need it anywhere else really.
8. ASV shorty levers - replaced my trusty Rizomas with these because they look so cool. Suit the SD more and won't break.
9. All the carbon panels I ever found on Ebay - I like the way carbon looks.
10. Clear back light with built in indicators and a Rizoma numberplate hanger to clean up the back end.

There are more but you asked for 10.
