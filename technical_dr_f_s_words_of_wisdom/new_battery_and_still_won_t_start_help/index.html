The ( dat / soft = Date / Software ) will come up in the instrument cluster when the voltage drops and the cluster resets. This under-voltage could be from: poor power or ground connections( excess resistance ), the starting system ( pulling too many amps ) or a battery with insignificant charge or capacity ( not completely charged or battery amp rating too low ) I would first recheck all of your connections battery , main relay etc. ( I know , Damn battery box ) because it's the cheapest to repair.

Good luck, Kuerbis
