Mate.

After the battery location travesty that is the SD's embarrassing major fault the next one is the oil level top-up issue.

There is no cure I'm afraid except that you can buy a dip-stick, (for KTM for the Adventurer), that allows you to see the level of the oil from the right hand side of the bike rather than crawling round to try and see the stupid oil level pipe on the left hand side whilst holding the bike perpendicular to the ground and then running backwards and forwards to make it right.

It's one of the SDs characteristics. Get to love it or accept it.
