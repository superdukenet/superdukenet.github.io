I was having this urge to take on the big one......I will give this Idea, and this up front, a forum member here offered the stock header pipe to me but we never spoke of the particulars, I was going to take a stock header pipe and make it look like the RC8, or Venom pipe.....straight through,,, motorcycle manufacturers used to do this sort of thing long ago.....scrambler was what one was called too. I have given up the notion of this cos of money and other "real" mechanical things I need to take care of first, but if you try this or something else, I would like to see the results. You would want to cut the pipe in 2 places, take the J shaped pipe and turn it out to the side or slightly have a turn upward, then when you have the angle of the outlet correct, mark it with a permanent marker take it to the welder, mount it up wallah, loud as the dickens pipe, (underbike) a minimalist dream. other than the noise, more than likely too loud.
