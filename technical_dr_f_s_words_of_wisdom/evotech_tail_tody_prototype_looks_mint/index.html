Ok guys, did the install on my 07 with race Akras. It took me a good four - five hours, I know, I like to work slow and make sure I get everything right! It really looks mint and makes the bike look much more compact and my mates commented that it looks much smaller. However, the indicators are just touching the cans, I went for a ride today though and they're fine. I'm doing the mrbubblehead mod and integrating them into the tail light anyway.

There are a couple of additions to the instructions for the 07 vs. the 05:

-To get the undertray off you need to loosen two screws holding the upper 'sockets' from under the tray.
-Cut the zip tie just below the tank.
-The indicators don't need to disassembled only unplugged and the holes for the wires in the under unit is slighly to small and needs to be drilled out a little bit.
-Ensure you cut the 'bill' all the way to the weld otherwise it won't fit because the screws supplied are so short.
