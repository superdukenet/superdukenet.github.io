TC's SD

2010-12-10 11:24:00 UTC
I have a set of Q2's on my SDR right now. So far I like them but since the weather is cold now I really can't compare them to the Pirelli Diablo Rosso Corsa tyres I had on this past summer. I can stay they have very good grip and track very well too. The rear tyre is not as tall as the Pirelli so there is less weight on the front tyre. I believe I will try another set next spring/summer. The price for a set is $100 less than the Pirelli's so as much riding as I do a lower priced tyre is a must. From what I read in a few tyre tests they rate the Q2's as good or better than most tyres out right now for track/ street use. I say for the price per set it is a MUST TRY tyre.
