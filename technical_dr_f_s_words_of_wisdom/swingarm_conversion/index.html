Hhhhmmmmm..... will have to call by the local Ducati dealer in the next few weeks and have a look at one. I'm not a big fan of altering geometry, KTM spent millions in R&D, so I doubt changing the arm will make it better. The numptystrada is the closest I've seen to fitting so it's worth a look. The pivot points on the SD swingarm are very narrow, so I would be suprised to find something else that will fit, but I won't write the idea off until I've seen one close up.
