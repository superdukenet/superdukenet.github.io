To sell or not to sell?

Hotbrakes

2009-11-05 00:40:00 UTC

I've had my 06 SD for 2 months now with over 6k miles covered in that time and I absolutely love riding the bike. But it seems like something breaks on it every other time I ride. I've had many bikes and nothing makes me despise a bike more than unreliability. And it all started within a few days of ownership...

1st Problem- Oil sight tube splits 2k miles from home
2nd- Recall on some piece of the oil tank or oil intake line (bike goes away for 8 days)
3rd- Starter torque limiter is loudly failing, unable to get much cooperation from dealer, unable to fix due to 4th problem
4th- Where is my coolant going? IN THE OIL! Why is there coolant in my oil? Water pump needs a rebuild, parts on backorder, winter fast approaching

Just wanted to vent. Its a great bike but if I'm dealing with these issues all the time I would be better off replacing it with another Big 4 brand. Typically I ride 20k+ miles a year. For those of you who have experienced these same issues, were they recurring? Or did KTM fix the design flaw rendering your bike perfect and reliable?

I was hoping the SD would be able to replace my VFR for touring as well but with the lack of luggage options and RELIABILITY issues that is an unrealistic dream. I got her at a good price, am willing to spend the money to make her healthy, but she must become dependable if she's gonna be mine.
