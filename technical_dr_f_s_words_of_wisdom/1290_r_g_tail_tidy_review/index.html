1290 R&G Tail Tidy Review

jmann

2014-03-19 10:14:00 UTC
Have just spent the day fitting and eventually modifying the R&G tail tidy.

On the surface this a very neat unit however not perfect. It is not perfect because R&G must have decided re-use the original indicators but not the plate illuminator. Instead, they supply a new branded illuminator that uses it's own wiring and connectors. If they had just used the original it would have been entirely plug-and-play and installed in 10 minutes. Instead, because I have an aversion to cutting bits of loom I modified the illuminator plate (drilled two holes in the correct position and enlarged the loom pass-through) and am waiting for the paint to dry. Once mounted it will appear to be the R&G product but using all original cables.

Drilling the holes was a real PITA because of all the angles making it hard to clamp down.
It's pressed mild steel and I wonder why they didn't make it from aluminium?

As I say I can't quite understand why they chose to do the half hearted thing but ultimately it will be very tidy. Score 6 out of 10 for installation. 8 out of 10 for function. 3 out of 10 for price but KTM is even worse
