Thought maybe the map is the problem: reload original map, try again: startmotor goes for about half a turn and nothing.

Anybody have a clue what I screwed up?

It was the battery, just started it using an old car battery! Thanks alot for the help guys!

Bert

2012-03-10 02:57:00 UTC
Check the ground wire on the battery and any other place a ground is used, for corrrosian and a solid connection. There are other connectors in that area, (battrey location) that can give a bad connection. That area is right in front of the front tyre which shoots water and road debris right into the electrical mess. Have a look before buying a new battrery. Let me mention once again the the grounding of electrical components are often overlooked and has giving me much fustration over the years. One thing good about a bad ground, it's usually cheap to fix.
