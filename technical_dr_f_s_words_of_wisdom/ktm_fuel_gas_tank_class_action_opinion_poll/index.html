Jim H

2014-03-05 19:01:00 UTC
Facebook KTM Fuel/Gas Tank Class Action Page:

https://www.facebook.com/pages/KTM-Fuel ... 0861631625

This thread has been generated in the interests of bringing class action against KTM for the following 990 Superduke, Adventure & Supermoto models due to the inadequate nature of the material compound specified & approved by KTM.

Detailed explanations of the opinion options are as follows:

1. Self explanatory

2. Subpoena KTM (Freedom of Information in UK) to establish compound & manufacturer of 1290 Superduke fuel tanks. Current owners to be consulted via NHTSA/VOSA & acquisition of their views of tank performance to date.

3. Stand alone steel fuel tank manufactured by company commissioned for 2014 KTM RC390's 9.5l steel fuel tank. This option maximises volumetric potential of fuel tank. Alternatively, smaller tank introduced prior to rotomould process to produce integral steel “polyamide skinned” tank – improved spark suppression but lower volumetric potential than stand alone option.

4. As above but plaintiffs will offer modest financial contribution* to class action process in view of producing steel variant of fuel tank for models mentioned above.

* To be expressed as % of manufacturing costs. Cost to be determine & disclosed to plaintiffs.

Class action lawyers have powers to contact owners via NHTSA/VOSA for direct communication.

Please see the following for more details on polyamide compounds, correspondences with polyamide companies & steel fuel tank variant/rust proofing methods:

viewtopic.php?f=5&t=22369

Aluminium not yet considered due to material/labour costs. Process benefits realised by commissioning of RC390 steel fuel tank manufacturer at this time are more appealing.
