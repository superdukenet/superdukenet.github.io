Just back from the dyno...

Aegis Bearing Mel

2008-04-07 13:48:00 UTC
That's my lovely wee beastie just had her custom mapping done through at YPE in Bonnyrigg.
Need to send the dude through a usb pen so I can get the graph from him, but looking at it on site seems to have made a nice wee improvement.
A moderate 109 hp, with an increase of roughly five cuddies across the range.
Not the most powerful of steeds, but HUGELY changed on the motorway ride home.
Couldn't play too much due to the sleet, snow, sun, BLIZZARD, sun, rain conditions, but she is uber smooth now.
The hesitancy and fluffiness at 5k up has totally gone, happy to hold 3k in third with no shoogles, which is pleasingly 30mph.

All in all, well happy with it.
He did a map for both cylinders individually, looking at it both are massively different, which is reassuring.
Only took a couple of hours, so time enough for a couple of bacon 'n egg rolls, two coffees and a read of MCN.


Don't feel that the cash really justifies itself for the little power gains alone.
However, for the ride difference and mucho smoothness, I have no qualms at all about recommending it.
I'll need to go for a proper wee play and see how it feels, but so far I'm as happy as a fat alcoholic in a licenced kebab shop!!
