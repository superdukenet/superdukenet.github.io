This is not typical nor structural for the SD.

I can be lively at WOT acceleration but not more than other light and powerful unfaired bike with wide bars and a sharp geometry. Shouldn't be scary at any point. During the last week I've been ending wheelies at 100mph...

Indeed check the usual things, tyres for pressure, balancing and roundness, suspension settings, wheel alignment, possible tension between the forklegs, free spinning brakes and last but not least, head bearings.

Are you either very light or very heavy? Do you wear a parachute for a jacket?

The same thing happening with your previous SM950 is 'statistically challenging' to say the least...

If everything fails, get your Monster back.
