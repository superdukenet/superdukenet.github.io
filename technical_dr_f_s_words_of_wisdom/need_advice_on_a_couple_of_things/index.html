Hey guys. It would be great if anyone could help me out.

1. Bike is leaking oil near the kickstand. It's not leaking at a rate that's alarming, probably a drop every 2 hours. Bike is currently near the max capacity on the sump as well (before had too little oil so decided to top it off).
2. Shifting through gear is not smooth at all. Only time where it's not rough is when I'm shifting around 4k-5k and driving at 40+ mph.
3. Blinkers blink at different rates. On idle, they blink at the correct speed most of the time, but sometimes start blinking faster. Both lights are working. Happens on both left/right, and also giving it gas will cause them to blink faster.

Any thoughts?

I have felt shifting ease fall off with deteriorated oil. I have seen what I called the neutral gear indicator seal leaking. This is, in my belief, what the gear indicator previously referred to is. If you are noticing clutchless upshifts more difficult I would not be looking at slaves or masters at this point. I have witnessed on two previuos occasions that signal blinking iradically is a sure sign that it was time to change the battery. This behavior was eliminated with the advent of a new one on both occasions. You are feeling the shifting through the nuetral gear and into second gear in your upshift.
