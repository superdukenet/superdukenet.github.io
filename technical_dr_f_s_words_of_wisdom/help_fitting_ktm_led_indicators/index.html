Sowewhere there, near right side connectors you should find two auxiliary equipment connectors, looking like this:



One of them on the side of the sleeve should have label "ACC1" - this is the one the instruction manual asking you to connect to. You use only spade male connector, spade female must stay spare, just in case you wish to connect something there in the future.
If you don't have them in your Duke (I can have a look on the drawing later on, not sure if those were available on 2006 SD) just connect to any negative wire you can find.

Is your Duke 2005/2006 model with older type speedometer? Because as I can see on the drawing even that one has ACC connection available, but may be located in different place, not necessarily behind headlight. I don't know this model, as I have 2008 R, but that wire to me can be connected to any ground you have access to.
The closest one may be on the right side bottom 12-pin connector. There is brown wire on the terminal 1, that should work, as really all you need is ground connection, just use piece of spare wire to join them together, then check. I can't see your bike flasher circuit being any different to later ones, so it should work just fine.
Where did you get new flasher relay from? I never saw anything like it. If it won't work maybe we should have closer look at that relay, as it is really straight forward and should work straight out of the box.
