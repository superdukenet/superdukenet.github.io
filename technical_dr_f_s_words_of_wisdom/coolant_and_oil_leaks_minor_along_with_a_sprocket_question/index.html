As Lowrance says - the oil sight tube is more likely to be split than a leak at the washer; this is exactly what happened to mine. Easy fix being either to replace the part or just the tube - you can lean the bike over enough to do this without loosing any oil.

Cush drive should be 45nm with loctite - I also put a little grease on the posts to reduce the scuffing/wear; I don't think it says to do this in the manual but seems to help.

For the coolant leak I would check your radiator cap for any crap (ie casting sand) - as if this clogs the whole system tends to over pressurize which causes small leaks elsewhere in the system. Again, I've had the same tiny leak at the thermostat which turned out to be the casting sand in the radiator cap and small amount in the expansion tank; this is after flushing the system extensively twice before ! This is probably why the water pump seal goes prematurely - so if you haven't changed coolant I would recommend doing this as its cheap to do. Read up on the bleed procedure though, as they don't run well with air in the system.
