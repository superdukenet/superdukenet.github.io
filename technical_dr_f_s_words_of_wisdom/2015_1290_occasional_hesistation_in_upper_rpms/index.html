2015 1290 occasional hesistation in upper rpms

ktmguy

2015-07-06 01:04:00 UTC

I picked up this beast new May 1. It's been a mix of utter joy and a couple nagging issues. First was my oil pressure light staying on for a couple seconds longer than it should and flickering at idle requiring a shot of throttle to shut it off until the bike was warm. That turned out to be the oil filter getting all warped after about 2500 km of use after the first service. I switched to a K&N filter and it's been great for the last 3000 kms now.

The last week I've been battling with intermittent hesitation in the upper end of the rpm range usually around 7000 and up. Sometimes, it happens sometimes it doesn't. First happened only for an evening. I thought maybe it was bad gas as it went away later that night after topping up from a different pump. Was good for a week but then again yesterday it's started acting up again and persisted this morning. When it first happened again, I threw some Lucas injector cleaner into it and ran it through a tank. Seemed better this morning but it reared its ugly head again later in the day on the highway. I've got an AR GP3 decat and K&N filter, SAS removed with the Akra map installed via the dealer. The problem began to occur after the parts had been installed and in use for a couple weeks. I'm going to be putting the Rottweiler intake on this coming Wednesday and was going to ask them to check my fuel filter, lines and spark plugs to see if anything looks off. Anything else I should consider here?

Thanks for any help.
