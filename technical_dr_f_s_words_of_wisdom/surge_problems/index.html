topendz

2008-08-11 10:58:00 UTC
I took the new toy out for a blast with Dutch yesterday and had a very enjoyable run, but was plagued by hectic surge issues along the way. 07 SD with dealer fitted/mapped Akra's.

We headed into the essex badlands and around about halfway I got reall issues with surge. I'd head into a corner, brake, shut the throttle and tip in, and then on the way out open the throttle again and the bike would bog down for a second or two before getting a kick up the arse in and finally kicking in. As you could imagine it was a PITA, making me change lines mid corner etc. After we stopped for tea it got worse.

After heading home on the M11 for about 20mins or so we hit the 406 and once back in london it was fine. It felt strong and smooth, and didn't encounter any probs. Nice long 1st gear wheelie off the lights in fact!

Any ideas? Already spoken to the dealer and they're more than happy to take a look at it/book it in if necessary when I drop by next week to pick up some parts.
