An easy out or extractor might not work. If there is any bit of bolt sticking out try to weld a nut on it. You might have to do that few times as it breaks of easily but it works without damaging anything.
If no meat left try a left turning drill, sometimes that is enough to just grab the bolt and unscrew it.
If this fails an easy out might be the only option left but I find that in low quality screws and bolts like this it makes it expand and harder to get out.

If this doesn't work drilling it out completely and an heli coil is the only option.

Tip, don't use a torque wrench on these small bolts, use a 1/4" driveT handle and develop a feel for it. Hand tight plus 1/8 of a turn approx...
