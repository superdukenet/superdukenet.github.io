The LSL damper kit will not fit 07/08 models unless they are making a new mounting kit, of which there is no mention in the LSL website.

In their website it is specified the kit will fit the 05/06 SD.

In the french SD-Forum the subject was already discussed and a guy bought the damper (in april this year) just to find out that it doesn't fit his 07 bike.

This picture comes from LSL and shows a 06 SD with the damper



This one is taken from the forum and shows the same one on a 07 SD



As you can see the two tanks have pretty different shape around the mounting point. The red arrows show where the damper touches the tank.
