Viking

2012-05-14 13:47:00 UTC
Good haggling. Nice one.

Only problem I see though is I can't see it giving any real gains. The 07> geo is raked out a bit, so as you push the forks though it has a more significant effect on the trail figure. Eg, every 3-4 mm you drop the forks it adds 1mm trail.

With the 05 yokes being steeper, pushing the forks through won't have the same effect and will do more for just raising the front of the bike rather than increasing trail.

There is a bit of setting up with the Nichols. As in, it's not just a case of throwing it on and dropping the forks 15-20mm. The fork preload has to be adjusted to kinda compensate for the added height. If not the bike does become more stable, but it also reduces load on the front with it being higher. So you end up pushing the front as you try to get on the throttle. As I found and it confused me for a bit. So you go from losing the front due to too steep geo, to almost losing the front due to lack of load.

I got to a place where the front was almost dialed in and I could finally start pushing on a bit, but the front tyre was graining and if pushed in certain corners it ran wide. I've traced this to the std 160 spring being too soft, so even though static and rider sags were in range it was squating under load in the corner. Upped it to 180, and surprisingly sags are able to be set identical to the 160 spring which you wouldn't think would be the case.


Honestly, the difference between 05 and 07 isn't like the 07 is a tank to steer. Even with 07 geo and the forks 13mm through the Nichols it still turns fast.


Not being negative dude. Just my 2p worth. I don't think it will give the benefits that are being targeted. The Nichols was designed with the bike on a chassis measurement computer. I don't think we can really just guess at this. An 05 would need to be measured, them forks dropped 15mm and re-measured to see what the outcome is.


In saying all that though. It's all rider specific. There's guys like Jody doing 1min 45s round Cadwell on std 05 geo. It matches his style.

I personally just couldn't get on with the front end so have had to go the this length to get it where it feels confident for me.
