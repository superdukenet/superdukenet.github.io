Superdan

2011-06-22 08:19:00 UTC
http://www.g2ergo.com/g2-quick-turn-thr ... ube-1.html

This is the "quick action" that most of use believe it comes with whats called a 50 cam, some use a 25 cam for even shorter twist.

You can also get the cntrol kit from them which comes with a 100(same as stcok I think) 200 and 300 cam, albeit I dont think they quicken the throttle.

So in essence if you got a 50 cam and 100cam you could swap for track and road.

Do you find the bike snatchy or smooth?

If smooth go for quick action.

If snatchy, make sure you have done all the mods that cure it the most. O2 elims, Tune ECU/powercommander, THEN get the quick action.
