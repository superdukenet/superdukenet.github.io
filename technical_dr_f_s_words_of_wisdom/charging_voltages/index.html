Sounds in the ball park.
You actually gave the answer yourself. Optimate = OK
Running it every day = OK (menas it is charging)
Standing still = not OK Means battery is fooked.
The SD needs a good battery to get going (as other v twins) and it seems depending on the abuse like temperature and frequency of riding it is done for in about 2 years or so.
This is the reason I gone for a antigravity lifepo model. See here: viewtopic.php?f=5&t=18373
In my bike the bat had an easy life, always stored inside, ridden regular and kept on trickle charger when I went overseas to preserve it. Still it gone downhill after 2.5 years.
Couldn't believe ho much better it started with the new bat.
