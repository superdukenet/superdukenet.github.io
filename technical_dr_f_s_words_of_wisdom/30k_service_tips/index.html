"AND my return throttle cable busted, WTF. Should have lubed it beforehand but it is over 30k"

Had that happen on one of my high mileage Ninjas. Coming back from a trip up in the Canadian Rockies. I was pushing my bike around in a camp site with the motor off on Mount Rainer. I like the slack taken out of the throttle cables and I think I over did it. I pushed on the loud handle and felt the cable snap. Great ! Luck had it it was the return cable and made it home without issue. It was a worry about the throtte sticking open but kept my eyes on the kill switch. Make sure you have the cables adjusted so the hard stops do the stoping instead of the tight cable does the stoping.
