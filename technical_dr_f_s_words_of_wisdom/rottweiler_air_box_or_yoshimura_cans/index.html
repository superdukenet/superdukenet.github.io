Hi guys.

I'm looking for a little advice.

Over the last few months I've scraped together enough cash to replace the stock air box with a Rotti air box, remove the sas and replace the stock exhaust with a set of Yoshimura cans.

ktmguy

2014-09-03 21:45:00 UTC

No point doing the air filter without the exhaust.
Exhaust first and it looses weight and gains looks in the process.
You have to block off the SAS or switch it off via tuneecu.

You can always do both and ride the missus to work on the back!
