Been looking to do this for a while.

Got my mate up with the corner weight scales we use to set up the caterhams and hillclimb cars.

Fired the old SD trackslag on......

Front weight- 94kg
Rear weight- 86kg.
Total- 180kg exactly!

That's real world weight.
3/4 tank of fuel and oil and coolant levels on max.

Main places the weight has been shed are.....
No lights
Fibreglass tank panels
Shorai battery
Then there's the hundreds of small things like no switch gear, rear side panels, horn, sidestand etc etc etc.


Happy with that, combined with its 125bhp at the wheel it's a pretty capable little tool. Shows how potent a sorted SD would look on paper if you quoted like manufactures specs of dry weight and crank bhp.

Just of the top of my head that would be say 145bhp at the crank and 165kg dry weight? I imagine with a bit of engine mods and scraping every kg off it with carbon etc we could see over 150crank bhp and under 160kg. The press would be wetting their knickers over specs like that!
