Yes the self arming is immobilisor only, not the alarm. The loom connection has the immobilisor circuits 'broken' that are connected in order for the bike to run by either the disarmed alarm or the plug. Having the plug there made it really easy to find all the wiring required to fit a non-KTM alarm!!
