Fuel Exhausts now make cans and Y piece for the SD

RRR

2011-11-25 21:33:00 UTC
Hey all

Fuel Exhausts now make cans and Y piece for our beloved SD's . The quality is superb and all parts are hand made . I took my SD down and they used it as a pattern , straight off Damian and Louise were as friendly as i remember from dealing with them on another bike for me .

We decided on 350mm stainless ovals mounted flat like the 916 . I collected the bike the other day and was blown away by the quality .

Even had them noise checked with baffles in and baffles out . Got readings of 94db with baffles in and 102db with baffles out . Measured 50cm away and 45 deg offset at 4500rpm .

Will let the pictures do the talking .















Price is expected to be around £450 to £475

Due to crappy conditions riding home couldn't really push on very hard , but bike ran very well if a touch rich due to baffles being fitted when my bike is mapped for a motobox and open pipes . Decided to play it safe as the pipework is a little bigger than my previously fitted Graves cans .
