520 Conversion Kit

Lowrance

2011-03-29 03:03:00 UTC

Due to a forced sabatical (fell off supermoto bike at Adam's a week ago), broken collar bone and a couple of ribs, I've decided it's time to focus on the SD. Got over 10K miles so it's about time for a new chain and sprockets. Was gonna go 525 but decided against it as the 520 parts were so reaonably priced.

I just purchased a 16T CS sprocket and two 38T rears (7075-T6 - black of course). The front was $25 and the rears were $56. Also purchased a DID 520ZVMX Gold chain for $135. This should be a pretty stout set up that will eliminate just under 2lbs of rotational (and partial unsprung) weight as well as free up a couple of ponies at the wheel. Will look the dogs bollox as well.

Happy to post the suppliers if anyone is interested. Pretty good prices on this stuff.

John
