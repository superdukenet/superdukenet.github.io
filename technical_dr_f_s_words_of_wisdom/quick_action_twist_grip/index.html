Hi Allan P, I pretty much quick throttle every bike I get. Its easy to do yourself, costs nothing and takes about 10 minutes. If you pull your throttle tube off you'll see where the cables run on the outer part of it, its about 5mm wide (which is conveniently the same size as a zip tie). Cut a zip tie to length then glue it to where the cables usually run on your throttle tube. Reassemble and jobs done.
