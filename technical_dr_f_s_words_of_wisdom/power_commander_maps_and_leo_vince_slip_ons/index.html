i'm not sure where you guys are getting your dyno's done but it sucks they won't map both cylinders for you. Both dynojet tuning centres i've used were happy to, for an extra small fee. Pull out the 02 sensors and you're in business.


Lamb, I had a similar situation with mine. I had a nice 'surge of power at about 7000rpm. Turned out my bike was mapped too lean through the midrange (13.5:1) and needed to be richened up a bit (13:1) cos the lc8 likes it a bit rich. Try add about 2-6% fuel with the 'mid' button on your power commander and see what that does. From memory that's about the change they made on mine when I had the map checked out.

On another note, since you have a power commander and can map your bike properly, you may find that getting rid of the LV baffles will lose you torque, this motor actually needs some back pressure to get the most out of it. My custom cans had 50mm outlets, I put baffles in them to reduce them to 36mm (half the area) and picked up a few ft/lb of torque in the midrange.



Runfile009 is 50mm outlet
Runfile024 is 31mm outlet
Runfile028 is 36mm outlet after checking a/f

The main difference is that the 50mm outlet has piss weak midrange. Tried with 31mm baffles were too small. I'd say go with the drilled baffles that some other LV users on here are sporting perhaps.
