Hi Gregz,

Excellent post and definitley a talking point...

Yep, I agree with the 16T, Akras and the PC/Tuneboy etc.

I have the gel seat and I find it too hard for any serious riding. I should have bought a standard black seat (I thought the orange seat was a poor colour match).

Quick throttle mod. Nutter !!!!

Heatwrapped zorsts? Hmmm, not keen on this one. How about this? http://www.camcoat.u-net.com/Exhausts.htm

Levers. Yep, great mod but I can't be arsed as the stock ones are fine for me.


Others I've done...

Scottoiler. Keeps the chain lubed so you don't have to. Proven to increase chain life by up to 7 times and loses less power than a dry chain. So far after 1800 miles I still haven't even had to adjust the chain tension! Only drawback is the chain guard is too short so oil flicks up. I have lengthened it by 2" to help with this.

Bar riser kit. Makes the riding position a tad more upright and comfortable (and helps with wheelies - so I'm told)

Heated grips. Oxford ones fitted to my SD. Slightly thicker grips than stock but keep the fingers toasty. Not the best solution for long trips but OK for me.

Black gel seat (see above). Far to hard and makes my arse feel like it's been kicked by a donkey after 1/2 an hour riding.

Alarm. One of those 'gotta have' mods, sadly.

16T sprocket. Oh, yes

Akra Evos. nice but need to adjust the dB killers like yours

Crash bungs. Need I say more?



Extras to do. . .

Ceramic exhaust coating.Keep the temp down and cover up the discoloured headers. Cheap too!

Longer hugger to keep crap off the (expensive) WP rear shocker.

Low pressure turbo kit. Garrett T25 or similar IHI unit limited to 0.5 bar with intercooler and recirculating blow off valve. May need to lower the compression to 8:1 for this mod.
