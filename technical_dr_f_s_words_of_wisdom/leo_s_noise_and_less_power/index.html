Fack me these things are loud !!! Buy shares in earplug companies guys, I'm going on a buying spree!

Thanks Plipton, but I got some great news - Went for a quick 150km trip, and the power has returned - In fact, doing around 6 top gear runs it felt it pulled to 200kph a bit harder - Not huge, but there, and easy to feel - so HAPPY DAYS!!!
Oh, and they're so loud they drown out windroar at 180kph!!!

I guess the ecu must have needed a chance to adjust to the pipe, I can't think anything else - ambient temp is pretty close to the first ride.

How loud? On around 50% throttle I had 4 cars swerve away in fright, and most stare wide-eyed when coming up from behind. And 2 neighbours yanked open their kitchen curtains to see what was going on, when I came home . I'm going to be in trouble soon, one was reaching for something, a gun or a cell, I don't know !
Oh, very little popping on the overrun, little ones and not always.

Other good news? Yes! Before, I couldn't even maintain a steady throttle under 3000rpm without engine shudder, - now I can run as low as 2500 and even accelerate slightly, and no shuddering at all!!!
Other? The gearbox was great before the service. Now, it's almost telepathic! I put in motul 300V 15w50 (ktm didn't have motorex ) and so far it seems to be a good choice!

So,
Smoother
Quicker
Louder (by a fook-load )
Lighter (by the same measurement as above )
Maybe not the best-looking pipes, but already growing on me and shout 'competition' imo.

Did I mention the cans were loud?


(sorry guys, I know you all have pipes and they are nothing new to you, but just last week I was close to selling the bike due to financial problems (sob sob, poor me ), but a promotion means I can keep it, AND get the cans! So I'm like a 5 year old at Christmas - beyond mere excitement!)
