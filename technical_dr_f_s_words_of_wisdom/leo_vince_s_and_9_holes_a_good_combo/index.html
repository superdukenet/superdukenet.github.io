Its a good idea to add more holes to the plates.as in standard form they do look very restrictive to flow with just the original 5 holes..... Drf had already sussed this and drilled some extra holes in the plates when i purchased the Leo's off him,i just decided to remove the plates completely from the system,didnt experience any fuelling issues like this on the standard mapping,but with the ktm open pipe or akro map as they call it its very very good...... admitidly they do sound loud but nice in this form,and have no desire to see how louder they would be with the rear silencer baffles removed .... keep plod happy if pulled anyway as they still look legal with them in and E stamped.
