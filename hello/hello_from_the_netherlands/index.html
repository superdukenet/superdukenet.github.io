2011-12-01 08:55:00 UTC
Hi, just thought I'd introduce myself. I have a mostly stock (other than Yoshi pipes) 2008 SD which I bought in the UK in September, the bike has been off the road since then as I'm going through the process of registering it in Holland and getting Dutch plates and insurance. The most time consuming thing in the process has been getting the right hand traffic headlight which I eventually picked up from a scrap dealer on ebay. The bike's been inspected and all I need to do now is pay the import duty. Why go to all this hassle? Well with the exchange rate the way it is I can pick up the SD in the UK for 5500 GBPs, in Holland the same bike goes for a minimum of about €8000 (7000GBP) (2008 SDs from dealers here are more like €10,000). Previously I've owned a Speed Triple, Fireblade, Firestorm, SV650 and a VFR to name a few. Riding the Duke is just so much fun, even the stock exhaust sounds lurvely and the motor is just crazy. I have a whole list of bits to fiddle with (tail tidy, engine sliders) but first its got to be the exhaust. The Yoshi pipes will have to go which is a shame as they sound awesome and with the ECU map they give the bike a kick but they're very loud (even with baffles) and they have no ECC stamp on them, unfortunately the police are a bit keen here by the look of things. I think I'll sell them in favour of some Remus cans which are more on the road legal side (ECC stamp and removable baffle).

Anyhow, hello to you all!
