Hello everyone. I am a new SD owner from Seattle, Washington State.

I have been lusting after a Superduke for quite sometime now but they aren't very common over here. Most had high miles, rebuilt titles or were just beat up looking. After about a year of checking my local classifieds, I found a white 2008 SD with 2,400 miles and all stock, besides tail tidy! Of course I had to have it and drove down to Portland, Oregon and bought it then rode it back 150ish miles in the pouring rain. WELL WORTH IT!

I have owned the bike exactly 8 days so far and LOVE IT. My other bike is a 2010 Husqvarna SM510R Supermotard, which is a blast. I sold a 2000 Honda RC51 so I could buy the SD. I like the more upright riding position of the supermotards and naked sport bikes.

I have already installed Pazzo black billet shorty levers. I have also ordered the FMF Apex exhaust, KTM axle sliders, Motohooligan frame sliders and G2ergonimics quick turn throttle tube. I will be getting the Akrapovic map flashed when I install the FMF's. Before summer I plan to get a rear spring for my weight, SAS removal, Motohooligan airbox, secondary butterfly removal and get a custom tune.

Anyways, here is a picture of the bike the day after I got it with a quick clean up.





April 11th EDIT:

FMF Apex, akropovic map, ktm axle sliders, motohooligan upper frame sliders (lowers on their way) and g2 quick turn throttle (50 cam), ktm grips.
