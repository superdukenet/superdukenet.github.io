Yeah so I've been riding all manner of bikes for the past 40 yrs. Always wanted one of these, this becomes available but I have to let one of the bikes go....so I sold my WR400 supermoto to make the room.

Bike is really well setup, pcV w/proper tune, stacks, airbox, the exhaust, anodised bolt kit, rizoma farkles, carbon fiber sprinkles, the damper (keen eye on that one!). The motor is awesome, I still have to dial in the damping, the suspenders were just rebuilt front & rear with the addition of air bleeds on the fork caps but I've got a serious mid corner wallowing (that's the only way I can describe it) that happens in the presence of any significant bumps. I start another thread for that in time.

I took a brake from super sports for a while and since 2011 had 2 supermotos (WR400 & SMS630). Got rid of the WR to make room for the SD, rode it once and sold the SMS a few days later. Unless I decide to return to the track I don't think I'll ever see another SS in the garage. So far I love this bike. The sound it makes is intoxicatingly awesome.
