When I bought my 990 back in 2016 I thought that I would naturally progress up to a 1290 after a couple of years, my touring bike at the time was a Vstrom 650.
Eventually the performance difference between the two bikes became just too annoying and so at the end of 2018 I sold the Vstrom and bought an 1190 Adventure. This pairing seems pretty good.

The 1190 is more... more power, more comfort, more range, more mpg.. it's a great bike... but the 990 has shown how delayed the bigger/newer KTM engines are. The fly by wire throttle is slow compared to instant reaction you get from the 990. The 990 feels raw and makes a lovely noise through the Akras.

I think the contrast between the two keeps me interested in both right now. If I were to get a 1290 SD I'm not sure they'd feel so different from each other, well apart from their size, but I have heard the 1290 SD's feel a bit porky in the corners.
Also I don't think the 990 is lacking in power for road use... so in a way the 1190 has killed the want of a 1290 SD.
