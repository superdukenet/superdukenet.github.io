The Brisbane boy who ditched the Fireblade for the SD

Agent Orange

2008-07-07 12:54:00 UTC

Quite literally actually..
Little did I know that fateful day in the end of March when my Repsol Fireblade fell over the cliff between Mt Nebo and Mt Glorious that a few months later I would be on the fun machine of the century! I can see now that it was probably the best thing that happened to me for a while.

This was my second venture off track on that road but this time the tow truck had to drag it out and the insurance company write it off..

15 or so test rides later (including the Speed/Street Triple, new Fireblade and the B-King) I'm now a proud owner of a 07 Anthracite beauty less than 9 months old.. I picked it up this weekend in Wollongong and drove 1100km's back this weekend.

I'm in love! I'been looking for something special and I believe I've found it!
Even a ride to the video store is a ton of fun!

There's nothing more to it, this thing is an absolute hoot!
