Hello to everyone!

I've been reading the forums for about a month now and learning a lot of things about my new bike. I actually saw the bike for the first time a few months ago when a friend sent me pictures of the SDR. I instantly fell in love with the bike and even had it as my wallpaper even though my bike's a Ducati.

When I went to Malaysia for the Sepang round of MotoGP, I decided to visit a custom bike show and was gob-smacked when I saw an SDR that was on display. Little did I know that my local Ducati dealer (who also sells KTM) had one that just arrived. Upon my return, I decided to visit my dealer to get some parts for my bike when I saw the SDR as soon as I walked in. Suffice it to say, I couldn't resist drooling over the bike and after reading the posts here as well as many other articles, I was sold. I talked to my dealer and the deal was made! I am now the proud owner of a new SDR!

At any rate, just wanted to say hello to everyone and thanks for all the great info in the forum!

Cheers.
AJ
