2012-10-26 19:03:00 UTC
Hi all,

I'm all new to this KTM business, having been a fan for Suzuki GSXR's for the past 4 years. I really loved my GSXR's but it wasn't until I sold the last 750 that I realised I must have become used to, or even bored with the bike.

So, it took me 2 weeks and I had started to look at more upright rides with an aggressive street fighter look.... and hey I bought myself an 07 Super Duke R - in "SITH" black!

I've just returned from riding it the 30 or so miles home in the freezing cold, ( all my gear is summer stuff - but that's never really stopped me before, I did 4000 miles over Christmas after I passed my test!).

First impressions are - WOW! She's quite an aggressive beast isn't she!? The furious amount of torque just babbling away, waiting to be unleashed is quite something!

After a few miles and some bends, the bike started to feel more normal, which is reassuring after being so low down on the GSXR, it did feel quite odd to be sat so upright into a corner.

Love it, Love it, Love it.

Yoshi cans have been ordered.......

Nice one.
