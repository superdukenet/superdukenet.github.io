Hello Everyone!

I'm the proud owner of a new 2016 1290 Super Duke R. I park it next to my 2007 950 Super Moto and they get along just great. In Montana most KTM riders own KTM dirt bikes. In fact most know nothing about KTM street bikes. I'm trying to change that Every where I ride I get looks and question about the "Beast" What is it? How much does it cost? How fast will it go? Where can I get one? Can I ride it on the dirt?

Anyway, Love the bike... don't care for the speedometer that doesn't tell me the truth. Trying to get KTM to do something about that and pointing out that hundreds of other owners are spending their hard earned money having to buy speed sensor rings not made by KTM to get the real speed from their bikes. Other that that this bike is absolutely amazing. Too bad I only have about 5 months a year to ride before the weather stops me. But, for those 5 months I'll be riding every minute I can.

Ride safe and Ride on..........

Wayne

This made my dealer quite angry at KTM and he told me that he felt this wasn't right. He suggested calling KTM customer service which I guess is my next step.

I thought I would just update this post to say that it looks like KTM still isn't taking any responsibility for this.
