Thought id better say gidday to all you Superduke fans. Havin had dozens of Japers, 3 ducati's and 2 Aprilia's over 30 years of riding bikes, I was looking for something a tad different from sportsbikes to replace my 05 Aprilia RSVR. After months of surfin the net for reviews, this forum, etc, the 07 KTM was looking bloody good. My local KTM dealer only had the R model in stock but it just isnt me with a brick for a seat and a single seat, been there, done that and never again. So i ordered an 07 Orange Duke with Akra Evolution pipes and a few other bits. The Duke is down on HP from the Aprilia's but im sure it will more than make up for it in the BUZZ factor on my home East Coast twisty roads, so roll on next week, party time with a Superduke.
