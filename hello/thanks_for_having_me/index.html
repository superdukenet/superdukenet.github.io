Thanks for having me

Paulzilla

2013-07-15 14:20:00 UTC
I live in Saint Paul Minnesota. I had a KTM 950 Supermoto for the last 4 years and I loved riding the bike. I just didn't love the way it looked. At the time there were several Super Dukes for sale in the area, so I put the Supermoto up for sale with the intention of buying one of the SDs. By the time my bike sold the Super Dukes were gone. It took several weeks of looking and a SDR came up for sale in Denver Colorado for a fair price. I flew to Denver and bought the bike. After the longest wait in a DMV line I had ever endured, I took a canyon ride up to Estes Park. Then next day I rode it back home.

Sadly the previous owner is a twit. The bike was supposed to be in showroom condition and he was supposed to be super meticulous about care and maintenance, It's now clear he is just an idiot. For example,
he had removed the rear fender and turn signals and replaced them with a small aftermarket bracket and tiny turn signals. He had twisted the wires together and wrapped them with duct tape. Nether turn signal worked and one was missing it's lens.

The bike only had 4300 miles on it, so I'm hoping he hasn't done any other "improvements".

I do like the bike and I'll be asking lots of questions here about Super Dukes.
