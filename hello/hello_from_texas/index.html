Hello, Gentlemen:

Well, I first came across this site in 2010 when I was contemplating purchasing a Super Duke, but having gone a different direction at that time, I never registered or participated here. Not that I wasn't tempted anyway by all the clever banter and technical know-how. But I digress.

Like recent addition Scott Miller from Michigan, I too, am the new, lucky and giddy owner of a 2008 R. I found the bike in Arizona and it was lust at first site. Think "Sofia Vergara" and that will just about explain the attraction. Of course I had seen R models before...lots and lots of pics anyway...but something about this one kept whispering sweet nothings in my ear. So I closed the deal.

The bike is not in my possession quite yet but when it arrives I will certainly post some new pics and provide my KTM-newbie impressions for everyone to nod knowingly over. Or shoot down in flames. Whatever.

Details on the bike include that while mods by the previous owner (the original owner at that) have been few, they have been well targeted, at least in my opinion anyway. Mileage is 3,340. The bike has the full Akra treatment including the ECU remapping from the dealer download; it has a Suter slipper clutch; it has a virtually new set of Dunlop Q2's with only 100 miles on them; it has had the O2 sensors removed with resistors wired in; it has the "canisterectomy" with block-off plates and hoses removed; always full synth Motorex oil and OEM filters, except for the DNA air filter; and it has R&G frame sliders. The owner didn't like the shroud decals so he took them off, but he also bought a complete replacement set to sell with the bike. Cool touch and good thinking. In fact, all stock bits including the exhaust and the clutch pack and sensors etc are coming with the bike.



Same guy currently has a beautiful 1098R on eBay.

Anyway, I'm looking forward to experiencing the bike and getting the benefit of the combined knowledge and wisdom of all you liars, cheats and rubber-shredders here. I expect to learn a lot!

Cheers,
Steve
