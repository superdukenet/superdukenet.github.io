2009-11-16 03:34:00 UTC
I'm in South Orange Curtain (Laguna).

Ordered the bike a couple weeks ago through Three Brothers Racing in Costa Mesa, California.

I had all the mods planned and ordered when I bought the bike. Yes, Christmas came early.

I've had my eye on the SDR for quite some time. Been reading and learning in this forum. Such a great resource. I really enjoy wrenching and the SDR seemed like it would be fun to ride and fun and easy to work with.

You can sense KTM's dirt bike background when you tear into the bike. This is one beefy bike. The air box design is odd but the Motobox will take care of that. The battery placement is also odd, but I guess it's better down low than up high. And that catalytic converter, the definition of heavy, wow! I know there are a lot of complaints about the snatchy throttle and it does seem a bit abrupt off idle but I'm hoping the mods might smooth things out a bit. But my other bike is an MV Agusta Brutale 910r and it's a bit of an all or nothing in the fueling department as well... but a custom ECU has fixed that recently.

Anyway, I'm still hoping this thing fires when I get everything back together. Changing so many things at one, not so bright, but I've followed forum directions to a T, so hopefully all will be well!!!
