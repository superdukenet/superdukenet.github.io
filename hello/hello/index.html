Hello from a SD newb in Sanford Fl. Picked up my white 08 Sd about two weeks ago and love it more with each ride. But the truth is i didnt even want this bike it was sort of a last resort.

After crashing my aprilia tuono (broken collarbone and 10k damage to the bike ) about two months ago I started looking at new bikes. The SD WAS at the top of the list ..... Then I started reading this forum !! Holy crap it scared the shit out of me, snatchy throttle this crap fueling that ,locked up brakes bad this and bad that. And on top of that no 09s for the USA. Could not bring myself to buy the damn thing.

Looked at almost every euro naked you can get in the USA. Ducati monster 1100s (built for people who top out at 5'10 max and 14k for 87 rwhp?) Triumph speed trip (nice bike but again small and cheapish parts) BMW k1200r(nice and large but so is the price and no end to its mechanical issues ) and so on.

So im looking at the ktm web site and if you buy a SD you get a 1400 dollar accessory kit for free ! So on top of the dealer knocking the price down to 10300 bucks and the 5.9% intrest rate I had no choice but to buy it. And im glad I did! Its not the monter some of you made it out to be. Dont know why I had fear of it at all! Its not alot more snatchy then my aprilla was and I would have to say its less snatchy then the monster s2r I owned. I dont get the "hunting" thing I read so much about (maybe im lucky?) And above all its not made for midgets!

Bought it with the intention of trading it in on the new multi strada when it comes out but now im thinking Im going to stick with the SD.
