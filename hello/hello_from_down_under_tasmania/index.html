Hello fellow super duke hooligans!

My names Michael and i live near hobart in tasmania.

Lets just start by saying i love bikes and i love riding, it is such a big part of my life, done a few track days and enjoy a spirited ride down the coast of tasmania with my mates.

i have known about these bikes for a few years now, a good friend of mine got one a couple of years ago and swears by it!

some where in my mind i always wanted one.
recently sold my 2008 gsxr 1000 and got on the hunt for a super duke.

after many weeks of searching for a good deal i was about to make a purchaase on a standard 07 SD when a SD-R popped up for only 1000 more than i was going to spend with only 3000 KMs on it.

i jumpped on it before anyone else could.

the deal is done and it is on the way to me at the moment.

so for the last few weeks ive been looking around this forum and reading all about these awsome machines.

Nice to meet you all, im looking forward to sharing my KTM adventures with you all soon!


Cheers
