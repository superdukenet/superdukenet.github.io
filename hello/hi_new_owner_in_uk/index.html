Hello and welcome.

I too had an RSV and moved to the Superduke, not been disappointed in 6 years of owning the Superduke.

Also like you, I had a spine injury, not as severe as yours sounds. In order to make life a little more comfortable I had the suspension at both ends modified, which included a softer spring at the rear. Most will say the softer spring is a route to poor handling. Well I guess http://www.reactivesuspension.com/ did a cracking good job, because I have no complaints.
