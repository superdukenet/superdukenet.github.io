Coolberg

2007-07-06 14:10:00 UTC

Hi to all! Due to this forum and the nice things about SD you've been writing I made up my mind to change from Z1000 Kawasaki to Superduke

I found it on EBAY in Italy COMO, went there last week by plane and bought the bike went to MONZA and put it on trailer. On the way there I poped into EZTUNING.IT shop and found the beautiful SD tuned versions also got a LAVIZZARI double exhausts for a sweet price.



So now you can congratulate me for being the first owner of SuperDuke in the country .





I really love the bike and handling - just miss the sound of 4cylinder and have to get used to the V-twin

This was my bike before Z1000 which was a good learning ground for wheelies - if you can pull up the 258kg bike with such a long chassy then every other bike seems a piece of cake for wheelies.



I also have now the quad bike which is fun for the period when weather conditions are not so good to ride 2 wheeled bike




This is a great forum for sharing ideas and getting info - a grear place and nice people.

I am from Latvia - Baltic States

Cheers,

Coolberg
