Just got home with the bike. Rode it 400 miles so far and love it!! Stuck to mostly back roads for the breaking in so took about 10 hours . I was surprised how comfortable the riding position is and the handling is amazing. Not so happy with the bumps though, so I think I need to start by getting the suspension dialed in. Fueling was surprisingly good (knock on wood) after all the horror stories I have heard but I did notice a an awful lot of vibration through the foot pegs from 5000rpm up. Much more than on my 950 Adventure.

Anyway, I really love it and can't wait to get everything dialed in.
