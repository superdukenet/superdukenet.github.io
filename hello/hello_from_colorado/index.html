Hi Everyone,

Just picked up a lightly used 2007 Super Duke last week and I'm thrilled with her. When I got her she only had 960 miles and still had inch wide chicken strips on the OEM tyres!!! Lightly used to say the least. The previous owner traded it in on a H@rley and had already installed Akro Evo full system and PCIII with a dyno-tuned map so all I have left to do is SAS elimination and maybe some airbox mods.

Whoever tuned the bike did a damn good job because this SD fuels as well or better than any bike I've ever had (and I've had my share). No snatchy throttle, hunting, or scary low speed action at all. She'll pull 3rd gear around town from idle to redline smoother than any big twin has a right to! So I got that going for me, which is nice.

Anyways, here's some pics. Glad to be a part of the SD community!
