Hi guys, been a lurker for almost a year now and i'm a bit shy to introduce myself. I'm larry, a retired mechanical engineer who has been riding bikes for almost 35years now. Started on a Harley, owned a sportsbike, a novice motocross rider and loved to trail our mountains and rivers till my body started complaining...haha.
I have been riding an '06 SD for the past couple of years and most of my mods have been guided through the unselfish contributions of members here. i have, oval secondary flies, bmc filters, akra slipons, akra map loaded, sas removed, O2 sensors eliminated and modified my breather hoses which i hope i can post pictures of. will contribute my share of mods and post pictures of our beautifull country in my travels north, east, west and south. An open invite for those who plan to visit in the future to serve as a guide.

Cheers and More power to superduke.net!
