Hi guys,

Thought I'd say hello. Been reading the forum for a few days now. It all kind of started with a wheelie, you see... It was a bit longer than I was used to, and I was a bit tired, and then I bottled it, and I braked. Which was bad. This handful of brakes ended up launching my K1200R into the tyre wall, myself skidding on the tarmac. I'm pretty pleased to say I'm fairly OK (nothing broken!) but the bike is quite likely a write off.

So I've been trying to figure out what sort of bike I want next, and I seem to be coming back to the SuperDuke. I rode one breifly several years ago, so I do know a bit how they feel.

Some of you (Ducati Porn, Couch Potato etc) might know me from Visordown (SuperMoto forum specifically), and Ratemut knows me (I think!) from k-bikes.com.

It now seems like I'm trying to decide whether go for a pre-07 bike (cheap), or 07-or-later bike (better tank size etc), or the SuperDuke R (more expensive, but oh-sooo-gorgeous!!). Opinions gratefully recieved!

Went to Premier Bikes on Saturday to have a nose around, but I must have been wearing my invisibility cloak because even after 10-15 minutes loitering I hadn't had as much as a "Hello", so I left. They don't have to take my money...
... Anyway, so as soon as my finances fall into place with regards to the K1200R, I'll be hunting, most likely, for a Super Duke R (or non-R, who knows!). If you're thinking of selling yours, let me know.

Hope to see you out there!
