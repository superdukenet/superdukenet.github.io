KTM owners day at Mallory Park for £99 on the 2nd July

EDIT, please note this is now the 2nd July, same track.

This is because KTM could not make the first date and really wanted to be there.

Cheers.



105Db limit so full race systems welcome

After discussions with Focused Events on location, dates and viability the date has been give out as above.

Partly the decision was based on track length/size/style, partly on location and partly because we ideally wanted it in summer.

The track is a LOT more suited to SM's than many of the longer and faster tracks, it has an excellent Cafe and the chicane section for Edwinas and the Esses are great for SM's... as is the Hairpin and Bus-stop though really.

Personally i think the weapon to be on will be either a Superduke, or even a 690SM

Proposal for now is 3 groups. Novice/Inters/Experienced and KTM owners/riders only to be allowed.

I will of course be contacting all dealers and getting this out there and filled as quickly as possible.

The event is now on Focused Events website and ready to book via

http://www.focusedevents.com/events.asp?event_type=UK

Focused can be contacted on 08702 646 268 if you wish to book over the phone.

I will produce a colour Flyer, if you want a copy then please feel free and i'll print them out so you can give to your local dealers etc.

List of Names to be added as and when you say 'Yeah'

I will this year now i've got more time for planning arrange a 'pig' for the evening.. (No lads.. get your minds out of the gutter) i'm thinking of a Spit roasted pig !!!!! Stop it !!!!

Hopefully this all fits in with you guys well and we can make this a brilliant KTM only event.

I truely adore Mallory park, being my favourite UK track in many ways, i was quicker relatively than any other track, and also the place where i had 2nd and 3rd places and picked up my one and only trophy from club racing back in 2006.

Any questions/discussions/etc feel free to throw them on this thread.
