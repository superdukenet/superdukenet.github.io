Keenaz

2012-10-29 02:31:00 UTC

So with the new Super Duke release just around the corner I've anticipated trading my 2008 SD in for the new model when it becomes available. However, considering the new SD will cost at least $15K USD and my SD would probably only fetch about $5K for a trade or $6K if I sold it myself it got me wondering if it would even make sense to trade my current SD for the next generation model? For example, for less than the trade in difference between my bike and the new SD I could buy a zero miles New Old Stock 2011 CBR1000rr and keep my current SD. I guess until we truly know what the new Super Duke is going to be it's not worth worrying about such things but it is interesting that you could potentially have two awesome and completely different bikes for the price of one of the latest greatest.
