Hey guys, what's up?

Been quite a long time since I last visited here, ever since I sold my SD 990. A sad day indeed, thought I could hold on to it alongside the Tuono but after a few months of sitting in the garage it went to its new owners that took 4 years of pristine ownership, maintenance, moding and fixing OEM problems (and hooliganism) and turned them into shite... Anyway, not the point.

So the reason I came back for a visit is I started Motolixious, which is a new YouTube channel, kind of a blog meets vlog channel that will have bike and gear reviews, news, features and just general vlog stuff (just not the type of a dude cruising around for 30 minutes and just talks everything that comes to mind, that's not really me).

I was working for the past 7 years for Fullgaz Magazine, Israel's largest moto magazine, and the past two as senior road tester. Got enough of that, decided to go the millennial way.

The channel is new so still not a lot of content there but I have high hopes and plans, and trying to get some initial traction so thought I'll post here and hopefully some of you might find it not a complete waste of time and subscribe www.instagram.com/motolixious
