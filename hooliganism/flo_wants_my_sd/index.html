Flo wants my SD!

nezorange

2012-02-09 00:38:00 UTC

I'm new to the site but have owned my 07 Superduke since Nov. Well this Sun I laid the SD down because this chic comes to a complete stop prior to entering the merging lane for the highway. I was looking to my left to see if there was any traffic in the other lane and when I look forward there was a car not moving about 20 feet in front of me. I tried to stop but grabed too much front brake and laid the duke down at about 25 mph. The duke slid into the back of her car and I slid along the side of her car. The SD didn't seem to be hurt too bad and I rode it about 2 hours to get home. I have full coverage insurance with Progressive and they looked at it today, I haven't heard the total yet but the parts alone are over $3000. The two big items were the gas tank and radiator, neither are leaking but the rad is dented/scraped and the gas tank as a hair line crack on the left side and a short deep scrape on the right. I was bummed out cause it took along time to find the superduke that didn't have alot of miles. If they do try and make it a total loss I will try and fight it or buy it back.

I think Flo just wants a real bike in those Progressive Commercials, other then the Ducks.


The damage sure doesn't look like it's that bad.
