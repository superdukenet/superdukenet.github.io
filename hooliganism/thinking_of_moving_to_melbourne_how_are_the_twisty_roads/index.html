The bike scene is awesome in Melbourne with heaps of rides, pubs and circuits. I used to do a loop out from dandenong over the mountain and up the black spur a couple of time a week and race at the 3 circuits there. The clubby scene is awesome and great support for classics. It IS bloody cold in the winter tho but there are two tracks that stay pretty dry for most of the year as the are on the other side of the mountains. If I could I would still live there for the bike riding but work has pulled me away (plus I'm not sure if I could do the winters anymore!)
I second the Perth comment still a nice place to live tho enduros are more the go over here.
