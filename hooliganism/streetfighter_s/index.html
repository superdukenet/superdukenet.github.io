You are a little ahead of me Lobster, I went down to Laguna, THE WORST DEALER IN THE WESTERN WORLD, on Sunday just to get a test ride on a Streetfighter. They didnt have one, useless as always.

I have heard the Streetfighter has a funny riding position so make sure you are happy with that before wedging out. But otherwise, why not? The Streetfighter makes a genuine 130bhp, I dont think any SD/SDR can claim that, unless you measure it on an american dyno. The Streetfighter has tonnes of Ducati knowhow if you think of it as a 1098/1198 that you can use on the road it is a no brainer as a bike purchase. I still think its syling is a bit shit in places. It looks a bit like it has already had a front end smash and those shotgun exhausts that are right where you put your foot bug me. That said I never really liked the SD look either, only the SDR.

Also, you have to figure that KTM havent delveoped the bike since they designed it, alright a token top end rework on the 08 SDR that doesnt account for much, If you keep your SD you will likely start to find fault with it. Maybe little niggles, maybe big things, perhaps it is better to jump ship now. You can always come back if KTM release an naked RC8R. In my youth I always traded bikes cars and women every two years. I thought it better to leave with good memories and see where the next one took me. It has cost me a few houses and I owned 6 washing machines before I ever wore one out but that is life. As soon as I had kids I was stuck, for better or worse (those vicars are lying cunts it is just for worse) if I could trade her for a sexy latin model I would love too...but probably wouldnt if it meant losing my kids
