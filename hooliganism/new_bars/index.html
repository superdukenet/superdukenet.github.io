trackday junkie

2010-08-04 23:22:00 UTC


These are stainless steel, heavier than stock . Nice welds on mine. The '08 SDR has 8mmX 30mm bolts threaded into the steering stops. I cut down a couple of 40 mm to 35 long to keep from pinching paws against the gas tank. You loose a bit of steering lock. I Don't care, It's a track bike. YMMV
They place your hands a bit forward, aprox. 2 3/4 " lower. They are welded up flat, so in order to get 'em rolled down you give up a bit of back.
IMO-you want some down.
Life is good. I ordered these pups on Monday and they where waiting when I got off work today.
