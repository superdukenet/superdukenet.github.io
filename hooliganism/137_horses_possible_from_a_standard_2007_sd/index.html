Funny that. I just done some research on the injectors in the standard SD. They only flow enough for about 65 hp max per cylinder.

Either fit SDR - RC8R injectors, Ducati 1098 injectors (expensive) or adapt a 12 hole bosch or denso injector with 550cc flow rate.
