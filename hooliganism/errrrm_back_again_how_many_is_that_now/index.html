Errrrm, back again, how many is that now?

weeksy

2016-08-19 15:04:00 UTC
I've had 4 superdukes in the past as some of you may know, along with 8 other ktms. Most recently a 690 duke 4.

Anyway, I've been having deep and meaningfulls about a Superduke in the last few months, but the right deal with Couchie came up and I pounced on his newly purchased superduke after he'd tested it for me at donington this week.

Its a 2007 early shape model in anthracite grey and only 7500 miles on it.

Me and the lads are off to Germany and Spa mid next year so I wanted something with a bit more. I also found after riding Crusts 990 recently the grunt is quite addictive and whilst I couldn't throw it around the lanes with the same careless abandon, I still really enjoyed it.

I get back to the UK on Sat evening and then have 2 hours swapping bits off it on Sunday before meeting up with Couchie on Monday to collect the new bike.
