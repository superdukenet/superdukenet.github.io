Trip to the dyno

Lowrance

2011-04-06 23:51:00 UTC
well some of you might remmeber i took my SD to the dyno a few weeks and it was lean as a mofo, using one of the maps.

So anyway, the guy who tunes my car was over from the states and we were tuning some cars. When we were waiting for some other cars to arrive we threw the SD on the dyno dynamics rollers. strapped it down and got to work

My mate initialy adjusted the fueling before i rode to the dyno from the lean map above, so anyway did the first pull it was 106 whp and still very lean and fueling off

so we reverted to the stock ktm akro map and had a little play, the fueling is bang on now, some more nice mid range and top end, feels great from 5k... only added 1 deg of timing atm.

we wanted to play with it some more and part throttle/2nd throttle, and the ignition map, but due to time constraints and car tuning up we took it off the rollers.

over all at the moment the map is good, safe and runs great but more work is needed to make it 100% perfect, but he is back in a couple of months and we are going to spend an hour on it and book no cars in, and hopfully it will be bang on then.


It made on a car dyno dynamics 116 whp in the end and 70 lbtq. iirc

i might drop by the orginal dyno i had my bike done on and see what its putting down now, but to be honest the bike feels 100% better.

updates to follow
