Typical bloody tourist parked in the middle of the road (P/T),neat photo's, the SI is such a special place, motorcycle nivarna. I live in the North Island & have covered over 8000k's down there this year over 2 trips. One thing about the SI is that due to the lack of traffic people that are not used to driving on our side of the road (keep LEFT) sometimes forget which side of the road they should be on. Lost a mate to a head on 3yrs ago on the West Coast of the South Island, as they (tourist) left a one-way bridge on the wrong side of the road, dulp straight into the path of my mate approaching the bridge (your not really expecting the on coming vehicle to be closing your options down on your side of the road).Bad learning curve for the tourist. Remember take it easy out there !
