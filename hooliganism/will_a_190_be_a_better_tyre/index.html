Menlo-SD

2010-04-18 03:59:00 UTC
I just put a 190 Michelin on while doing a 520 gear and chain swap. The 190 has a steeper profile then the 180 and is close to the chain but there's no evidence of the chain rubbing the tyre. I use my SDR mainly for the track where a 190 offers some advantages over the 180. If I were to use this bike daily on the street I would stay with the 180.
