I picked up my lift from Greg yesterday. I had the opportunity to have Greg demonstrate the lift on the SD he had at the shop. He made it a point to emphasize the importance of fully engaging the pin in the bushing. The bushing that replaces swing arm pivot collar nut does not have the integrated hardened washer that is on the stock unit. That is the only concern I have with torqueing the bushing down to 96 ft/lbs is that it is not fully distributing the load across the contact surface. I want to install a washer but am not sure if the bushing will be too far out from its original intended location. Maybe Greg can chime in with some information.
Anyway, it was great to meet Greg and his grass roots operation. He had a couple of nice Ducs sitting there. I have to admit, I was drawn to his Hypermotard complete with his accessories and carbon bits. I like the big Supermotos which prompted me to visit our local Duc, KTM, Husky and Guzzi dealer that was down the road from MotoMfg.com to see what he had in stock.
