How light is the Akra Ti System? Answer here.

bic_bicknell

2010-09-02 19:25:00 UTC
So I have both sets of exhausts here so have done a comparison for us geeky weight saving types.

This comparison is between a standard '07 Superduke steel system with Akra slip on round silencers and a full titanium system with Akra Evo silencers. (My slip-on silencers have been re-skinned in carbon and shortened by 40mm so they will be slightly lighter than the standard slip-on.)

DOWN PIPES
Standard steel - 3.350 Kilos
Ti - 1.490 Kilos

'Y' PIECE
Standard steel - 1.210 Kilos
Ti - 591 g
Those bores have increased from 50mm diameter to 55mm

Silencers
Standard Slip-on (carbon, shortened) - 1.274 Kilos
Ti Evo non round - 1.546 Kilos

Total weight of Standard Superduke Steel system with slip-on Akras - 6.106 Kilos

Total weight of Full Ti system with Akra Evos - 3.355 Kilos

Looks like there is about a 50% weight saving for fitting the Ti system even though the Evo silencers are heavier than the round slip-on ones. This should give me about a one second a mile advantage while trying to keep up with Klinky and Shade on those mad Sunday runs.
