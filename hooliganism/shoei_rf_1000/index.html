had AGV Arai and Shoei over the years. The Agv just isn`t as well made/finished and is heavier comparing equivalent models as the other two.
Not much to choose quality-wise betweem Arai and Shoei IMO but I think the Arai has the edge.
Subjectively the Arai seems noisier but with earplugs there`s no real difference. I read some noise tests on helmets(?Ride magazine) which concluded that the Arai Corsair was actually quieter than it`s Shoei competitor(X-spirit) when measured with a dB meter,though the testers felt it was noisier.
The main difference for me was the fact the Arai is much more comfortable with spectacles. The Shoei wouldn`t allow me to wear some types of glasses(Oakleys especially) whereas any type of spectacles can be worn under the Arai.
Be interesting to see what these new EU regs turn up when the various helmet types are tested but I`ll stick to Arai. Keep thinking of Nakano`s 200mph get off at Mugello........he was wearing an Arai and suffered no head injury at all.
