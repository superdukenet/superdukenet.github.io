Colonel_Klinck

2010-03-27 17:40:00 UTC
This is a hobby. I started in composites making my own snowboards. I modeled it up in a 3D CAD program and then had patterns made using a rapid prototype method called stereolithography. I used the patterns to make a fiberglass mold and then wet lay up the carbon fiber in the mold and vacuum bag it. Aside from the rapid prototyping it's a pretty standard technique. The air filter is a K&N E-3672.



I'm thinking maybe around $250 including the filter??? I'm expecting a new bottom plate pattern this week and then I have to make a mold and then make a part and then check fit on my bike (the bottom plate in the pics interferes with the secondary butterfly linkage by a hair). I don't think I would have any ready to sell for a month at the earliest.
