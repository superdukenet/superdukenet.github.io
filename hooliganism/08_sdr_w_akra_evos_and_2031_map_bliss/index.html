The bike runs really well now. I may have felt a very slight dip at 4K while riding it home from the dealer yesterday but that's it. The fueling is good with plenty of horsepower and a solid linear torque curve. The off idle, snatchiness, lunging, lurching whatever you want to call it is far better than it was. Imo people need to realise that part of the problem has to do with the SD/SDR being a twin but I think it's a good as any twin I've owned. I think my bike, engine wise is dialed. Since have to get sag set and a few screws turned with the suspension and I'll be good to go...

I hate to be a party pooper, but give it 200 - 300 miles and see if it still runs as cleanly. In my experience they end up as snatchy as before - then you reset the ECU and they are fine again.....for a while!

So far the best 'cure' for snatchiness I have found is a combination of 3 things;
1. remove the O2 sensors and replace with Eliminators (get 'em from Dynojet for about Â£30)
2. reset the ECU
3. fit a ramped throttle cam

Anyway, I hope it stays good
