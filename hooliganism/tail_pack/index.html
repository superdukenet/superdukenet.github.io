Kriega John. I have the US 5, 10, 20 and 30. A size for every occasion as it were.

The US 5 is not very useful. Its really suited for dirt bike use.

I generally ride with the US 10. It has enough room for tools, a small pump, tyre repair kit, a litre of Gatorade or my jacket liner, and various other bits. The US 20 will swallow quite a bit more, and you can combine the 10 and 20 to make a 30. I also have the new US 30 stand alone. I haven't used it yet, but it looks like it will hold quite a bit.

I could probably be bribed to loan you my US 20 or 30 for your trip.
