I would also check the multiway connectors behind the headlight mask, which are easy to get to by undoing the two allen headed bolts in front of the handlebars. Make sure both sexes of each connection are properly mated, and ideally disconnect, clean and pack with dielectric grease. Also check the state of the cables and make sure non of them are kinked, snagged or have exposed conductors.

Secondly check the earth bonding point on the right hand side of the engine, and make sure it's not loose.
