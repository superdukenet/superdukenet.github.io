Rottweiler Intake Giveaway Contest - FOR INMATES OF ADVENTURE RIDER.COM ONLY!!


To celebrate over 1000 Rottweiler Intake Systems for all big twin KTM's currently in service, (Continuing to make the world a better place for Adventurers and big twin owners alike) Rottweiler Intake Systems is celebrating by giving away one complete intake system (990 or 950) with SAS plates and your choice of Pre Filter to one lucky ADV Inmate! Second place will receive one set of two pre filters (Red 40 cell and Grey 60 Cell) and third place will recieve one set of Rottweiler SAS plates.

Contest rules are:

You must post pictures of your ride and explain to us in a brief (or not so brief) paragraph why your ride deserves a free Rottweiler Intake System kit. (Starting an account with Photo Bucket seems to be the easiest way to post pics unless there is a nice inmate here somewhere that wouldn't mind explaining a better / easier way)
You must vote for at least one others ride in a comment box separate from the one you posted your ride in. You may not vote on your own. (Post your rides early so inmates can see your ride before they vote!)
You may encourage other inmates to vote for you with whatever means necessary.
Ties will go to the earliest inmates that posted their ride.
You may post multiple rides but it must be in separate comment boxes than your other ride.
You may vote even if you have not entered a bike. (This is encouraged)
It is okay to post your ride if you already have a Rottweiler Intake System. You can win another one and buy a second Adventure.
The contest will run for 3 weeks from the initial posting and Rottweiler Performance will keep track of the voting counts and announce the winners. Shipments will go out immediately and will be paid by Rottweiler Performance within the lower 48.
There must be a minimum of 10 rides entered so start encouraging your inmate pals by whatever means necessary.
International entries are also encouraged but we will ask that you pay just the difference for international shipping.
If you do not follow the rules we will allow you to make the changes necessary by explaining who hasn't after the 2nd week. If you do not make the necessary changes, we will be forced to disqualify you.
We would like the winners to post up pics of their prizes once they are installed.
Good luck!
