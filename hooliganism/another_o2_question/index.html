Just a recap of what KTMguy said

In simple terms turning them off in TuneECU just stops it taking readings from the sensors so you no longer need to unplug them.

it is unplugging them that gives you an error, not switching them off.

The supplied KTM Akra maps from the tuneecu guys indicates that the o2 sensors are switched off in 2 of the akra maps but not the 3rd, one map which switches off is for the full system but I'm not sure what the difference is between the two slip on akra maps available as to why one has them off and the other doesn't so as suggested, the only way to know for sure is to plug in and check.

It's as cheap to make up a tuneecu cable as to buy a set of o2 eliminators.
