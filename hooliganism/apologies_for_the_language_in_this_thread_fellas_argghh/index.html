Sorry to hear about your problems.

I also want to point out that when the red "charging" light comes on it's all over.
- at which point it's already too late. It's common in vehicles that the light comes on if the alternator doesn't charge. That's NOT the case for the SuperDukes.
Therefore it's not a charging light, it's a low voltage light. Totally useless. The only thing it's useful for is to check that you don't overload the bike with too many gadgets.

Hope you get your bike fixed ASAP and that you'll find whatever bike you desire and that it'll stay reliable!
