Cheers for the comments guys,

The camera was on a BMW GS, man that guy can ride, but did you notice me speed up when the sportsbike went past ?

As for the sportsbike overtake, he was a local guy who had Little Doctor on his leathers, guess he spends his whole life on those bends... (lucky fecker) when he overtook he pulled a wheelie past me!

It was like a mini Nureburg ring, during the short time I was there a bus and several articulated lorries went through, well over some of the white lines and behind the armco was mostly nothing but fresh air

I did the whole stretch in 2nd as it's quite a steep uphill run and the SD engine braking is soooo good I only needed a light touch on the rear brake now and then, on the way down I noticed EVERY bend had a single tyre skid mark right into the armco'...ouch

Apparently the record is held by a 125 who just nailed it the whole way up???

Was a fun ride and I "know" if I spent 2 days there practicing I'd knock a minute off the time and have an even bigger, stupid, grin
