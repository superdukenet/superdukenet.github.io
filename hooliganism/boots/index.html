Ducati Pete

2011-03-04 20:29:00 UTC
Been riding for 30 years. Tried everything. Spent a lot of the last decade in Alpine Stars of different sorts - pure race through to waterproof versions. good....but.......

Three years ago got a pair of Daytona Evo Sport, (gortex version), from Ebay. I think I paid about £130. They were already a few years old but they are without doubt the best boots I ever owned. Comfortable, light, reassuring, well made. I've crashed in them six times on track and they have done their job superbly. I have had the outers repaired professionally and they still are good for years. I think I will be using these boots for many, many years to come. Still have a pair of waterproof Alpine stars for commuting but the Daytonas are the bollox. I really, really rate them. They make all other boots seem cheap and plastic. The only thing I would trade them for is the Security Evo versions with kevlar inners. I scan ebay all the time for them but they come up rarely in my size which is common.
