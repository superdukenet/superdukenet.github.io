Like it mate!

Funnily enough I have been considering the idea of using circuit board pattern to make a design for my SD panels. But as all my panels are carbon I was going to make the design up in lazer cut vinyl and then use this as a mask to get the surface of the carbon vapour blasted. Then peel off the vinyl to end up with a matt/gloss effect on the carbon. I did a few samples and the result is pretty subtle but stealth-like. The curcuit board effect seems to go with the KTM functional, raw aesthetics I thought.

Do you work for an auto graphics company? (The van in the background??)

Keep us posted - you will have a lot of takers if you can produce high quality graphic panels. There are a few people who have developed the line work/illustrator files to fit SD panels.
