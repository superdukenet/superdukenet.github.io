You will open up a can of worms here!

Keep in mind . the earlier SD's with steeper steering angles have their nuances . Head shake / less stability ! My 07 would sometimes shake like a sob on me till i learned how to be lighter on the bars. Point is when pushing my 07 pretty damn hard into the turns ( at speed ) i could tell the front wanted to wash out ( Too short of trail ). This is with the factory setup ( 1/8th" fork tube sticking above the triple clamp )... i have sense lowered the tubes about 1/4" into the triple clamp making a world of difference and stability improved / less head shake / and that washout feeling much much reduced ( basically gone ).... Point is if you get pre 07 you may need/want to go with that Nichols triple clamp so you can give yourself some rake back and some stability . 2 cents
