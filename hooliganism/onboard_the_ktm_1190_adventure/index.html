KTM666

2013-05-10 21:33:00 UTC
Redline have lent me their demonstrator while my RC8 is in the shop.
Bad bits:
1. the bars feel as though they are kinked up at the ends - makes the grip feel a bit weird after a while.
2. the saddle is weird: not uncomfortable as such just a bit odd to settle into
however all that pales into insignificance:
This is a fantastic bike! The motor initially feels smooth and fairly strong but it really rewards full throttle and high revs - then it really goes.
2nd gear wheelies off the throttle ALL the time. Mirror, signal, pull out and hoist one
Handling in Sport mode is excellent.
All in all a top bike.
and WAY quicker than an SDR.
