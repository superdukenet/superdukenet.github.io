Fluids. You need to be hydrated. Once you're actually thirsty, it's over. Water, Powderade, Gatorade, anything that actually hydrates you w/o caffeine. Caffeine is a diuretic, which basically dehydrates you. Food & water, lots of water. I stop every 100miles or so to stretch, pee, and drink. Every other 100miles I stretch/pee/fuel/drink. 500 mile days are good, 600 gets you farther, but you better be mentally & physically fit. Easily done here in the west, but when you can see the curvature of the earth in front of you, you want to go!
