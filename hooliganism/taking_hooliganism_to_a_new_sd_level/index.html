You're crazy!

More serious, go for a Mitsubishi TD025 or TD25 (don't know exactly) because these will spin up easily and will have a great spread of power for a motorcycle. Lots of replacement and revision parts available as well as documentation.

You might want forged pistons to handle the extra pressure, lower the compression a bit (spacer under cylinder) and use a high quality pressure release valve (0,5bar extra pressure should be enough)
Placement for the turbo will be a challenge, you might consider placing it at the spot the stock exhaust (now Y piece) sits and using a single muffler since a turbo absorbs a lot of sound energy.

Also, use a tuner with turbo experience on motorcycles or it might get very expensive...
