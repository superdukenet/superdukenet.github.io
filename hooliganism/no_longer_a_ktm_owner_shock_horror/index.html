No longer a KTM owner. SHOCK HORROR. !

weeksy

2009-05-03 19:10:00 UTC
Due to finances and the wife wanting to upgrade teh house and some windows etc, i decided to do the right thing and downgrade my bike freeing up some capital. I don't ride that often now because of time constraints having a 10 month old son, so it made sense.

After lots of discussions i went for



Yamaha XT660x. Pretend Supermoto.

Has a tank range of 170miles, weighs less than a modern 600 supersports and is a big noisy single cylinder. Built by the Japanese too, so servicing costs etc should be low.

The bike is truely great. Proprt proper fun tool. Comfy up to 90mph and tops out about 105mph. It's far from being the quickest thing in the world, but is a blast to ride and handles like a dream.

It has many things in common with KTM's namely hunting/fuelling issues it's crap at 30mph LOL. However doesn't bother me, as it's not common for me to be in town etc.

Picked it up for Â£2500 on an 06 plate with 1300 miles on the clocks... BARGAIN !

Just thought i'd update you lads

I had a ball on my XT660X, even took it round Cadwell once. If its an 06 there are a few small mods that will make it much better though:
- first get a Hagon fork spring kit
- then fit a DNA stage 2 air filter kit (and remove the snorkel)
-and lastly, there is a chap in Oz that sells a kit to tune the air temp sensor readings (so that you can richen it up a bit) - not very expensive and you can get more details on the XT660 forum
