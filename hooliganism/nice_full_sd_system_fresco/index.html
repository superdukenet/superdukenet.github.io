2013-02-01 22:09:00 UTC

The SDR headers at both longer and wider than the EVO's.
this I'm told is for the extra revs the SDR has and so is able to move and flatten the torque curve up the rev scale.
My SDR has less mid range than the 06 but more HP and a very linear power delivery.
SDR headers may just move the power out of a useable range for the standard model.
This effect is seen greatly on Harley V twins when people get the ported and polished, allowing more flow. This moves the power out of the usable rev range of the motor and they get little if any benefit.
I'd be interested to find out if you do decide on them.
