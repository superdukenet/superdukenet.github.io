For those that don't know, Chuckwalla is in the middle of the desert and we were in the midst of a sand storm for most of the day. We had sand blowing across the track right at the apex of the majority of the corners. Didn't pose too much of a traction issue but definitely caused some cheek clenching moments in a couple of the faster corners.

All in all, great day. If you live close you really should get out and check that track out. It is an absolute blast and the SD works really well there.
