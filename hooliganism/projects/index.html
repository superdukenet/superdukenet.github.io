I have a few projects but this one is closest to being done --- it went from this


to this -


it's a 1973 175 yamaha that i got just to ride in the dirt to get some dirt skills back.
Now it's got a different barrel ( DT instead of CT ) for more torque - Blaster piston clearanced ( Huge ports in piston skirt ) RD400 reed cage - Boyensen reeds - Webco highh compression head - Gutted exhaust pipe turned into a expansion chamber - flat slide carb a couple sizes bigger - transfer ports relieved - exhaust port polished ..... Probably less than 10 hours before it's first kick ...

Painted it KTM orange thinking i'd use the color scheme of a SDR but got carried away with paint...... Handlebars are Metal Mulisha aluminium with Renthal bar mounts . Alloy rims laced at home . Recovered seat - custom tail light -

the other projects are the big and bigger brother to this one ---- DT-250 yamaha and a RT-1 360 yamaha - the DT250 strictly for the dirt and the RT-1 i'm going to shoot for 50 hp out of the 2 stroke engine and put it in a chrome molly frame all stripped down hoping the end product will weigh in about 200 lbs .. Should be fun

I have a suggestion for you. Dirt bikes made huge improvements with suspension and brakes in the 1980's.

Shop right and you can pick one up for 1K.
