Don't know what state you are in but some states no longer require them to be displayed In those states you just need the receipt in your toolbox Etc.

If you are not in one of those states I used to just use clear laminate (book covering material) to make a sticky patch that I'd put the label on then stick the whole lot onto my swingarm. Very neat and they stay on for years hence the row of three I have on one of my bikes. If they hadn't changed the law here in Sunny South Oz i'd have just moved onto the other side of the swingarm after a while. The're easy to remove - just peel off.
I've had quite a few cops say "Umm never seen anything like that before - do they come off?" Seems a strange question when there are three of four lined up
