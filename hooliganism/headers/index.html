The Akrapovic "full" system has headers that are a larger diameter than the standard headers. These are not to be confused with the exhaust system on some later SDRs which ave the Akra logo etched into the surface of the down pipes - these are still the standard diameter pipes.

There is a slight increase in performance, both power and torque with the full system but I found that the biggest change in engine characteristics was that the engine seems to want to keep on revving right up to the red line. It just keeps on making power.

I don't think you should get fixated about needing the bigger down pipes but they are a nice to have thing if some come up second hand. (Which is very rare but one is for sale now through this forum). Full system in titanium is of course a lot lighter and looks great when the colour effects take hold.

Your coating will be a ceramic coating. Thee was a bit of a craze in doing this a few years ago. Most people, I remember, found that the finish didn't last that well.
