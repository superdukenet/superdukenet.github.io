2008 SDR vs Ducati Hypermotard SP

Sarasota_Steve

2015-07-15 02:07:00 UTC

I currently ride 2008 SDR but have been looking at the updated 2013+ Ducati Hypermotard SP. Used those could be find in $10K range and I have been really thinking to say goodbye to the SDR and get HM SP.
Hypermotard SP comes with ABS, TC and few different riding and ABS modes. Also, starting 2013 those are liquid cooled with service intervals of 18K miles, which is much better than before. I love the SDR but the new HM SP is so amazing reading the spec. A little over 110HP and 66TQ. Which puts it very close to the SDR numbers.

What do you think?
