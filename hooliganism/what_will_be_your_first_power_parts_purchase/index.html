Serious answer:

I'll ride on track and see what I think it could do with.
I suspect it may be a bit of reworking in the forks, but I certainly won't look to the powerparts for that: Steve J will do them at a very good price.

As far as the rear sprocket is concerned, 2 teeth more will give you some extra thrust, wether it's needed on a bike with 160BHP is open for debate.

One nice thing with the new bike is that it's geared for 180MPH+, so if you do decide to lower the gearing, you'll still be able to hit long straights like Almeria and Parc Algar without hitting the limiter in 6th way before the braking zone.

You've got TC etc on the 1290, it's not likely to be too much.
