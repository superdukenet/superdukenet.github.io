New Race Bike. Moving on.

Viking

2013-10-12 21:59:00 UTC

Got my new bike all ready to go.

Felt it's be right to post up and thank all you guys for your help and support from the time with the KTM. Loads of you really helped out when needed. Really appreciate it.

The bike has been sold to a guy who is racing it in the Welsh Hillclimb Championship. It should go really well there tbh. Really happy it's still gonna be racing and aking its big ole tractor stomp on a championship.

I've still got all the info we gained from 18 month of trying everything we could to get the bike working, so all this is avaliable to anyone who need some help, info or guidance. I've been setting up a few local SD's with good results, so if your in the area pop by and i can have a fiddle.

We're still 3rd in the Streetfighter championship, ut have enough of a gap to 4th that i dont need to enter the final round, so i'll be racing the new bike in the Elite 600 class at Donington.

Cheers guys. Best of luck with the new 1290. Hope it turns out as good as it sounds!
