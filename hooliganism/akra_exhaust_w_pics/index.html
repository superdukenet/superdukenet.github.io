I've been so inspired by the picture of the SD with R1 Akra cans that I've just bought stock 2006 R1 cans and Y-piece cheap off ebay to experiment with . I will try and graft them onto my SD this winter. Although the cans are stock R1 and don't look as nice as the R1 Akras, they give next to nothing away on power and are only slighter heavier at 4kg for the pair, ie about 7kg lighter than the stock SD silencer!. Just back from the dyno to test the latest mods (modified heads, TBs & airbox) and figured a better exhaust might further improve power. Obviously a full Akra system would be the way to go but I don't like the price or looks of it!. The stock R1 cans are good enough to flow around 160rwhp worth of exhaust gases so should be good enough for my 125rwhp SD!. Watch this space for progress and dyno results
