Dainese size 52 used to fit me like a glove when I had a 32 inch waist. Trouble is that was 10 years ago and I now need a 54.

You will find that you are either an Italian shape in which case Dainese, Spidi, Spike etc will fit nice

Or a chinese / taiwanese / korean idea of US shape in which case Arlen Ness, Berik, Frank Thomas aka BKS will fit nice.

Furegan were the weirdest fit I ever tried, great jacket fit spoilt by funny short little leg trousers obviously to fit some little bandy legged frenchman or Klincky

Wolf seem pretty good are also a good price and pretty much bespoke. Not only that, their agents are keen to do a good deal on price why not get another set from them?

Quick observation on leathers. I have had loads of two piece sets on the basis that they would be more flexible than a one piece. Looking back I don't think I can ever remember wearing the trousers with a diferent jacket or the jacket on its own.

My point is simple, on track one piece are more comfortable and in the real world no less flexible and on the road a real jacket (the top of two piece leathers tend to be too short at the back to be much use as a stand alone jacket) and seperate leather trousers work best for me.

I'm 6 foot, about 13.5 stone and Alps for the road and either Clover or Dianese for the track have had the best fits for me.

Try before you buy is the best advice.
