2012-03-11 23:16:00 UTC
I use tyre warmers.
It enables you to have a base setting for your pressures - once the tyres are warmed up with their electric blanket you are never more than a couple of psi away from the ideal track pressure.
I can't really comment on the dual use of road and track but a number of magasines have said good things about the latest road/trackday tyres from both Dunlop and Pirelli but they made the point that you need to be aware that they are a compromise and won't be as good in the cold and wet as a more road orientated tyre because it won't warm up/retain its heat and typically won't have much side grip in the wet.
