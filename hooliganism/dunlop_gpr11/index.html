BASH69

2011-01-14 09:47:00 UTC

I ran them last year for a handful of trackdays.

Very grippy but i given up with them now, it didnt matter what i did i just couldnt stop them tearing.

I monitored pressures real close after every session on track and managed to go from cold tear to hot tear all in 1 day. So after that i fitted a brand new shock and the next trackday exactly the same thing. I even had geometry tear on the front tyre which is something ive never seen before with the SD

Im sure theyd make an excellent summer scratchin tyre but ill go back to Racetecs in the future (or maybe Bridgestone 003) as ive never had any tearing problems with them!

KTM666

2011-01-14 15:36:00 UTC

I have used on on several track days fantastic Grip and wear really well.......I have had tearing (same area)

You need to much use lower PSI than any other tyre on the track (Road 34psi Front and rear)

Track 19 psi (cold) Rear and 30 psi (cold) Front

Track 23psi (Hot) Rear and 33psi (Hot) Front

Sounds crazy low but works a treat (Ntec belt prevents tyre growing at speed and so they can run lower psi)
