The kit I got (through KTM)



had the 100/200/400 cams, and the 200 made as much difference as I needed. The 100 is the 1:1 cam, as in the same as stock. The 400 is too gradual at first, and then makes up the difference at the end by being too abrupt.
200 or 300 is perfect, 300 for heavyhanded riders or slippery/bumpy conditions.

No reset, just grease & install properly, and let the engine warm up to 3 bars before touching the throttle.
