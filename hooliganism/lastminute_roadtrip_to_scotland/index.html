2011-08-01 20:50:00 UTC

Vikings routes are top notch! might I suggest that once you get to Fort William you try the A830 to Mallaig. AFAIR. Smooth tarmaced roads with whiteline borders.
You'll like it so much you'll want to go back.(Erm.... you have to.)

Btw there are also a few East coast borders routes that are so tempting.Turn of at Moffat and go the A708 and you will eventually come to St Marys Loch. Probably one of the most popular Scottish Sunday rides/meets.

Selkirk/Galasheils and A7 to Edinburgh takes you to the Capital. The best Scottish road riders and Rally drivers Earned their craft in the Borders Jim Clark/Jimmy and Colin Macrae/Steve Hislop/Louse Aitken Walker/David Coultard/Johnny Dumfries.

Checkout the network of roads in that area. Proper SD country
