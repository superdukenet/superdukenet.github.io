I just returned (last night) from South Africa and I managed a 4 day ride at the end of business. Did a loop out of Cape Town with a buddy -- down to Cape Point and then east along the coast to Arniston, then to Franschoek and ended up in Paternoster on the west coast, north of Cape Town.

All great riding, mostly street with some gravel road sections thrown in - the area around Franschoek had the best twisties but all of the scenery was great. Had some issues with wind and rain. It's the second year in a row I've gotten to ride around the Western Cape and it's all been fantastic! Nambia next
