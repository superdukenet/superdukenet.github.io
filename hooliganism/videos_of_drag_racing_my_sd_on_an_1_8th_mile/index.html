Videos of Drag Racing my SD on an 1/8th mile

Davo-Singapore

2011-06-22 04:41:00 UTC
Took my bike out to an 1/8 mile drag strip this pass weekend. Brought my GoPro along and made a short video. The first 6-7 runs, I had allot of missed shifts. Basically my shifter needed to be mounted lower but I didn't have any tools with me. While riding on the street, I sit more centered on the seat and more upright and the shifter is fine, but when I was at the drag strip, I was all the way forward on the seat and my chest was basically on the tank, so my legs were angled forward a little more. Once I got used to it, I put down my best runs at the end.

No music so you can hear the bike. The first run where I get smoked, I was up against a ZX-14 with an extended swing arm and a drag tyre.



Short video of my best run again.
