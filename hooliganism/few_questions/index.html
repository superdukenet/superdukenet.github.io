Too bad for the Buell, good thing you're ok and back on the saddle with a great bike

Slip Ons - leovince, fmf, akrapovic, yoshimura, diferent styles, sounds and prices. All do the same thing. Remove extra weight and open up the lungs.

You can get the ecu flashed with akrapovic map @ dealer or get pcIII. Second allows better fueling maping with some extra $'s.

If you get the motobox the pcIII will be a good buy, just for the exhaust you can get away with the akro flash.

Out of the box you may need the rearsets to get your feet up, the exhaust to get it ligher and ecu flashed to upgrade the ignition and remove the gears limiters.

1st off welcome to the ONLY forum youl ever need for the SD and (hopefully) welcum to the family

Thats alot of questions so here we go -

Every SD is diffrent in regards to fueling etc, some have real problems with the herky jerky twichty throttle response whilst some bikes run perfect as stock!

On a very basic level for looks/sound/lose of weight alone you will want a set of cans and as your dealer stated you will need a simple re-flash of the ECU with the commonly refered to Akra mapping. If you really want the Motobox on top of that for the extra HP and noise then go ahead, there are some people here that run them with just the Akra mapping (and no PC3) and the bike will run fine but id suggest getting it on a dyno anyway just to check A/F ratios. Obviously for perfect fueling youl want the PC3 and custom map but its not a `must have` item! The 02 Sensors come with the PC3 but can be bought seperatly, i wont claim to no what they do though so i shall lkeave it there!)

Yes an intergrated rear light is available - Ebay is your friend!

The 2/3rd gear mod is a simple, cut 2 wire job that removes the restriction at the top end of the revs to keep noise down - take a look in the `How-To` section for details

Maintainence on these bikes is fairly simple and a full work manuel can be downloaded from this site for free - very useful

Apart from the above it is just a case of what you want from the bike, your already on the right track though as your looking at buying the fastest coloured SD - Black
