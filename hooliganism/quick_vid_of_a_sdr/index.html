Yeah no worries mate, I found it interesting watching the clips myself today.

Biggest thing when I am currently doing wheelies is I never get it right up to balance point, I am always at least slightly chasing the front wheel. I will one day get to the stage of rocking balance point all day long, but that is still a while off yet.

To clutch up in 2nd, I can do it from either a steady speed or while accelerating. Any speed below about 90km/h or so it is the same action, of simultaneously pulling the clutch in and twisting the throttle. The clutch is only in for like half a second.

It will make more sense when you see the video. Going to start chopping it together tonight after dinner.
