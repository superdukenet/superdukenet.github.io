Factory white Superdukes have painted and decaled plastics with a clearcoat. Here are the part numbers you'd need:

6110800300020A left headlight mask $49.99USD
6110800400020A right headlight mask $49.99USD
6100804100020A left side seat panel $89.99USD
6100804200020A right side seat panel $89.99USD
6110805000020A left side tank spoiler, $230.99USD
6110805100020A right side tank spoiler, $233.99USD
6100802014420A chin spoiler, complete, $171.99USD

The prices above are from KTM Cyclehutt in the US, but the part numbers should be useful everywhere. It's not cheap to swap with factory painted KTM parts. I did it on my SDR because I wanted white, but KTM USA wouldn't sell the parts to me, so I bought from KTM Versand in Germany. KTM Versand sold the white SDR parts as a complete kit, but I can't find it on their site now. They might have the Superduke parts as a more affordable kit than buying separately.

Decals to do the conversion are also available if you want to do the paint yourself. KTM part numbers are below:

61108098300 TANK STICKER SPOILER 08 $47.99USD
61008096500 STICKER FRONT CHIN SPOILER WHITE 08 $47.99USD

The white 990 numbers were shown, but no part number or price listed.

If you can paint yourself, you could save a lot. Good luck with the switch!
