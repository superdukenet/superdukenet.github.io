SDNerd

2011-03-05 07:55:00 UTC
Took one for a test ride today, very nice road bike suspension is very compliant compared with the sd immediately felt comfortable on the thing, engine has great low down torque although I was surprised it didnt have more top end it only had 600 odd kms on it so was probably still very tight engine wise fueling is so much better than the ktm which is no surprise to anyone. Handling wise i would say it is not as agile as my 05 but more confidence inspiring I would immediately be much faster on the road I would say then again I have never found a sd suspension setup I like for the road, as far as the track goes I would say the sd would be all over it funnily enough you would think the vtwin would have less power up top and more down low than the triple although this seems totally the opposite from what I experienced. Worst thing about the bike is that the seat seems to lock you in position and it is hard to move around on the thing foot pegs are also much higher. I am in the process of turning the sd into a track bike and am seriously considering the speed 3 as a road bike alternative.
