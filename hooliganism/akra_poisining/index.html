This fume thing is a pain for those who suffer it. Quite seriously, I'm not small (just 1/2 inch off 6 feet and short legs/long body) and I never had this problem. Even with the bird on the back (a one-off-never-to-be-repeated event) there was no smell either on our togs or in the air.

It has to be the rider's shape causing disrupted air flow which sucks the smell back. The wider the rider maybe causing higher than expected low pressure zone over the rear of the bike???

Joe, try sitting further back or forward and see if that helps. Try a cushion under your butt to raise you up a bit for a mile or so and see if that cures it - obviously not a permanent cure but it might point you in the right direction.
