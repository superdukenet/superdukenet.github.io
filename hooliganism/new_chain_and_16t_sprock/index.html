Got my chain and sprox done over the weekend and went for the 16t front sprock.
The difference the new chain has made is massive. I think I left my old one on there far too long. Trouble is as something goes off over a long period of time you don't notice it degrading. Anyway the bike rides brilliantly again with no rattly ships anchor chain accompaniment.
I've gotta say I'm hard pushed to notice any difference between the 16 and 17t sproxs though. I think I'm generally pretty insensitive to subtle changes cos I can't tell the difference between tyres either, but if I didn't know I now had a 16t sprocket on, I wouldn't have been aware anything had changed....

What year bikes do you guys own? Not all of the US spec machines use the same sprockets so you may think you had a 17 when it may have already been a 16...

I say this because some documentation provided by KTM themselves states the wrong sized sprockets... And I know that with ANY motorcycle I have ever owned, a 1 tooth smaller front sprocket is always quite noticeable...

Just for the record, I have a 2007 and it came with a 16... And I will be going up 2 teeth on the rear when it comes time to swap the chain and sprox..
