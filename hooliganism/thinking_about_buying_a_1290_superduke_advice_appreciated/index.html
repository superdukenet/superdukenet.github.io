The 1290r is the best bike I have owed to date. Yes it has issues! Front rotors drag when rolling it around garage, some have oil leaks. Most have the play in the rear wheel. But most of it was taking out with the higher torque spec bulletin. Are at least mine was. Stock seat is to low for proper riding position. Power parts seat much better for carving in the corners but a little soft though. OEM tyre are shit. Bike want to fall in to corner. Replace with Dunlop Q3. Problem solved. Neutrals Steering.

What's to like: it's all about the motor . Also they got it right this time with a smooth throttle. Handling is good as well. But if you want it even better put the Ohlins on the back. 20% better with a plusher ride.

I also got the race dongle and a firmware update so the bike now remembers prior settings. So yeah, I love the bike!
