Behind the headlight, lower left (left side of the bike).

I replaced all my blinkers with LED ones, and had to replace the flasher also, but the aftermarket flasher didn't work either for me. I had to add a third LED per side on the circuit to get it to flash. Just a single 12 volt LED tapped into the connecter under the seat. One for each side.
The SD electrical system has something weird about it, as I have two other KTM's with dualsport kits using LED blinkers, and they both work fine with the same flasher (pep boys #36 LED flasher).

A tech at my dealer told me to reinforce the ground leg on the circuit for both sides to battery negative, and that the stock flasher should work with the LED's. I have not tried that yet, nor have I tried the stock flasher with the third LED.

Let me know what you find.
