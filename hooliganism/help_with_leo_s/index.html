I'm with KTMGUY here...do not get any of the Akra maps loaded by KTM...too rich. Better off running stock map until you get the MH airbox and SAS removal kit. The pipes will get you 1 or 2 HP at best. The real benny is the weight reduction and music! Get the MH airbox! You will not regret it...I guarantee you will love it for the performance gains alone 8hp in the mid!!

I've personally not jumped on the TuneECU band wagon yet. I've got a PCIII and custom map (not worth the money IMHO, bike will run fine on stock map with Leo's and airbox (will run a little better with map but will do no harm without one)). One day I will get off my ass and dive into this Tune ECU thing as it looks like a good thing. Just don't have the time at the moment.

Have fun and keep us aprised of your progress.
