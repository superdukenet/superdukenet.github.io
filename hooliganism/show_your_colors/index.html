Here we go.
You have to admit that KTM orange is different.
It's not that I don't like it, but I haven't had to deal with it on my other bikes.

I know...as long as I'm seen. Maybe I should go hi-viz, but OMG...hi-viz green and orange? whoa...
What, if anything, did the experienced KTM people do to resolve this strange predicament?
