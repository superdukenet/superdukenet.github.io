I have Schuberth S1 (my 2nd one as I jumped off my bike with the first one), and my chicadee has got the R1. The R1 is a bit more suitable for arse-up-head-down bikes. S1 is better for real bikes.

The price you found is probably RRP. If you don't go for the S1 Pro, you can get he S1 (don't think there's much difference between them - I don't remember exactly but it wasn't worth the extra money when I bought my 2nd one) for Â£199.
http://www.helmetcity.co.uk/Schuberth-S ... AJDA7.aspx

They're really quiet; I only use earplugs on really long journeys (if I remember to put plugs in). They're really good aerodyniamically, they don't strain your neck at higher speed. For my head they're very comfortable too. I like the features; Pop down sun visor, very tight fit on the visor and sealed with a rubber lip. The first time I turned on the top vent it felt like taking a hat off, almost drafty.
Downside is that the shell is quite big, and you kinda look a bit like Dark Helmet from Spaceballs.

Try one!
