The Gin Reaper

2013-04-13 19:44:00 UTC

Yes it did. Tbh unknown to me at the time my bike wasn't in tip top condition. Only made 121 and a bit. Then went and did a trackday at Snetterton and she wasn't running right. At the end of the day GinReaper took my bike off to Track Electronics for John to have a look at it. Got a call a couple of days later saying bike wouldn't start and it was only putting out 1bar of fuel pressure when it should be 3.5. After investigation it seems there was loads of shite in the 2nd hand 09 SDR tank I'd bought. Fuel filters were blocked. As were injectors. James aka GinReaper was a legend and spent all his day off swapping my throttle bodies out for a spare set Ronin has and getting the bike running again. John put it on his Dyno and it was running so rich now it had full fuel pressure he had to load my original map that I emailed him. With a quick tweak he got 127 and he runs a Dyno Pro same as Projex. John thinks with an hours Dyno time he can get 130 out of her. The dude is a bit of a genius. Sets up a lot of the BSB bikes. There had been a BSB test at Snetterton a couple of days before and he'd been there working on bikes and some of the bikes were at his workshop for him to keep working on. Were some crazy electronics packages in the tail sections that he had designed. So I will be taking her back in a couple of weeks for him to work his magic. Will post up results then. Kev at Projex is a lovely bloke but not many are in the same league as John.
