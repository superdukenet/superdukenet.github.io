Flighty/Nervous front end after new tyre?

Aphex

2017-06-21 17:05:00 UTC
Maybe it's just because my last tyre had over 10k miles on it but I replaced my old RoadSmart II front tyre with another of the same model and it used to be rock steady up to 138mph now if I go over 115mph the front end starts to oscillate, lucky for me I had a long long straight road to ease off the throttle and let things settle down.

But it's definitely worrying since I used to be able to take sweepers at 100mph+ but I'm very nervous to even try that now.

The tyre pressure is exactly the same as it was before 32psi for canyons and 36psi for commuting. No changes to the fork tubes or preload/compression/rebound damping either.

Maybe I just haven't felt a truly round front tyre in a long time? The bike is definitely more willing to change direction at speed which is nice but I lost so much stability (I'm very very light on the bars).
