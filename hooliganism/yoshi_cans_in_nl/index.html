Supaduke

2011-12-09 15:58:00 UTC
It's not like in Germany (not yet), where the first thing they look for is the EEC mark.
And I've never been stopped for loud can(s), and been riding with them for over 30 years or so.
But, beware when visiting events such as WSB and TT at Assen, or motorbeurs Utrecht, there will be a place somewhere near the circuit, where the police is checking bikes for noise, and other (illegal) things.
But, in my opninion, if you (youre bike) is not TO loud, you wil not be bothered.
Maybe next year things are different, and noise becomes an issue, for now not so.
