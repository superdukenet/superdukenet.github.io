AGRO!

2011-09-30 06:14:00 UTC
I'm not going to tell you to buy a super duke. But for me its the best bike I have owned.
Its not hard to fix with Tune ecu just have to know what you are doing. If you need help there are a number of Great blokes on here that can help.
It seems like all the blokes on here are great people.
But for me the first thing is get rid of those standard mufflers as they get really hot and don't let the engine breath how it should.
Then get Tuneecu cable and download a new map turn off the sas plus 02 sensors and take out flies and turn them off also with tuneecu.
Balance throttle bodies again using Tuneecu and to make life easier get MH air box.
You don't need to do all these mods if you don't want to you can still get the bike to go fine with standard equipment but you will need Tuneecu to turn the sas and 02 sensors off. Or you can just buy the resistors to eliminate the 02 sensors but your better off spending an extra $15 on the tune cable.
For me the less Super Duke's on the road the better as you don't come across many when out and about so its a bit like owning a one of and you get a lot of looks.
I know that Ducati have had a lot of fuelling probs also and it costs a whole lot more to get things right with them.
So good luck with you choice.
