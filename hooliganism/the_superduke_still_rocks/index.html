I totally love my SDR
After 2009 I started thinking about driving the KTM Superdukebattle
Fun for me is, getting faster on the track. Fast laps = fun

But after one year on a Yamaha R6 I wasnt sure if I would be able to get faster on a Superduke and I wasnt sure what to do

Now, after 2010 and one year driving my SDR nearly as fast as my R6 (which wasnt that slow either) and even faster on some tracks, I loooooove this bike
To show up with this bike on the track and see all the superbike driver with a very confused look, this is great

But like I said, I want to develop, get faster and I think I nearly reached this limit on my sdr

So now in 2011 I have to let her go and will change to a RC8R - 2009

I hope this will be nearly as much fun as my SDR, but Im not sure yet


Im gonna miss my SDR
