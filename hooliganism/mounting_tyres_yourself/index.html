Bought this for bead breaking but to be honest i use it more for changing off road tyre's





i've found this simple bead breaker far better and quicker



A usfull tip if your gonna have a go is leave your new tyre's in the sun to warm up or next to a radiator for 10 mins they slip on far easier when warm.

Balancing is also very easy, the back wheel never seems far out so normally i dont bother adding any weights,the front i will split the weight evenly between both side's of the rim.




IMO it's worth getting your own gear as you save time and cash, you'll also need a compressor though to pump em up!
