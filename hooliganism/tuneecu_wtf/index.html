Bert

2010-10-28 09:58:00 UTC
Klinck, I guess you want to know which color from the black plug goes where?
You'll have to use a multimeter to verify what color the wire on position 4, 7 and 16 have.

The multimeter should be in "Ohm" setting. The display should read "OL".
Put a paperclip in the plug, put one lead from the multimeter on the paperclip and the other lead on the wire. Now try the different wires. When the display changes to "0.00" you have found the wire.

If the OBD plug you got only has the 3 wires and they are black, yellow/orange and red there is a 95% chance that 4 = black, 7=yellow/orange and 16 = red.

Hope this helps.
