Colonel_Klinck

Went out for a quick ride this morn. Got 10 mins from home, opened her up and she died No major noises that I heard but it was sudden. I have a slipper so no wheel locking up or anything silly like that. Lucky I do have one as I as in a right hand fast bend at the time and if she had locked it would have been very very nasty. Tried to start her and she refused to turn over. Called a cab, came home and collected her in the van. Decided to whip out the plugs and try and crank her by hand. Rear plug came out and my heart sank. This is what it looks like.





Something has hit it. I can't imagine the piston could actually come free in an instant and hit it so it's most likely dropped a valve. I hope it's not something I did last week, I didn't touch the valves at all. Fook knows how much damage its done on the inside. Going to have to strip the rear cylinder and have a look. Not what I need less than a week before the next race and am feeling very broke without this. Maybe I should have changed the valves when I had the heads ported and polished 8k miles ago. Too late now though. Will update when I've found out what's caused this.
