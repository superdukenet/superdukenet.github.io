KTM WTF?!?!?

990EXODUS

2010-04-30 00:39:00 UTC
It seems there are quite a few people here whose experiences with the mighty SD/SDR have been varied, to say the least. I am kind of surprised. Are these fellas beating the living snot out of their bikes on a daily basis? I have nearly 7000 trouble free miles on my 07 SD. It has been a flawless back road weapon, and I have NEVER enjoyed EVERY little ride on any other bike anywhere NEAR as much as this.
I can't believe that KTM's production tolerances are this variable. I can only hope that in the near future, KTM's commitment to 2 wheeled nirvana becomes the benchmark for quality products with excellent, reliable engineering.
