Some more info:

The coaching day takes you through 3 levels on the day, so there's a lot to take in. Gates open at 7am for 8 am start. Coaching day 9th...$295 + $20 MA license

Friday 10th...track day....$190 + $20 MA license + $40 for unpowered covered garage, or $60 if you need powered site. You can share the garages 3 or 4 to a garage is cosy.
Otherwise you'll be out in the sun/rain ( hopefully not) unless you have a tent.


Link to Motorcycle Sportsmen who run the event : http://www.motorcyclesportsmen.com.au/

Link to Warwick accommodation : http://www.morganparkraceway.com.au/accom.htm

Hi Reb

You're right the Friday track day always precedes a weekend of racing there. I've done a few there, and it never creates any problems. In fact is is grass roots old style pit next to the racers and talk your heads off!

As the whole show is run by the Motorcycle Sportsmen Club, ( a Fantastic group of people) unlike other track days it is focussed on education, fun, and camaraderie amongst like minded enthusiasts. Not focussed on money making !

This is the only venue I've found that PROVIDE and encourage lap timing devices, so that you can monitor your times for consistency, indicating good rhythm and race craft and improvement in times. Also cameras on the bikes....no problem.

Plus this will be my first time on the new extended track. Some really fast sweepers to complement the tight original track.

Looking forward to it.
