I have run inline fours on the street for the past 19 years and it is all I have ever known. I have ridden twins occasionally (BMW R1100S, Honda VTR1000, etc) but never spent alot of time on one. I can tell I have some things to learn but I am catching on quickly.

My Z1000 was a 2006 model and I liked everything about it except the inferior suspension. That is why I sold it. If you are a casual rider the bike is great but I rode it pretty hard and I could never get the chassis to do what I wanted. The motor was great and liked to rev, the brakes are good and overall the bike was fairly comfortable for it's type. The bike likes to wheelie as well so be carefull the first time you wack it.

The Z1000 is totally new for 2007 with alot more power and better suspension components. I saw one a few weeks ago and in my opinion the new one is butt ugly.

I have ridden my SD for only approx. 100 miles so I can't really make a good comparison between the two bikes yet. It was 19 degrees f. when I picked it up so I wasn't able to really have too much fun.
