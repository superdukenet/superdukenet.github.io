Got the Evotech product today. It really is very sturdy and very well designed. Quality is top notch. The only issue I had is that the wires from the turn signals seemed to be a little bit short. Not sure if this is an issue with the USA models or not. The two white connectors just barely exited the tail tidy housing and they end up about half in and half out of the hole to the storage space in the tail where they ideally should be inside of the storage space. And there is a little pressure of the tail tidy structure pushing on those connectors. I may take it back apart and see what I can do. The wire from the license plate light was plenty long enough.

Bottom line is everything is plug and play and it all works well and looks great. I guess it is pricey but you get a very high quality product and I am very happy with how it turned out.

The directions online are the best I ever have seen. Really clear.
