2011-08-22 08:57:00 UTC

I have the Cycle Control rearesets on mine, but if you get some, don't stress too much about where you move your pegs to, as due to the length of the various linkages, there's actually only a couple of positions that really work (you need to keep the push-rod thingy at the right angle, and the adjuster plate for that is less adjustable than the main bracket) - so it's not like you have a wide choice.

Mine have ended up back and upwards about 5cm, and like anything, you get used to it - and it's MUCH better on track.
