I would just be looking at the perforations and venting.

You would be surprised how efficient they can be at cooling if well placed. I usually try and get perf in the arms and across the upper chest. Simple tricks can help get air flowing as well such as unzipping the cuff of the sleeve, lets a great amount of air in.

I am really not a fan of mesh jackets, as you dehydrate quicker in them than leather.

I have not heard any bad reports about Joe Rocket stuff, but I have not heard any good reports either, take from that what you will.
