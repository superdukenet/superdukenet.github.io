I had the Go Pro wide angle (pre HD). I wasn't a fan of the controls and abandoned using it after I unwittingly videotaped myself breaking various traffic laws while believing the camera was turned off. The LED screen should prevent such misadventures.

I recently purchased a Contour GPS. The camera has a huge slide bar and red LED that indicate it is recording, so I shouldn't have any more mishaps. The only particular downside I saw to the Contour was, again, its lack of a viewfinder. Contour recently rectified that problem by releasing a bluetooth card and I-Phone app that allows you to view the camera's output on your i-Phone. It works pretty well, but has frozen on me a couple of times.

The reason I bought the Contour is that you can make some pretty cool videos, complete with gps locations and speeds using this software: www.Dashware.net. Of course, since I bought the camera, I haven't had an opportunity to get back to the track so, it sits in its box waiting to be unleashed.
