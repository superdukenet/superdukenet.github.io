rocketchick

2011-03-27 05:07:00 UTC
I have one on my SDR. It work great. I use it because my wrist tends to get real sore at the end of a long day ride. arthritis I think.

I live here in Norther California. And while there are lots of good twisty road around my area. There are also good roads out of my area that require me to hit the Freeway for several miles. This is where the Kaoko throttle lock come in.

The Kaoko throttle lock is fully adjustable. You can set it so that when it is fully locked. it can easily be turned off. What I mean is there is just a enough pressure holding the throttle open with out the throttle spring returning the throttle to the off position. And if you like to life dangerous you can adjust it tighter.

I believe there is two version. One for Hand guards and one with out. I have the one with out.

It's a good product that's well made. But not cheap.
