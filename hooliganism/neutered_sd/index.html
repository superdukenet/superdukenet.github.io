I've been happy with baffles in but then it turned out at Brands that even with baffles in I'm still hitting 103-104 decibels so I don't think it'd be a good idea to take em out.

On the RC8 I've left the baffles in and it sounds great but then the akra for that is not baffled for the road either and so is still 102db.

Overall though the RC8 sounds louder than the SD which is obviously due to the location of the exhaust and so it seems like you've got the baffles out anyway without actually removing them so it still feels like you're getting that louder unbaffled sound compared to other bikes but without pissing off the general public as much, plus being low down it doesn't pump noise and exhaust gases into the nearest open car window in traffic which can only keep them even happier.
