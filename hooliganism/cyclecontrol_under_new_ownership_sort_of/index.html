Dr Honda

Hi All,

OK... I know there are probably a bunch of people here who are wondering about CycleControl. (and who are mad because they are waiting for parts)

What has happened:

Ian Yong has CF. Many years ago, he had a lung transplant, and everything was fine. This past spring, he had complications due to the anti-rejection meds he was taking. Basically, his kidneys shut down. After a 6 week stay at the hospital, back in May (The original time I had to help out) it seemed like everything was going to be OK. After about a month or so, he started to have problems with the tubes in his chest that are used for the Dialysis. (infections) Since then, he has been in the hospital for almost 4 months. Every time the infections would clear up, and he thought he was getting out... he would get bad again. This is why parts have not been shipped. There were a few more health issues... but we will leave it at that.

As of the writing of this post, Ian is still in the hospital, but he has agreed to hand over the company (all inventory) to the control of A&L Motorsports.

As some of you know, I worked with Ian in the R&D stages of all his parts, and I am the manufacture.

Right now, I am going to try to take care of all the unresolved issues with open orders, and after that, I will have the parts posted on my web site. Since I am the manufacture for all of CycleControls parts now... the quality will be unchanged.


With that said... if you have an open order, please email or PM me with your name, and what you ordered. (and order number if you know it) Also tell me if you would like your parts or a refund.

Last thing... obviously, since the parts will be sold under my label, CycleControl will soon be shut down. (as soon as Ian is able to close it down)

I am truly sorry for all the problems that are going on with CycleControl, but hopefully things can be set right again.

Thanks

Tony.
