Got this off another site. Rider sent queries to several tyre companies on whether they use mold release agents. Dunlop and Pirelli said no, BUT (combining the answers) ride cautiously for the first 100 miles to get the feel of the new tyres and scrub them in as tyres are shiny when new as consumers like the looks.
This is the response from Michelin:

Thank you for your email. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you.

Concerning your question, Michelin has a mold release agent on all
motorcycle tyres. This will cause the tyre to be slippery in the first
few miles, but that goes away after the tyre is scrubbed in. This normally
occurs in the first 25-50 miles or so. Until then, the rider should use
caution in riding the tyre at accelerated speeds. Michelin always
recommends obeying the speed limits and using care whenever riding.
