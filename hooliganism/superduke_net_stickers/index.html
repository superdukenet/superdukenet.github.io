well i suspect that as bikers we're all closet sticker junkies, so i've been working on some stickers for this site for quite a while, and thanks to Ride_KTM and lockers i've finally gotten round to pushing this project forward... here are three colours for all you fast orange owners








and one for the even faster black SD owners





the above images are all a photoshop job, i'm still working on tracing the letters with the right amount of curve in the baseline so as to compensate for the curved exhaust cowls

i'm thinking of printing them at a friendly store who print motocross/enduro numbers, so the quality will be the best available and they will stand up to pressure washers/rain/etc

as Ride_KTM pointed out, i'll see how many people are interested -the more stickers i print the lower price per sticker will be- and i'll try and send them out as soon as i get them printed, OR i will add a download link here and you can take the file (adobe illustrator .eps) to your local printer and get them done yourself in whatever colour suits your fancy

i'm open to any other suggestions - some people might find the size / positioning too obvious or too large, so let me know what you think people and i can make some alternatives

oh, and thanks to you all for making this a great site!
