2010-08-04 13:36:00 UTC

I have this Shoei XR1100 which I use mainly on the road and on my RC8, it fits my intercom nicely into it for my sat nav as it has recesses specifically for this and is very comfortable and the best ventilation I've ever had, the only downside of the ventilation being that there's no mesh on the inlet ports and flys seem to be pushed through into the airstream that runs over the visor and bounce around in there occasionally hitting me in the eye so that's a pain when it does happen but it's a great helmet so I'd still get another one if I needed to.



If I knew it existed at the time I may have been swayed to this colour scheme instead



Then on track and some weekend blasts on the SD I use this AGV Stealth

My only issue with it is that the padding around the ears is really tight on the ears, if I wear it too long my ears hurt, it pushes the ear plugs too, it was a complete disaster when I had my intercom fitted and so I stopped wearing it unless I needed the intercom as it would have been too much hassle to keep taking it in and out, so when my old track helmet disintegrated that I was using instead I was very happy to replace it with the xr1100 and be able to use it again.
