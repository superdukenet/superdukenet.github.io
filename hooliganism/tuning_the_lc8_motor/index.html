How would you overbore alloy-Nikasil cylinders?...



Considering the SDs highly over-square 101 x 62.4 mm bore & stroke ratio, increasing the bore further might not be a good thing. Even the considered borderline 1098 Testastretta Evoluzione engine from Ducati has a 104 x 64.7 mm bore & stroke. That's a less extreme ratio than an SD.

Aprilia RSV 1000 R Factory: 97 x 67.5 mm

It's stroke you want. Much better for low-end and midrange power & torque too.

It's not the thickness of the liner that is the problem, some engines have the coating direct onto the cylinder walls, it's the thickness of the 'skirt', the part of the liner that extends below the cylinder and into the crankcase. Too thin, and this will break under preasure from the piston skirt at BDC.
