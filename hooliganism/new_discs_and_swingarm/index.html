New discs and swingarm

bic_bicknell

2011-12-20 21:57:00 UTC
Here's my latest upgrades. Those fantastic American cast iron discs and carriers. Worth the wait and effort and price. Combined with those Brembo monoblocks they are the best brakes I ever rode with, bar non. And that's not even waiting for them to bed in properly. Check out the photos, first one is them just fitted with about 200 metres of braking done. Still black and coated. The other is after 25 miles of fast A roads getting them hot at 100mph and then a bit of B road fun. Still got a slight bit of the black coating but the feedback and bite is awesome. Total one finger stuff for most riding. Can't wait to try them on track. Not sure if any events left this year, maybe have to wait till spring but this bike is ridden all year round so we'll see how they fair in British salt - not started yet but imminent.

Also fitted is the '08 black swing arm and back brake caliper that I've had sitting about for months. Got them from eBay and not had time to fit them till now. Purely cosmetic I` thought 'till I tried the brake and realised that it was about ten times better than the brake I've been using for four years???? Not sure why, same disc but different brake and pads. Anyway I locked up the back wheel a few times with the usual heavy footed effort I've been used to using in those situations where you actually use the back brake. I'll have to re- calibrate my brain now.

Swingarm has not made any difference at all to riding experience. But Black is way time better than silver so I'm well chuffed.
