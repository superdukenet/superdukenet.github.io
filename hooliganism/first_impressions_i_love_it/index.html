2010-03-15 05:29:00 UTC
OK...rode it quite a bit this weekend...what great weather! First thing I noticed...people in cars suck! I had so many different idiots either cut me off or just ignore me. I'm brand new to street riding and I have to say that you need to be on your game all the time. OK...rant over...the engine does feel a little better but it is still somewhat jerky at low throttle. Bear in mind this is a completely stock bike. I wonder if the stock pipes with the stock mapping are just really lean? I don't know but I really do want to look into some new slipons. The stock cans also make it hard to hear the bike...for me and for cars. I rotated the bars forward and moved the controls up...much better. Also noticed the wind for the first time. Guess it's all in getting used to it. Love it though. Trying to piece together a must do upgrade list by educating myself on all the options. Taking my time. Hopefully get some nice evening rides in this week with the time change giving us another hour of daylight here in the west!
