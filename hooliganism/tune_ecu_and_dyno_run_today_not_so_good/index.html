Tune Ecu and Dyno Run today.. not so good

TC's SD

2011-03-16 19:20:00 UTC

Well some may have seen that I was going to get my bike on the dyno to see how things were with the afr. I have never been or bothered about what the HP is (says a man who has a 720 bhp car lol ) My main reason was to check the AFR, as i have always been worried about using other maps etc

So anyway

My set up

07 SD
Akra slipons
Motohooligan Airbox
SAS removed
Motohooligan 2ndry Flies

and using tuneecu this map
08 SD james' map for grim and motobox (will run motobox as it is rich enough).hex
https://files.me.com/craig.wiltshire/88oy9d

So to the bit you all wanted to know.. well not good news really. it was running really lean !!.

I will post up he plots when i have scanned them in, but from 2000 rpm to 3000 rpm its 17 on afr. 3000 rpm to 4000 rpm from 17 gradually to 14. then from 4000 rpm to 5000 rpm drops to just over 13 (bang on

I have my car tuner from the states over next week so may get him to look at it aswell.

I am not slatting craigs map, so please dont think i am, it is just not good for my bike, which is a shame. I have noticed though opening up the map that some of the values in the table you can see where it goes lean, from the 35% at 2000 to 3000 rpm right through to 100% throttle between 2000 rpm and 3000 rpm.

Oh and it pulled 110.7 and 68.45 ftlbtq
