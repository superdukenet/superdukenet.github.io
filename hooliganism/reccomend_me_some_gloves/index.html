2010-08-01 11:40:00 UTC
If you want the best, and no budget, Kushitani, or RS-Taichi.

Dainese and Alpinestars maybe the best of the rest, but made in Japan is way better than made in Pakistan. The stitching is important. It doesn't matter if the leather is good enough or not, if it doesn't stay together.

I bought Kushitani race gloves some years ago. The online shop was only Japanese. I was translating each part of the ordering form, then it turned out you had to select a prefecture (province), so i had to give up. I emailed them...got a very polite reply, got help with sizing, gave card details and so on...they sent them out the same day, the Japan PO sent them out that day - EMS - and I got them about 6 days later.

When you hold them in your hands and look at them, you'll see, they are quite different from Alpinestars etc. The Alpinestars and Dainese they sell in the shops are not what they get made for motogp teams.

I can't find the page at but highly recommend, if you like to get emails with nice pics, good photo work, one pic a month. Not catalog pics, photos, the gear on riders on bikes.
