Ok guys,. I have to come clean...

I love my SDR to bits, but I keep looking at bikes from the likes of Roland Sands and Kott, and the Jesse Rooke KTM LC8, and even ooogling the new Norton Commando... and I like these timless butch machines that are little more than an engine, tank, seat and wheels...

I'd love a retro attitude bike that handled like an SD, with looks that were timless, not retro or modern... and the SD is after all a simple V-twin in a trellis frame... a great starting point for a nasty streetbike with backstreet brawler looks...

I did this cut'n'paste pic (below) in about 5 mins at work (using Word, LOL) but I wanted to see how an old style SD (I prefer the shape of the tank) would look with carbs and header tank from a 950SM, spoked wheels, no rear panels, a low exhaust and simple slimline headlight... Am I sick, or perhaps inspired? I reckon it's do-able (as a second bike of course)... Think of it as Eric Buell working for KTM.

Dutch,

Your not crazy. I get it. I fell in love with the SD back in 2003 or 2004 when I saw it naked in a preview-review. I don't care for the looks of the bike dressed but I like the trellis frame, big dirt bike engine, massive forks, and no linkage rear. It is basically my dream platform. So the rest isn't perfect, It will get there over time.

Think of just cutting the BS off the bike, and it will kind of build itself.
The rear side panels do nothing for the bike, and make standing on the pegs uncomfortable. Enduro style headlight gets rid of the huge thing on the front. The factory taillight is ugly as well, room for improvement. Low single pipe gets rid of the stink on the leathers and probably will cut weight,( I have my old Ti Akra from the 640 to go on when I can find a cheap header to chop). I agree on the fuel tank and have a few ideas. The oil tank/battery situation is the way it is for handling, but a small battery could be put in front of the Moto-box or under the seat and leave room for a SE/SM oil tank in place of the old battery. The wheels are nice but not necessary and will end up adding weight, ( I want a set of 18"-19", single disk for some dirtier conditions).

What would be left is a lean, mean and not over stylized. Murdered out or a nice orange frame, either way I think it would be the tits.
