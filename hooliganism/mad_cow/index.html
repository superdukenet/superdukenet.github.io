Hello there fellow riders....well, I knew I would be writing something here sooner or later, so.....



last saturday night, I was going back home from my aunt´s house in Iguala to my house in Taxco ( mexico by the way ) I was going about 70mph on a very cool and twisty road....riding my KTM Superduke 990, when in the middle of the road there was a freaking HUGE black cow just chilling and waiting for me.....I hit the brakes hard, but was only a feet feet away from it......BOOM.....I flew atop the cow, my bike did a front flip and landed on its tail, I landed on my head first and then my back and slid about 15 feet more....................

I was semi atgatt, I say semi because I wasn´t wearing full boots, I was wearing alpinestar´s snicker style boot, first gear armored pants and jacket and Zox full helmet, no gloves......

results : cow....dead from impact, 180 degrees looking the wrong way, spine broke and the body twisted, that was freaky looking.......ME : both ankles SEVERILY sprained and some minor fractures on the right one, my right knee susteined some heavy lacerations but after x-rays and all, all looks good, we´ll have to sit and wait 3 weeks from now to check back on it......road rash on back of the hands and a 5x5 road rash on my left buttock, damw it hurtz
