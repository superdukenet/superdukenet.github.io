Picked up a GoPro HD Motorsports package today!

So far, I'm extremely pleased with the camera. You can mount it virtually anywhere using either the suction cup mount or the 3M adhesive mounts.

One issue I ran into however: I tried using my 16G PNY Optima Class 4 SDHC card in it and it kept freezing the camera. Upon looking into it, it seems this specific card wreaks some kinda mean havoc on the GoPro and it's a well documented issue. For anyone wanting a GoPro, avoid this brand of card.
