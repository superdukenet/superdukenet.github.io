The tank and bar bolts on the SD are the same diameter (8mm) so if you get a RAM from Bob Telfer ask for a 50mm bolt length and cut it down to suit. Email him a pic just to be safe though.

You are welcome to try mine on your bike - it will hold the tank down (about 5 turns of thread) but ideally would be longer. Worth a try if you have 5 minutes spare!

Just for the sake of passing information on; I've just taken delivery of a replacement bike mount for the Zumo. I had lots of problems with water ingress on our recent European trip, i.e. water got into the USB connector under the Zumo. This meant taking the cradle off the bike and drying it in a rest stop hand dryer.

After that, the music & directions sometimes only came out of the r/h speaker, whilst Autocom chatter was in both.

The original is now behaving itself, so I have a cradle for both bikes.

Happy days!
