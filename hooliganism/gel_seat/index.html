Just got done with a short stint on the Gel Seat and I gotta say it's waaayyyy better than stock (at least for someone of my size). The firmer marterial keeps you on top of seat in lieu of sinking down into it allowing you to move much more readily from side to side making quick transitions from left to right much easier (not that it was that difficult before, you just don't have to post up as much). The comfort is substantially better as well.

I'd say if you weigh over 220lbs this is the seat for you. The problem with the stocker is the foam density is so soft that you blow right through it and end up sitting on the hard plastic seat base...not good for freeway expansion joints or bumpy roads. If you are under 200lbs the stocker is probably fine for you.

Crotch, thanks again for letting me take a put with your seat!!! Like I said earlier, I almost didn't come back to the Rock Store, just wanted to keep going. I will not ride the bike again without one!

Hope this helps...sure helped me!
