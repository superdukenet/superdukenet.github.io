Crashed the SD today

oafboy182

2011-01-10 03:27:00 UTC

I lost the front coming around a right hand corner on my way to my usual ride with a friend and slid across both lanes into the shoulder where we came to rest in the weeds. Good thing it's not a well traveled road. Not sure exactly how it happened but it was only 38 deg this morning and the road might have just been a little frozen. I didn't see any ice but it's been moist and cold so who knows. I dislocated my left index finger and dislocated and broke my thumb. the doc couldn't get my thumb set all the way so I have to go to a specialist this week. The bike might have a bent sub-frame and the front end off is out of whack. Handlebars and wheel don't line up. The best part is the guy who was working on a fence saw me crash and when I showed him my hand and asked for a ride to the hospital he said he was to busy. He didn't do much to instill my confidence in the Hispanic population where I live. His attitude is pretty much how the majority of the hispanics are around here and the reason most non-hispanics want them all sent home. I'm just glad a nice lady came by just then and was willing to turn around and take me to the emergency room. All my gear did its job and held up well.














I put the last one in there just to finish on a high note.
