These appear to be facsimiles of the SIMPSON BANDIT helmet. They are mostly not street legal but do have a few legal models. I looked at getting one a few years ago, but couldnt try one on anywhere and also heard they were shocking at coping with wind! I'd advise searching for a REAL Simpson helmet to be more confident of a quality lid if this is the style you seek.

As Shade says these are copies of the old Simpson Bandits.

I had one back in the 80's. I think they were made for car racers but have since been adopted by the streetfighter brigade.

They are / were excellent for posing around the town looking badass.

I doubt the original Simpson would be accepted at trackdays either. However, these are copies so may be made to modern standards.

I would say try one on before you buy and be prepared to have another lid for general riding.

Coolest looking lids ever though
