Viking

2013-02-28 23:04:00 UTC
The german kit is really thick and heavy though.

For a road traffic bike I'd get a Skidmarx one. Or that extended standard one. Think it's called a mojo or something.

The Replicast. I got two and they're both slightly different. The fitting kit they supply with it is actually for the Skidmarx one and you have to make it fit. I suppose if it's going on a road bike and just for looks you could get it on ok. But if your using it on track it needs to be mounted as high as possible to stop it dragging the ground at lean. Even up tight it will still touch down. If you've got an evo system if is real tight on the left hand side at the rear header and melts the pan. I had to wrap the exhaust and bond heat matting to the inside if it and after our last a few sessions at Oulton on tue it's all black and bubbled the decals already. Takes a little bit of fiddling about with spacers and bending brackets to get it spot on.
