I have always run my Akras without baffles. Both the round cans and Evo cans. But if you are doing a custom map on a dyno then you really need to decide which you will run the most as they need different fuel/ air settings. Stratkat

2017-02-16 16:28:00 UTC
Well I couldn't resist hearing how she'd sound with the baffles out so rolled her out on driveway and fired her up - lovely!
Much freer sounding, and maybe even freer revving. Can't explain the sound difference but I think the baffle really choke the pipes, the baffles added a kind of tappiness to the sound.
The outlet have gone from 31mm to 48mm so no doubt she's breathing better.

I think she has been running rich and guess she was mapped with them out.

Reason for thinking this is in the short time I've run her, a section of one can has started to discolour due to heat - for this to have just started to happen something must have changed, like the baffles being fitted. I guess the PO installed them for the trading in, or the shop installed them.
Finally the baffles were very clean considering, the pipe insides have much more carbon on them that the baffles did.

I suppose I'd know for sure once I road test her, which isn't until at least March, though it has been a lovely day today.
