She's Home and tucked away in the shed.

chip

2008-11-22 11:35:00 UTC
Looong day.
Drove down to Sydney early this morning and picked the SDR.


The bride followed me to a couple of our kids place where we locked the bike away and headed off to the Sydney Motorcycle Show. No as interested this year strangely. (That Aprilia Dorsoduro might be good fun though.)
Back to the kids place and loaded the bike as we had reports of snow and ice in the Blue Mountains. It got down to 3 deg C and then we saw the remnants of snow, then the hail and rain hit around Lithgow. Very happy that I was in the ute.

Anyway, she's home and tucked away waiting to see what tomorrow's weather brings.
