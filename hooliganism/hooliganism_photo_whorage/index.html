Supaduke

2011-09-09 06:13:00 UTC
Sitting upright…not hunched over the tank Accelerating part throttle (smaller opening the better) 5500rpm shut off and instant…. bang full throttle (aggressive actions)

......some people have anti wheelie software installed in brain...it will not let them twist the throttle fast enough…and the speed of the twist is the key

....er.....second gear or third gear is best.....things happen a lot slower…….and contrary to belief the faster you are going the more stable it is

On a big twin shutting off the throttle will bring the front back down….or a touch of the rear brake
Hands off the front brake completely......try not to Comfort touch the front brake as you want the front wheel spinning ......for stability and touch down ….and funnily enough it does not work anyway

………this will prevent lock up on landing (which is very often the reason you see people binning it on landing)
