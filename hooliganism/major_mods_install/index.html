The O2 sensor eliminators defeat the O2 sensors in the pipes. These sensors are constantly trying to lean ou the F/A mixture to appease the EPA. This results in jerky throttle action. If you bought the Tune ECU cable you can turn them off when you load your new map.

If you are not familiar with Tune ECU I would refrain from messing about too much. That software will allow you to F things up pretty good if you do not know what you are doing.
