So which is it? Are you looking for more aggressive or more comfortable?

If you're looking for more comfort than a riser (taller than stock) is your best bet. If you're looking for more aggressive, then get yourself a Ducati Streetfighter handlebar. It's noticably lower and more angled in a 'sport' orientation. I have one on my SDR and its the bees knees!

Sammy, just make sure that any handlebar you get is a 'fat' type or sometimes called 'tapered'. This means that it will fit into your OE clamps and also fit the controls and levers. There are a few threads on this - I remember measuring all the diameters and posting them up - I think the thread is called, "Handle bar and bends". Rizoma do some good flat style bars that fit right on. And they do them in black too!

There is another reason why I wanted flatter bars that is not to do with handling and weight distribution at all - and no one has mentioned this. The SD has no wind protection, (don't even come back and say it has a fly screen!! Like a few have said it's all about posture on the bike where you feel happy.
