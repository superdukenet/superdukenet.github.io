The 1290...........what more can u say its just sex on wheels
After the missus dug her kneels in and is not willing to work the corner for me so I can buy one im now resorting to the next best thing, make my sdr look like one.

This has partly come about after last year a f@#kin stupid ass women pulled straight out of the supermarket and took me out one evening. So far
Ive trimmed up the carbon front gaurd (had a nice crack in it) and painted
Relocated the ignition to the rear
Painted the tank
Removed the headlight and a german streetfighter units going on
Made a new mount for the speedo
Stripped and started on painting the rims
Side pods and tail section are prepped for spraying. Existing airbrushing is going to stay on the pods but going to have the "R" running through it.
