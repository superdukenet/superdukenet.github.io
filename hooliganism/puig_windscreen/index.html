I don't get it guys. The SD is a naked sports bike. That means it hasn't got a fairing or a big ugly touring screen. If you wanted a bike that protected you from wind blast and rain etc. why didn't you choose a bike that had a big ugly fairing in the first place?

I have to admit I've tried the KTM touring screen and another one, (Skidmarx), just out of interest and the difference on a long ride to neck and shoulders is not really that beneficial but the look is so bad that I couldn't live with them for more than a day.

I guess it's all a matter of taste really and historically in the USA you have often made your bikes ugly with big touring fairings. But as you embrace these European bikes - why not love them for what they are?
