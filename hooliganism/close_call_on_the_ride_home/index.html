Close call on the ride home..:.

matt_black

2011-05-07 17:30:00 UTC
So I'm riding home from work last night. I got off a bit earlier than usual, so that was good.

Anyhow, I was just cruising along, casually filtering through traffic. I came up behind a Harley Electra-Glide CVO. He's moving along at a pretty good clip, so I settled in behind him & flowed. After about 30 seconds of this, he moves over 2 lanes, so I figured he's clearing out of the way to let me by....

We both go by the same car at about the same time, then he decides he wants back over into the fast lane. Where I was. He cut back across the #2 lane, and damn near broadsided me. This ignorant fecker didn't even bother looking before he just jammed over.

I doubt he even knew I was behind him in the 1st place.

Scared the everlovin shit outta me. Mostly because I assume other riders would try to be as aware of their surroundings as I do. He probably had the cruise control set, and was looking for some Jethro Tull tunes on the stereo.

Gotta love Southern California traffic.
