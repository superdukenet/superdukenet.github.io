There is only ONE good mechanic in the LA area:

Eric's Motorcycles in Pasadena. Eric, and his chief mechanic, Brent, eat, sleep, and drink KTM. Also, there are stories floating around the internet about how he has a bad attitude, etc. But, from my experiences, he is a fantastic person, very nice to deal with, is no more expensive than anyone else, and he is HONEST.

Trust me, it may be a long-haul for you from MDR, but it is worth it. When they did the 600 mile service on my bike it was perfect. You could not tell that they had the bike torn apart. Well, a couple of wrench marks on the valve-cover nuts, but that was it. No grease, nor fingerprints, scratches. Nothing. It was a truly great experience. And the best part was they asked ME when I wanted the bike back, instead of telling me when I could have it back. Pretty cool!


Mark.
