Most of this self indulgent, obsession driven list is a direct result of joining this forum and reading about what I could do, getting sucked into the thrill and nearly driving my, (lets face - it very tolerant partner, Amy), up the wall.

Things got off Ebay cheap;

1. Carbon rear hugger and chain guard.
2. Carbon clutch and ignition engine protectors.
3. Dave Heal's Superduke series race shock for £125.

Things got that weren't cheap;

1. LED integrated tail / indicator light from Aus. Super cool I think, love it.
2. Rizoma number plate / indicator hanger.
3. Rizoma Zero indicators. Not, not cheap.
4. Rizoma flat, black, superbike handlebars.
5. New replacement footrests for rearsets, (after more than a few track crashes last year)
6. Motohooligan Motobox. Not cheap but best value buy of the year.
7. Motohooligan SAS block-off plates.
8. Dynojet PC111

Things that were good value;

1. Sebel Nautilus horn. Loud, loud, loud!:o
2. Glass exhaust header wrap.
3. Front suspension air bleed valves.
4. Stomp grip panels for tank - wow this makes a difference for hard braking!
5. New carbon sleeves for rebuilt Akras. (see my thread for suppliers)
6. Rivets, nutserts and fitting tool for above.
7. Packing for above. Akra prices absolutely suck - do not pay!!

Mods done because of Forum influence;

1. That Motobox!!!! OMG! You all have to do it. Just for the sound!
2. SAS removal. Good riddance, pile of heavy junk.
3. The 1st and 2nd gear wire mod thing - just in case map and PC111 don't.
4. Resistor mod to go with SAS removal.

Other stuff;

Tried to address the gravel damage sustained from several offs at Brands Hatch last year. Bike is pebble-dashed like the shell of a deep space cruiser from a science fiction film. Gave up - it's a lost cause and I console myself that this adds overall character.

Bought a Skidmarx screen and a KTM touring screen but hated the look. Sold them on Ebay. Don't care how much wind blast they divert they are pig ugly and I can't do that to my bike. I love it too much. It's like dressing Arnie, the Terminator, in a fluffy, pink bikini. I'll grow stronger neck muscles thanks.

What I need to do before the sun comes out;

1. Do an oil change!
2. Get a custom map for the PC111. Doing it at HM Racing.
3. Fit the race shock and get it sorted for my fat arse.
