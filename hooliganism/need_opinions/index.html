This is my view. I have a full titanium Akra system which I believe is probably the best exhaust for overall gains on the SD. But when FMF released their carbon pipes I was a bit envious because they definitely look better. So it depends on whether you want ultimate performance or looks but really the difference in HP is probably negligible and the difference in look is personal and purely subjective.

The other factor is track days. Here in the UK many tracks have a 103decibel, (or even less) limit and they noise test so you won't get on if you exceed this. My system just makes this limit but if I put in the baffles then it drops to about 95 or something and I can get on any tracks I want. From what I understand the FMFs will no way pass this test so for that reason alone I wouldn't have them.
