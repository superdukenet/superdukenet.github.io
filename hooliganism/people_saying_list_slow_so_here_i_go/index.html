Tail light's not been working . Put new fuse in ( was blown ) and all seems well while bike just sitting pressing brake levers.
However go for a ride ( whatever ) and notice tail light out again. ( Only thing not working ).....

Disconnect the rear tail light harness from the connector under the seat to see if it would still blow a fuse - sure enough - as soon as i turn key on it blows. ( FUSE 3 )...

Again , i'm not an electrical engineer and don't usually do well with electrics ( Getting better tho ) - what could be the problem - do i have to trace every wire to find a short somewhere ?

with the tail light connector separated i put a test light on the main harness tail light connector and it gets one hot wire . I suspect the issue if in front of that main tail light connection under the seat.

Now here's the other part of this - a simple ( seemingly should be simple ) tail light fuse results in me taking the whole tail section off the bike to get rid of that silver - gona black it out. and polish the rear exhaust "Y" section while i'm at it.

How would i go about finding the short though ( if it's even got a short ).... i guess i'll have to dig out the wiring diagram again....

cheers - have a good one.
