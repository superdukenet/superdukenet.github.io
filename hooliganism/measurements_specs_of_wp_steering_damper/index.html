I have the WP damper. It's attached to my bike so I'm not sure how I can accurately measure it. I will tell you that there is little tolerance to play with. The damper rod comes very close to the radiator at full lock. In fact, I think you lose some turning radius when you install the damper. (I didn't install mine myself, but I think that's the case. It seems like to bike has less lock to lock than it did without the damper).

Based upon my experience, my advise would be to learn from my mistake and get a Scotts damper.
