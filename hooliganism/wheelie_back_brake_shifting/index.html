Regarding the back brake, it's not that powerful but it stopped the couple almost-loopy ones ( ) I've had in the past. Just stamp down hard, it works! And I suppose the engine braking we get from the big twin also helped!

Regarding shifting, are you using the clutch? I've never changed gear while wheelie'ing, but have heard it's better with no clutch; just preload the shifter, close the throttle a bit and at the same time slip the gear in and get back on the gas. Also heard do this while the wheelie's still climbing to not lose too much height and/or momentum. Best to practice clutchless shifting on the level if you haven't done it before...

But like I said, I've never changed gear during one; from what I have read, hopefully Sabre or Bigtoby will chime in...
