It's a cracking little bit of kit. Loads of screen colours. Shifts lights are adjustable etc.

It is wired-
Red to ignition live
Brown to good earth (you'll have to extend this wire down to the battery, yoke isn't a good enough earth.)
Blue goes to the blue/white wire from ecu to tacho (3pin plug on right)
Green goes to speedo pulse signal. Just put the new mask on and forgot already. It'd either the black/orange or the brown/black in the 3pin plug coming from speed sensor. Just poke an led test light up the back of the plug with it all plugged on and ignition on and turn the wheel. You'll see a faint on/off of the led lamp as the bolts pass the sensor when your on the right one.
The white is for set up. You touch it to earth to cycle through the options and set everything up. I still have to set it up for gears but not got a clutch master cyl yet so can't ride it. Dunno how accurate it will be.

Spent a lot of time properly bleeding out the clutch and brakes to get the levers feeling good,

Removed the secondary flies and shafts. Another very good mod, bike picks up quicker off the throttle and has a noticable amount of low-mid rpm power

Might just be in my head but it feels lighter. Had a few mid hight wheelies and just kept snicking it up the gears with the quickshifter an it felt effortless where as before it sometimes felt like it needed a bit of a handfull to keep it up there.

Very happy with it.
