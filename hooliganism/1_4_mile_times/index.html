Joely Boy

2008-03-19 13:47:00 UTC

I've run a 10.64 at my local track - OK it's an old runway which has a bit of a downhill run: see:
http://www.superduke.net/forum/album_pic.php?pic_id=747

My bike is standard apart from cans/remap.

http://www.celticimaging.co.uk/pmsd1/index.html

To put it into perspective, I ran against a 957cc Fireblade & beat it (just), beat a FZ1 Fazer by about 3-4 bike lengths. The best bike on the day was a ZX10R with extended swinging arm and strapped down forks, he did a 10.20.

The performance was a surprise to me, the first run of the day, I lined up against a Buell City X and he ran a high 13's, on this run my time was was 11.08, this run was a little embarrasing (looked like I was 'picking' on the Buell). - edit - the Buell rider posted a clip on Youtube:
http://uk.youtube.com/watch?v=qqiCnJsCU0o

I had no trouble launching the bike at all, it was easier to launch than my old GSXR1000. OK I'm just under 12stone but I think my speed hillclimbing background helped getting the bike off the line, in hillclimbing I used to race a modified CR500 'crosser and where times are measured to the 1/100th of a second - my father was also competitive in Hillclimb and he embarrassed quite a few sportsbikes at the drag racing on his FZ1 Fazer.

Again for comparism, at the same track I previously ran a 10.8 on my 918cc carb model Fireblade (stock apart from dynojet,K&N & can).
