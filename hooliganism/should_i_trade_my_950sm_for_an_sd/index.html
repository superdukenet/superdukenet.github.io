Tricky one.

I've owned a 950SMR and a SD.

Sd has great points, so does the SMR. Both have downsides too.

SD fuelling is crap at low speeds. SMR's is nicer.

SMR is slow and underpowered compared to a SD... quite considerably to be honest.

Windblast is same on both.

Handling, well depedns what riding position you prefer to be honest. Both are pretty good.

Out of the 2... Hmmmmm
