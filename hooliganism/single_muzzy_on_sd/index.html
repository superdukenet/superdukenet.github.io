Single Muzzy on SD

ktmguy

2009-12-04 08:15:00 UTC
Hi, some pictures of this week's project. Started with a Muzzy carbon can for a 04 model ZX10. I had to make a new connection pipe as the original titanium link was never going to fit

Started with a 63mm Dia U shaped stainless steel premade bend. Measured it and cut in to pieces.

Measuring and tape them together with electrical tape to make initial shape. Got a professional welder mate to TIG it together as my welding is crap.

Started it up and so loud that I had to put earplugs in.... obviously designed for a bike with a cat in it or so.... Awwwww!!! (and I like loud)
Quickly made a baffle from a left over piece of tube. I make a proper one next week.
