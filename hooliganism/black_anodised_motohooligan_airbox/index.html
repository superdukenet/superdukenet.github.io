cdv478

2011-04-09 22:00:00 UTC

I ordered the black anodised airbox. Yesterday it arrived and I installed it last night with the new secondary flies, SAS blockoffs and SAS resistor. I don't have the 02 sensors disabled yet, but I do have the Akra map, and the the bike feels smoother. The thing sounds amazing. There weren't any photos of the black airbox, so I attached a few. I also attached a photo of the new airbox with the original. It's amazing how much stuff there is in there from the factory, and this photo doesn't include the SAS sytem because I hadn't removed it yet. All in all, I'm really impressed with this mod.

Matt_black, you are right. The stock snorkel is tiny. I'll do a search to find your airbix photos. I like looking at every install I can find to see what ideas people have that I might be able to apply to my bike.

Loony888, I thought the same about contrast with orange. Unfortunately you can only see the bottom of the airbox so it isn't that noticeable. I really like the look of the bare aluminium, but I figured the black would match the rest of the bike. Seeing the open throttle body area and the bottom of the airbox are subtle but very nice aesthetic improvements.

I rode it again today, and the throttle is definitely smoother even though it still has functional 02 sensors. It definitely has less engine braking effect, and the SAS removal got rid of the exhaust popping on decel as expected. Removing the 02 sensor input is my next goal. Even though I don't yet have the benefit of all supporting airbox mods, the Motohooligan airbox has made a great improvement in the refinement of the bike.

2011-04-13 14:04:00 UTC

After a few days of riding with this new airbox, I have to say its the best mod I've done. The bike is much smoother at low rpm (that's probably partly attributable to the new flies) and seems stronger above 4k. 02 eliminators should arrive and be put on today and then I think the snatchy throttle will be 100% gone. I already find myself able to lope along in 3rd at 25 mph with no lurching or bogging... could never do that stock. I have the Akra map and will eventually have a map made, but I think I will be really happy with it as is once the eliminators are installed.
