Is it one of the new hybrid SDRs with the dual seat?

Unfortunately I think the demand for this model will be less than the last of the true SDRs. They will always be seen as a bit of a parts bin special without the cudos of the single seat version.

But you're always going to take a big hit buying new and selling with low mileage. You just need to find the right buyer who appreciates that it's nearly new and wants a dual seat anyway because of pillion requirements. You just need to check what's the cheapest you could pick up a new SDR for and undercut this price by a good grand or more. Bear in mind that many dealers will do a deal to sell a new bike and add on kit and extras as an incentive. I got a set of Akras, carbon bits and a free service when I bought mine in' 07.

Best thing is to post up pictures on here and see if there is any interest.
