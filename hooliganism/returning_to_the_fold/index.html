Dude - how wicked was the maintenance on the SXV?

Regarding the PowerCommander and DynoJet's version of a quickshifter: you won't get better answers than from the factory direct. Seriously, call them up - they're helpful and friendly folks. You will definitely need a PC to use their quickshifter - it doesn't work alone.

Do you need a PC for an exhaust change? Depends on the change (to what), but probably not. Don't know how long you've been away, but search the site for "TuneECU". Buy/Make the cable; do your own map loading and modifications.
