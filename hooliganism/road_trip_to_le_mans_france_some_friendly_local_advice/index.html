Road trip to le mans (france) some friendly local advice

cloonster

2011-06-16 13:44:00 UTC
Yo

Just got back from a road trip to le mans (france). First of all i heard great stories about the roads in france and i wasn't let down, smooth tarmac and hardly any traffic around, awesome

About 30 miles from le mans we hooked up with a french rider who was heading down to le mans too, and we pulled up in a small town for a break. Really good bloke and he gave us the low down on what and what not to do.

WORD OF WARNING for anyone going to france, if you exceed 130kph you wheels get taken away!!!!! to be honest until we met the french guy i think our average was way over 130kph. He also mentioned that its pretty safe to get on the gas in winding corners but as soon as you hit the straights keep it down.

With this in mind, our ride back was a more chilled affair, although we did step on it at certain safe points.

Adding to this, I had to do an installtion in london a few weeks ago and was up and down the M3/A3 into london a few times. I got pulled twice for speeding and was let off.

My job allows me to work from anywhere over the net so i am starting to look at property on the isle of man.

This speed thing is getting me down man.

Anyhow just some advice folks
