Oil Consumption advice

cloonster

2012-04-02 09:07:00 UTC

Hello

Have had the SD since approx 1400 miles and right from the start i have been concerned about the oil consumption.

at the 4k service i mentioned it to the dealership and they stated I should take readings on how much oil I am using.

I am using silkolene 10w/50 4T and have worked out roughly that i am using 65ml/100 miles. this seems to me a lot and I am aware they can guzzle oil, however my mate (who runs a bike garage) always tells me that he can smell oil when i pull up or when trailing me out on a spin.

Additionally, when parked up in the garage and I rev her, always after a de-throttle a puff of white smoke emits.

She recently had her 11k service and i mentioned it to the dealership again, who said they have emailed KTM HQ but i dont appear to be getting anywhere with them and i want to resolve it once and for all namely because,

1.) am tired of having to always fill up with oil.
2.) possibly losing compression and ultimately performance.
3.) have a MH airbox en route and warranty void once fitted

I have now asked the dealership to find out what compression psi/bar the cylinders should be achieving in order for me to test myself as that seems to me to be the most logical way of seeing whether i have an issue or not.

I have also now purchased 15w/50 in attempt to higher the lower watermark temp of the oil to see whether that makes a difference.

All that said, i am after some advice really as i want to lay this to rest and either suck up the fact that is how much oil she uses or get it sorted.

So if anyone knows what compression the cylinders should be achieving I would really like to get me hands on that info, additionally any other advice or similar experiences would be much appreciated.

Thanks in advance.

Cloonster
