You can also find good deals on used exhaust on Ebay. I bought Akra Evos for $700, and while that isn't cheap, its better than the $1700 msrp, and I like how they sound without baffles. I would expect the FMFs to be more affordable used, but they seem less common, so I'm guessing they could be tough to find used.

Although many SD owners don't care about mileage, I have 8500 miles now and have kept track of mine since the first tank of gas because if it drops drastically, I know something is going out of adjustment or wearing out. With a MotoHooligan intake and flies, O2 eliminators, Akra map, and Akra Evos, I saw no decrease in mileage, but throttle response is far smoother. What did change my mileage was moving to San Diego from Fallon, NV. In Fallon, which is open, sparsely populated and flat, I regularly got mileage on the low 40 mpg range and sometimes as much as 45mpg. In San Diego I only get in the low 30s because I'm either lane splitting around 25mph or going 80, and even with lane splitting, my bike idles a lot more in traffic here. I don't care about the mileage drop because I love riding this thing here, and congested, hilly, curvy roads feel like what this bike was made for.

Good luck with the FMFs. I think they look awesome.
