2900 mile trip on SDR

cdlabate

2010-06-03 16:36:00 UTC
Just returned on an epic tour.

from CA to grand canyon, arizona and all the national parks in Utah and crossed over to colorado route 141. amazing scenery and roads in excellent condition.

I was too cheap to buy a radiator guard and it nearly ruined the whole trip. a rock had dinged the radiator resulting in a small leak. I rode about 7 hours and realised the coolant reservior was empty and radiator about half full.

luckily we were at a fuel station in a small town (cedar, UT) and found bar leak additives in an autoparts store. leak sealed right up and I continued.
