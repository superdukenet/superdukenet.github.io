Ducati Pete

2011-05-16 17:01:00 UTC
Hey fellas. Yes, me and Weeksy have simultanously discovered the merits of the Ducati Sports Classic 1000 Biposto with the proven 1000cc desmo engine that powers most of Ducatis current air-colled fleet of bikes. It's quite a rare beast - which I love - and few people recognise it while most think it's an old bike until they see the 09 plate.

Clutch is light, throttle response is crisp, v-twin rumble is deep and meaty, the seat is comfy, the riding position is tucked-up (but not as bad as most modern sportsbikes) and the power is a modest 92bhp at the rear wheel (so far heh heh) but she's tuneable, tweakable, and can be made very pretty and quick indeed.

I've had the bike three days and already have the cosmetics on order. She'll be black n' gold by the end of the month, with sorted suspenders and tail tidy with mini indies and decent Dunlop rubber.

It's a real hoot to ride, can fit the missus on the back, and compliments the SDR nicely.
