I always run premium unleaded RON95 or 98 here as the 'generic' fuel in Oz is only 91, whereas in Norway (and Europe I belive) the minimum sold is 95 which is the recommended standard for the SD.

I stay away from Shell as well, especially the v-power as it has cleaning agents in it that doesn't agree with motobike engines (especially sensitive v twins apparently) I was also told to stay away from it by the dealer when I bought my fireblade, if I had to use Shell, use the premium 95 only. Several bike mags here have also complained about its effect on their test bikes (I belive that was the 1098). They had to run a couple of tanks of BP 98 to make it run properly again..
