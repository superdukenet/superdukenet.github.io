I have looked into this in the past as I wanted an ali tank to polish and offset my obsession with black.

I even went as far as making up the flat sections of the pattern that would be required to fold into shape and then weld together to create an accurate copy of the standard '07 tank. Although it looks coplicated the angular, facetted design makes replicating it in sheet a simpler task than if it was all compound curves and surfaces. however, for all I know a good modern fabricator would make this with pressure forming or something using larger sheets of ali and not create it with sheet.

I was always expecting to have to pay upwards of £700+ for a custom Ali tank, maybe even a grand.

Anyway, I'd be very interested if there was a group buy to keep costs down.
