I know of a good exercise device that will help you recover. You might have seen them, or will see one. Here is what you need to make it. A set of your favoret m/c hand grips, a 32 inch long tube of 7/8 inch hollow tube stock, and a 5 foot long piece of rope. Tie the rope to the middle of the bar and duct tape it so it will not spin on the bar. Secure the grips to the bar. You will have to make up the diference between the loud handle side (right) with more duct tape. Tie the other end of the rope to a gallon milk jug and add water as you gain strength. Hold the bars straight out in front of you and roll the milk jug up and down. TWIST AND SHOUT
