Whacky bloke at the lights...

ShadeTheChangingMan

2008-11-11 20:21:00 UTC
so, I'm riding home via the Highway (heading east from Tower Hill [London for the foreigners here]). Get to the lights at the opening of the Limehouse link and some geezer rolls up next to me and immediately flips his visor and says "you got a death wish mate???" Stunned, I reply "no"....."the way you were riding that thing (mimics heavy rolling on/off of throttle etc) you are gonna kills yourself!!!!" etc etc. I'm so stunned i didnt tell him to go fook himself or punch him in the face...but I cant for the life of me think why he was saying all this to me.

I'd only been filtering like ALL the other bikes, stuck behind two scooters so I wasnt exactly pushing it. I've not ridden in the dark in the city yet so wasnt totally comfortable yet...taking it moderately is how I'd describe my style. I couldnt think how he could have mistaken me for another bike unless he was deaf and blind.....or retarded....it played on my mind all the way home even tho I tried to not think about it. I think i was more annoyed I didnt get to challenge his accusations (the lights changed and I just rode off)...but I'll be keping an eye out for him next time I'm there at the same time of day.

What an arsehole he was.
