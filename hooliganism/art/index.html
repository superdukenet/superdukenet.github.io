Funny this topic should come up now. I've only had the SD a couple of months and Being a shift worker,only ride on week days normally.

As it's been raining a fair bit here lately, I haven't gone out. Saturday was fine and I wasn't working so I was off like a shot.

Stopped for lunch at a cafe in Canungra where all the weekend riders usually stop, and I couldn't believe the amount of guys stopping
as they walked by ,to check out the SD, making comments to each other and even taking photos with their phones.

I've never had a bike that drew that sort of attention, and I was feeling pretty smug as I sat back finishing my pie.

I'm sure the sound of the Yoshis got a bit of attention as I left too.
