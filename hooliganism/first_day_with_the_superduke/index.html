first day with the superduke

rigga

2006-09-10 23:01:00 UTC

Well went on a 400 mile round trip yesterday to take my buell from birmingham down to plymouth to sort out a part ex deal on a bike.... upshot was i came away with a mint 05 matt black superduke strapped in the back of the van

This afternoon once some free time had been earned off the wife ( involved spending money on her natch ) decided to go for a blast on the new toy to see if all the hype was justified,suited up,helmet on,hit the button...... nothing i left it for half an hour,enough to charge it to start,fired the bike up and checked the charge volts so that side is ok,refitted the chin spoiler and off we went

BLOODY HELL WHAT A LAUGH

This has got to be the best bike ive had,i normally take a few rides in order to feel at home on a bike,but an hour or so later im flying into islands at speeds higher than my usuall rate of knots and grinning from ear to ear,what a hoot,the seating position and high bar gives a lot of leaverage to throw the bike around,power is good as standard and found from the buell i was under revving by a long way ( not used to bikes revving so high )slightly flighty at the front when really pushing it over roads that are not ultra smooth,but nothing a relaxed grip on the bars didnt solve,the sport suspension settings seem fine for me,and i was very impressed to say the least from my short time in the seat
Just two points i found out
1) Standard exhaust has to go...... far too muted and can feel it holding the bike back
2) Brembo front brakes feel wooden to me with reduced feel to those that i am used to,it did get better as i progressed so guess its something i will get used to,power is there in spades,just not as subtle as my previous bike with 6 piston calipeer and rim mounted disc

And to the guys on here who used to have a Buell.... and there are a few i recognise

What the hell were we thinking about!!!!

martin
