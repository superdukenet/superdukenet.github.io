A danish guy named Kleemann, known for his compressors for Mercedes cars, took a swing at modding a Harley Davidson V-Rod.
Unique look, I'm not sure I like it.. still too much Harley, and it's heavy at 250KG DRY!

In terms of competition for the Superduke's ethos and target market, I've come across:

Bikes that actually work:

Aprilia Tuono (old or new versions) - Nice and powerful, but handling isn't as good. Old Tuono is too long. New one is too tiny.
Ducati Streetfighter - Nice bike, and one I would consider. Not quite as pretty as the Superduke.
Triumph Speed Triple - Lovely engine, Great brand, not quite got the chassis parts, but a real contender.
...and maybe the Ducati Monster 1100S (full Ohlins, yum) - Better than you'd think from the originator of this class.

I'll ignore the Honda CB1000R (yawn) & Z1000 (yawn wider)

These are a nice idea, assuming they don't break down:

Moto Morini Corsaro 1200
Benelli Tornado (as you mentioned)

Not sure I'd include the Aprilia Dorsoduro, as it's more like a Supermoto or Big Trailie, competing with KTM's 990SM and SMR...
