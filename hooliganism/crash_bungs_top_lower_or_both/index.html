Rigga, i have the R+G swing arm bobbins, front fork protectors, bar ends and engine/frame uppers.

I have also tested them in a low speed high side, all the crash proctectors on the side that went down took damage including the clutch cover, riders and pillion foot pegs and hangers, exhaust heat shield and brake lever.

I reckon if i'd had the engine lowers fitted, i may have saved the damage to the clutch cover and riders foot peg and hanger.

But what ever happens, if it goes over at low speed, the pillion peg/hanger and exhaust heat shield will take damage, if you have removed the pillion pegs and fitted aftermarket exhausts, i think i can safely say you will trash the exhaust.

The R+G protectors saved the r/h front fork, swing arm, side panel, radiator and oil tank from some serious grazing damage from sliding along the road.

I've now replaced the damaged protectors and fitted the engine lowers aswell. And i consider the couple hundred quid i've spent a good investment against the cost of repairing or replacing forks, swing arm, radiator, oil tank or body panels in the event of a not so serious crash.

I do agree, they do look fugly but they will save you money and like i do with all my bikes, i remove them when i sell the bike and sell them off seperately, you'll always sell crash protectors.
