I lowsided without any sliders and the engine case didnt get touched at all. The handlebars (even though the mount bent and the bars wounded the tank) saved the engine and fork ends from touching down. I had stand spools on the back and they were mashed so I suspect the swingarm might have taken a scrape if they werent there.
The front side tank panel took most of the damage, then the headlight shroud. Bar ends, mirror and bars were total loss. Gear shift lever snapped. Pegs (both pilot and pillion) were scraped and a little bent.

In saying that, i'm getting sliders now, it could have been so much cheaper to fix if I had them.
I had the same thought that the lowers dont do anywhere near as much as the uppers do and unless it cartwheels and the bars snap, the fork end sliders are probably not critical either.
