Now for the contra view.

I am already on here talking about an uneasy feeling about Lagunas maidstone.

I was booked on a trackday 11/8 at Brands. On Sunday 10/8 I decided to change to wet wheels as is was p*ssing down and the forecast was same for Monday.

All went well until refitting from caliper when I snapped a bolt in half. I got the broken bit out but didnt have a replacement.

I should mention at this stage that my GSXR 750 K3 is the last of the pre radial brake models. Any caliper bolt from a GSXR600 / 750 or early thou would have done. As would one from a Bandit or similar.

I knew the answer before I phoned but called Laguna anyway (No other Suzuki dealer in Kent open on Sunday). Not in stock, £2 something. The teenage moron on parts could get me one for Tuesday. I have fell for this before. It doesnt mean next Tuesday it means some indeterminate time in the future, assuming they dont forget, that may or may not be a Tuesday.

As I was desperate I went down anyway. Which caused me to miss the start of the MotoGP race All I needed was a sensible mechanic or salesman I would happily give him £10 for a secondhand bolt from anything they had out the back. I took a ratchet and would have got it myself and paid for the priveledge.

No mechanics in cos it is Sunday. I spoke to Liam who said 'they would have helped BUT the boss was around and he doesnt give a f*ck about customers' or words to that effect.

SO F*CK EM. I used my fireblade for the trackday and I promised myself I will NEVER buy anything from Lagunas again. Not a bike, a jacket, a 10p washer. If they are the cheapest price for a new bike I will pay extra elsewhere or go without.

Furthermore, I will go out of my way to tell anyone I speak to what a uselsss bag of sh*t they are as a dealer. Although most of Kent seem to know already.
