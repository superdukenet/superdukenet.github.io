Gents,

i cant believe that i have not already posted this before (was sure that I had but obviously not). Back in october Indy and i went to Procycle dyno and had our bikes tuned (front and rear cylinders as well as ignition mapping). He also let us do a whole heap of testing on the DVS in varying guises, the guy (Andrew) is very knowledgeable and was the Dynojet instructor for the east coast until he started his own shop (so he is very happy to answer questions).

He was more than happy to use tune ECU and thought it was great as it was allowing more people to get dyno work done that would not normally go the whole hog (power commander and mapping). He also thinks that tuneECU is a great tuning program (little slow compared to tuning a PC3/5 but very powerful). Got our bikes running better than ever and was a really good bloke to deal with.

Details:
Pro Cycle Dyno

Address: 4/3363 Pacific Highway, Slacks Creek, QLD
Ph: (07) 3808 4003

Really good bloke and built/raced his own drag bike.
In the ANDRA Prostock Motorcycle Championship he won the 2009/10 season with a best time of 7.24 @ 183mph over the quarter mile, with a 1.07 60ft time!!

Cant really say much more other than great guy, happy to answer all questions (and for those of you who know me i ask a lot!), very talented tuner and likes tune ecu
