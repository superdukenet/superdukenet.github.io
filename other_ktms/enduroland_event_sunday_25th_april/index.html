Colonel_Klinck

2010-04-26 21:00:00 UTC
Twas an awesome day although I feel like I used to after the 1st rugby game of the season. My poor, not so young anymore body

Amazingly it was dry and didn't rain a drop all day. As we left London it started raining and was a monsoon all the way until about 15 miles from Warwick and it dried up

Dave had his new 08 450 EXCR to play on after ebaying the 400XR after just 2 days on it. We did tell him kick start is going to do your head in. Somehow he got nealry Â£200 more than he paid for it.Some poor ebay sucker At least he seems a lot happier on the EXC than the XR

First 2 laps were rubbish. Felt totally alien again and I was riding like a girl which isn't the way to ride a dirt bike. Weight over the back wheel and pin it That was an awkward 1/2 a lap back to the pits.

Took on the water hazard after straightening the front wheel back in line with bars. No problems first time and went through and up, cool and the gang.

It was in the afternoon that my big one happened. Was hooning round trying to stay with Jason, which is easier said than done. On a bit of lane after a tight section and boom! Suddenly the bike goes from underneath me and I hit the deck like a sack of shite. This one did hurt. I pick myself up, a guy stops and gives me a hand. He tells me I hit a rock sticking out of the lane that I just didn't see. Hit that hard that the front tyre is flat from a compression puncture and the bars have reverted to an opposite direction again ) but crow bar sorts that out.

Ginge and Jason are stars and have my inner tube changed in 10 mins. Cheers guys I really appreciated that

I decide against my better judgement to do 2 more laps. Foolish really as I tore ligaments in my shoulder playing rugby, that is part of the reason I gave it up. I can feel I've tweaked them again but fook it, it will be ok. Anyway I head across the water hazard and fail to get up the otherside. Foot down but right arm/shoulder can't take the weight and both I and the bike end up in the river. Fooking soaked Ginge and Jason are behind me and must be pissing themselves laughing. They get through and then help me out with the bike. That's the last time I'm going through there today! Manage to do the two laps but at a more sedate pace which doesn't feel right at all.

Was a fooking good day though. Everyone had fun. Ginge and Jason were as fast and competitive as ever

Next tiem out hopefully Dan will be back Ginge and Lee will be mended as well. Burger Bar Boys will be back in full force

Tell Jason thanks again for the inner tube. I'll sort him one out.

Oh I almost forgot... I see Ginge forgot to post a pic. Here he is tarting with Enduroland skirt
