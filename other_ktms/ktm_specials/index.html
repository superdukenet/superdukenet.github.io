I decided it was time for a ktm specials topic. So if you've got some nice pics (and info) of a nice KTM special: please share it with us.

The orange one is a bike made by Roland Sands, I've seen it a couple times in bike mags here in Holland, in short it's a 525 EXC which was customized by famous bike builder Roland Sands. Some pics:
(the blonde hotty is the owner btw )


The grey one is from Swiss bike builder Emil Weber. It's a 530EXC, stock suspension revalved reshimmed, leovince silencer combined with superduke headers, rizoma rearsets, lsl clip-ons, harley headlight, a company called Artek did the seat and the tank, wheels are forged marchesini's.
I prefer this over the orange one, the only thing that's missing is the spoked wheels from the Orange one.








It's for sale for as little as 24,500 euro
