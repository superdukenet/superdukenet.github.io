I have taken a few days and given this some attention in mind. The question is why do they appeal, not do they appeal. I would venture to guess that most peoples first experiances with firearms is with their dad or other responsible member in their immediate surroundings. There is an emotional connection that brings back good tymes. In many a case that was a right of passage for a youngster and the sporting and killing of ones dinner has deep ties to, you will be able to fend for yourself. This forum is about sporting, internal combustioned driven, device. Many extrapolate on the packing of more explosive packings inside the SD and in certain ways the two share common traights. When You pull the trigger at the apex and you are tottally in the NOW, you are experiancing nothing differant than sighting the ass end of a giant rooster that just scared the living into you. There are few that would dissagree that the smells that run through your nostrils, while taking a ride through the country are a huge sensational treat. Ask a hunter, with his/her eyes closed, what hunting season it is at I bet after one big whiff, they will be able to tell you. Most people are ready and willing to share thier craft or hobby and I have no doubt that with a few postings on Craigslist You will be in-un-dated with quality marksmen that would love to show you the ropes. Appeal, is in spades. Now go pull that trigger.
