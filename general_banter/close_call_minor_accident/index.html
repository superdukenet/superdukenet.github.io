Stupid lady was looking down into her lap at her phone and drifted right into me while I was in the carpool lane. I slammed on the front brake to try and escape (too high a gear to accelerate out of the way) but she still ended up hitting me with the left side of her car behind the rear wheel. I accidentally locked the front wheel up and she hit me while it was locked up, the front wheel swished around. I kept the bike up though, I knew if I went down it'd be really bad news. She goes, "Why are you looking at me like that, Im calling the cops"
Me, "Call the ****ing cops"
(I was giving her some serious death stare)

CHP dude gets there, takes my statement in like 2 minutes, then proceeds to talk with the lady for a good 15 minutes. Idk what was being said but they were both smiling a lot, laughing together, etc. I couldn't believe it. I feel like this bitch is going to somehow get me at fault for her bullshit.

All that made contact was my frame slider, it's bent pretty badly.

There is old damage on her car right above where she hit my slider. Looks like shes trying to use me to take care of an old issue.
