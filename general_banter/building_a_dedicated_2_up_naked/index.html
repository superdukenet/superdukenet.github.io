What sort of riding will this bike be for?
My SDR handles my weight fine (116kg + gear).

Will this still be a hooligan bike, or purely for 2 up touring?

Zx14 does well with 2 people on it, as does the Suzuki GSX1400. We did over 4000km 2 up in 6-7 days. That was both of us, plus 53L top box fully loaded, 2x 25L panniers and a tank bag, so well over 200kg and it was great. SWMBO says that it is so much better to have a grab rail rather than a seat strap to hold onto.

Other ones to look at are the 1050 triumph tiger, Hayabusa is comfy for both rider and pillion.

But with the amount of weight you are talking pretty much anything will be fine.
