Location, Location, Location

Lowrance

2013-11-30 16:52:00 UTC

Once again it's is great to see all the new blood the 1290 is bringing into the fold of this well renowned forum known as Superduke.net. Arguably the finest bike specific forum on the interwebs. Also seeing some new names popping up with questions regarding the current bike...nice.

You will find this is probably the most passionate group of owners you'll ever come across. There is a wealth of knowledge in these anals including everything from the most mundane maintenace task to the most complicated troubleshooting and repairs. From basic mods to get the bike to run right on up to complete engine balancing and blueprinting to sqeeze every last 1/10th of a horsepower this motor is capable of (most of us landed somewhere in between).

We're a pretty tight knit group with the usual squabbles that inevitably transpire when a difference of opinion comes up but generally there is much respect between us (except for the occasional oddball that surfaces). Great to see all of the folks from various nations that enjoy the unique charachter of the SD represented here!

Which brings me to my point of this long winded post...update your profiles to include your locations if you haven't already (thanks Don, G'day mate). Doesn't have to be too specific but it let's us old crumudgeons know who we are conversing with and will make some of the help/questions your seeking/we provide (and vise versa) a little more usefull.

Cheers
