Due to rain, I got the first ride in today to scrub the Racetecs. They looked extra shiny, so was careful at first.

On the run home I had some pace on, not quite to the edges but I am feeling good on them.
They feel squishy and the bike "moves" a little due to side wall flex, but no loss of traction at all. The front is weirdly neutral, and you have to turn more deliberately than on some tyres.

Cold and windy today, I ran 32 and 30 psi so I might up the pressure for the next ride. Overall I'm happy and they remind me of the supercorsas, with good feel and feedback.
Front end twitchiness was fairly apparent today, probably due to grip confidence and pushing a bit more than I would have on the old shit M1's. Hmmmm me thinking of steering damper again!

Cheers
