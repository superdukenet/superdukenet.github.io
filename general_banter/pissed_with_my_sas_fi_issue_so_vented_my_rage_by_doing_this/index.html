Steven's Cola Ribs!



1 slab of pork ribs.
1/2 litre of coca-cola.
3 table spoons of hickory bar-b-q sauce
3 table spoons of garlic powder.
2 table spoons of crush garlic.
1 tea spoon of cumin.
1 table spoon of ginger power.
2 table spoons of salt or to taste.
1 bunch of cilantro finely chopped.
1 chopped onion.

Mix all the ingredients in a pot, cut the ribs into single ribs and marinate in mixture for 1/2 hr.



Cook on stove at medium heat with no lid. Cook until most of the coca-cola has evaporated. once they are ready go ahead and sprinkle the cilantro on the ribs, cover the pot and let sit for 10 minutes.

all purpose sauce.

1/2 bottle of Italian Zesty dressing.
1/2 cup of white vinegar.
1/2 tea spoon of ginger.
4 lemons.
2 whole onions.
1 red bell pepper.
1 Bunch of cilantro.
2 tomatoes.
1 bunch of scallions.
1 8oz can of tomatoe sauce.
1 table spoon of mustard
2 havaneros.
1 jalapeno.
3 tahi peppers.
2 cayenne peppers.

Chop all vegetables very fine. chop only the stems and a little bit of the cilantro leaves, the rest of the leaves chop then up roughly and put in blender. Chop the white bottom portion of the scallions and a little bit of the green portion, the rest of the green gets chop up roughly and throw in blender. Chop 1 1/2 whole onions very fine, the other half gets chop up roughly then throw in blender. Chop all the hot peppers finely and trow in blender. Pour the vinegar, dressing and mustard into the blender. Tap on the blender bottom on and off until all ingredients are finely blended (do not exceed as to make it into juice, it should be a thick mixture), mix with the rest of the chopped ingredients, add the tomatoe sauce and squeeze lemons in. Mix well or shake if you have it in a bottle. And there you have it, enjoy.
