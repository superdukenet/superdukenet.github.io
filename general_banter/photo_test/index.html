Mr Brightside

I love Brands GP but you do get a bit passed on a Superduke on the back sections by the 1 litre 4's if you're not really trying hard. That's why the Indy circuit is so great because it's all about corners and not just speed.

I came to grief with Clearways because the inters. group I was with was a bit slower than normal and so I started overtaking round the outside rather than holding the inside line. I think I was on the negative camber bit and maybe also hit some loose stuff for a moment.

I can still remember the speed at which everything went bad. Been back since but it was wet and I never got my full confidence back for that corner.

I agree, Padock Hill is OK and really fast when you get used to it. Love the view as you hit the apex and sweep down that track!

Have to wait for next Spring now.

Twisted Jester

2009-12-07 20:39:00 UTC

Well, as we're clearing the air.......

Summer '97 and I'm approaching clearways and there's a pack ahead of me. So like others here I decided to take a very wide line and go 'round them.

All was going well until 3/4 round when a GSXR600 in front of me and to the inside had a bit of a moment and picked up. He drifted out in front of me and had a brake or hit him dilema.

I tried a bit of brake, but the combination of lean angle and being on the dirty part of the track ensured the front went. I was pretty cranked over so I didn't have far to fall.

I did a few cartwheels through the gravel, and the bike was so dug in it took 3 of us to lift it!

After that day I was always wary of just shooting 'round the outside.
