Here's a pic of my previous machine. She was beautiful and sounded like a demon, but she was also a real bitch to ride. The fueling was horrible (worse than the 990 SDR) until I uncorked her and tuned the ECU. I never did come to grips with the vague front end feel, ridiculous front brakes, and nervous chassis. After my second lowside due to lack of front end feel I decided to sell her. I literally lost the desire to ride after owning the 1098SF. In my opinion, my new to me 990 SDR is by far a superior motorcycle. It's more balanced, more stable, more predictable, more comfortable, more confidence inspiring, and MORE FUN. After a week of ownership I already feel like I can ride the wheels off this thing and my love of riding has returned. There's a reason the Ducati SF didn't sell well...it's because it kind of sucks. Keep your KTM's.
