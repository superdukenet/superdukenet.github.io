BMW S100RR..

Agent Orange

2009-10-01 09:04:00 UTC
HI guys,
went to the Aussie reveal of the bike the other day and I have to say I'm impressed! It is a very unique motorcycle and like one guy said at the launch - it looks like they've just created a very futuristic looking bike and slapped a BMW brand on it..

The bike looks great and will be serious competition to the RSV4, 1198S
and RC8R.. Mainly because it will be $26k on road (the others are $30k+ before onroads!).

I also has all the goodies -
electronic quickshifter, race ABS and
traction control that is linked to four different power modes from rain
to slick. The power modes change the throttle characteristics but also
adjusts the traction control and ABS! This means in rain mode the
traction control and ABS is a lot more sensitive than in slick, where it
is almost non-existent..

In addition it has wheelie control and even
launch control!

Lots of great techno stuff and all for the price far less the other
brands above.. If I were to get a track bike.. this would be a serious contender..
