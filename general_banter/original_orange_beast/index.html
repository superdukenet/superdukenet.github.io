My mate Dave had a 120 Jota which I rode around on for a while.

Heavy, high speed weave, high centre of gravity but it made one of the best noises I have ever heard and I really liked riding it.

Unfortunatly it was always breaking down. The carb sliders were cable activated and the vibration from the motor set up a resonance that shredded them. It was so bad that we carried spares on every ride and became proficient at roadside carb maintenence.

He got fed up and swapped it for an FJ 1200 or some thing like that

Funnily enough I am just back from a weekend in the Malvern hills organised as a birthday treat by my lovely girlfriend and we drove right past Slaters, creators of the original Jota. They are still open for business and had a couple of nice bikes in the showroom. I took a picture and may upload it later.
