I love things mechanical, including guns, but limit my interest to air guns. My father had the job of shooting the main guns on a battle ship and later spent quite a while roving round the sea exploding old mines with anti aircraft guns after the second world war so perhaps I got the love of it from him.

As a child it was somehow discovered that I had a talent for it so I ended up shooting everything from skeet through running boar to hand guns and long range target shooting doing OK along the way. Then a serious phase which involved as many of the government funded toys as I could get hold of and playing with things that explode. That was followed by some time with shotguns again bagging things that fly for the pot and butcher.
