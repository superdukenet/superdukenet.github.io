Love her... Hate her... the Great Experiment still survives.

I, as all Americans (even the Indians) are immigrants.

On my father's side the first Duke4Dirk immigrated to America from Germany in the 1850's and was a Stage Coach Driver. He made a family that later settled in Oklahoma.

On my mother's side they immigrated/ran away to America from Nazi Tyranny in Holland in 1940. My mother was 8 years old. They wound up in California.

In 1960 Duke4Dirk was hatched... in America.

It's been a great ride folks... this place has given me opportunity beyond belief. I've been self-employed most of my life and have reaped the rewards of hard work and dedication. I didn't become Donald Trump or Steve Jobs, but I've made my way through life on my own terms. I've raised a lovely family and now enjoy grand-kids on my knee.

So, for all you yanks out there that are feeling stressed out because things aren't so great or the future looks bleak... if a bonehead, overweight, near sighted, naive, KTM rider like me can make it here...well...
