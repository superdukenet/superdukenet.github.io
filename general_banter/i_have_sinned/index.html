I rode the very first edition in '09. I know that BMW subsequently played with the crank weight, but not much else until this iteration.

Though its been a while, from what I remember of my first ride compared to the 2012, there was a huge difference in steering. The difference is akin to the difference I found when I upgraded my stock GSXR suspenders to the state of the art Ohlins bits.

I'll be interested to see if you have the same impression after your ride. If I were you, and I was contemplating buying the shop's '11 red white and blue demo, I'd definitely try the '12 before you buy.

The one I'm looking at isn't a shop demo unit, but a private sale. I hear you on trying the 2012 first.
