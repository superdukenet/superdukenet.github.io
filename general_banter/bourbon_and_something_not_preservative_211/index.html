Ack! What are ya'll douhin' to yah fine wisskhey pourhin' that shugar in it?

Here's my take on the matter. If your going to mix it with anything other than, at most, a couple cubes of ice just buy any $15 bottle and you'll be fine. But if you want to taste what you paid for, like Aegis said, a cube or two of ice at most depending on what you bought. Makers Mark, Knob Creek, Wild Turkey, or Bookers are all fine daily drivers you cannot go wrong with (but there are alot of good bourbons in the $25 - $30 range). If you want to treat yourself to a "finer" bottle for not much more money try Woodfords Reserve, a Wild Turkey Rare Breed, or a Bookers 6 - 8yr old. I also had a Pappy Van Winkle 15yr old I really liked.
