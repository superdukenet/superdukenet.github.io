bic_bicknell

2011-01-30 21:20:00 UTC
Foot out of plaster so this weekend went for a 300 mile trip to Bristol for a party on Saturday night. Absolutely freezing, don't think the temperature rose above 1 degree all weekend. Forced to stop at Fowlers to buy some new under thermal gear to replace my old stuff and it was a bit better but could only do about 40 miles at a time before the cold really got to me and I'd have to stop to warm up. Those Akras come in handy as emergency warmers. Had so many layers on I could hardly bend my arms enough to do my helmet strap up. But it was worth it, great roads, no motorways, three tanks of petrol.

Also picked up some KTM gear for the twins when they are born. They WILL be fans! Cool
