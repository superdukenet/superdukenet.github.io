2013-11-06 16:50:00 UTC
My 2 cents on projector beams. I was going to use them on my SD build but took for ever to get to my address after order made, so I have a new set sitting in my spare parts bin. A stock Adventure headlight showed up so that's what I used, same as the SD. Main reason not to use projector beams is oncoming traffic has a harder time seeing the projector beams. I have them on my ST4S Ducati. My buds tell my lights are not on, when they are. Traffic infraction for no lights here in Oregon.
Another option is the LED lights you will see on the Adventure bikes. They are bright, which is a good thing for the blue hair drivers who want to make the left hand turn in front of you.
