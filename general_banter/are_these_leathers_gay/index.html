Are these leathers gay?

bic_bicknell

2010-05-19 20:40:00 UTC
OK, so maybe this should be an extension of my earlier post about Dainese leathers and my search for a (used and therefor good value) replacement to my shit Alpine Stars because the zips all broke. But I wanted to start fresh.

So, I'm kind of into black, understated leathers. Totally not into the neon, flashtastic, multi-colored stuff. Biking through in the early nineties put that all into perspective - I once owned a set of boots with pink, (YES PINK!) splattered patterns on it. Remember Foggy's leathers when he rode as a privateer in the IOM? Hideous!!!!

Found this two piece at the right price and the right size. But I really want just black - there's nothing out there though. Watching a couple of Wolf leathers suits that are black but they are both one piece and I already have a one piece for trackdays, what I need is a two piece.

BUT... is this black, white leathers thing gay or what? I put the question to the forum.

My partner, Amy, thinks these look cool. But I'm not so sure.
