Belly pan bling

bic_bicknell

2013-08-06 14:05:00 UTC
Here's my just arrived carbon bellypan. I got Skidmarks to lay up a carbon one instead of the usual GRP version.

Seems like it needs a bit of work to get it fitted, you need to drill all the holes and also it's made for the bike without a side-stand so I need to see whether this is possible to retain the stand. Seem to remember that Klinky has done this on his old bike.

Fairly good quality carbon, but not up to Akrapovic standards. Quite a few little air pockets where the first gel coat is missing but I'll just fill them with chemical metal before I matt it all down to fit with the rest of the bike.
