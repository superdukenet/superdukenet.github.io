TLS_Rus.....

Your list is awesome! I think we have had a parallel life - but I've not owned so many. Thing is, when you're younger it's not the bikes you owned really, more the bikes all your mates owned and brought round to your house for Sunday morning fiddling, and all the bikes you borrowed or rode pillion on. And all the bikes you sat in pub car parks dreaming about. And all the bike you saw getting crashed, and ruined and abused. And loved for and cherished. And all the mates you saw injured....or killed. (I lost seven mates from the 70's through to the mid '80s). I too list my bikes with the mods. and the memories and the injuries I sustained on them - they are inseparable.

But man, those RD Yamahas - I loved them. For me the aircooled ones were better than the LCs - 'cos I understood them. I have lost count of the hours spent with a drill and grinder messing about porting those engines. Ha ha we did not know what we were doing most of the time - ended up with very peaky engines. But I loved them. And Kawasaki triples too. They were my favourite. Rattling small bearings and oiled up middle cylinders. We all wanted, (and occassionally got), Swarbrick expansion chambers. They were miles better than the ubiquitous Microns and Allspeeds. Handmade stuff.

Anyway, respect mate.

PS. Oh, except Honda SS50s were really shit!! What you doing owning those!!! I had an AP50 but wanted an FSE. Now think I had the better one. But those Hondas were s....l....o....w!!
