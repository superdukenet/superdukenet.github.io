2015-04-23 19:48:00 UTC

The OE cover survives crashing well. It's also cheaper than any aftermarket solution shown. That's not to suggest that many aftermarket solutions aren't handsome.

You guys go on and on about crashability and survivability ... Do you really think that if you were to crash in a way to where it would grind through an OE cover - that with some heavy, overbuilt cover and/or protector that wouldn't necessarily grind through (most are untested) - that the bike would otherwise be rideable and that YOU would be able to ride it? You're kidding yourselves. Seriously.

See-through is bling, offering nothing additional functionally. If that appearance is important to you (that is, others able to see the clutch spin around when the bike is parked and engine running)- go for it. Want to see the clutch? Buy a dry-clutch Ducati.
