Went Helmet shopping today. Went to a large motorbike accessory shop near my place. They only sell gear, no bikes, great place to wander around. As I have a massive noggin I was a little bit restricted on my choice of helmets.
Was looking at some Shoei's, Arai and Nolan helmets. As usual the sizing was a joke. XL couldn't get past my ears. XXL just fit but pushed my cheeks into my eyeballs and my chin rubbed the chinbar. Ended up with an Rjays SP2 in XXXL. Yes lads that is 3 X's. Found it to be very comfortable , looks good (imo).

This picture doesn't quite do it justice because of the yellow lighting. It is Pearl white,black and a bit of silver. Total Price $349 AUD. A little cheap some may say, and I myself was at first a little skeptical. The assistant (an older biker looking dude) assured me it was a good helmet and it met all Australian standards



Anyway once I got home I decided to do a little research and found several reviews and ratings including this one
http://www.crash.org.au/ratings.php
Which rate the Rjays SP2 quite well
Seems expensive does not always = better and cheaper does not always = shithouse
Just curious what sort of lids you guys have and there pro's and con's, would you buy another one etc
Time will tell how well my cheaper helmet holds up to the rigors of regular usage. From all reports most people that own one are very happy with them

P.S. I need a large head to hold my massive brain parts (cough)
Huzzah to my fellow cranially well endowed brothers and nuts to all you pinheads
