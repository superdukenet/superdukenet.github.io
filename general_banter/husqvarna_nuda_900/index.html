My local dealer I do a few jobs for just got one in but it's sold so I can't test it.
I like the styling, a bit like the SD angular. Nice to see them step away from the "they all look the same" japonese style and getting away with it.
The only thing I personally don't like is the MASSIVE end can on the left. I'm sure there will be aftermarket ones soon to solve this. I don't think Husky is probably to blame for that but more the EU wanker mp's.
All up looks nice.

He will have an R in as a demo soon and I'll have a go at that and report back.
The dealers went for a demo weekend and he liked the way it handled, it is not as powerfull as the SD as he only managed to get 200 clicks out of it on the track.
This seems on par with the claimed HP figures.
Other similarities with the SD is the brembo brakes and supposed classy suspension out of the box. The R has Ohlins shock and sachs forks, the standard model sachs all around.
Here in Oz standard with pirelli rosso II's.
