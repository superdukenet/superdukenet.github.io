My 950 Adventure is 440+ lbs. I got knocked of it a year ago in traffic at 25 MPH, did a Super Man with out a cape and ripped out my ACL.
Tried to stay of dirt bikes last year to heal up. Took my 950 Adventure to our club ride so I would not be tempted to ride trail. Got the dirt bug bit real bad so I thought I just run down a little bit of trail, just a little, mind you. Front end washed out, head butted my wind screen with my lip/nose/teeth. Thought I knocked my teeth out / split my nose. Got back to event parking lot after another guy helped my pick my big dog up....fooker's are HEAVY....to get patched up, bloody mess, everyone looking.
Of all the bikes I have I will have to say I like riding my 300 2 smoker the best...hands down. I have a fantastic place to ride and been doing it the same place since 1977.... Not all have a place like this and I have worked my ass off for it.
Not a big deal trucking my bike to the ride area. And new real knobs are like Velcro. Riding road to get there will destroy them. I put new tyres on last Friday night and felt the tyres going off within 20 miles. A good starter size bike is the 250cc range. and some where in the 230 pound range. Rest is over kill.
