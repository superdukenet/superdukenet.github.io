It's time for another Opie Oils competition!

In something of a reprise of our competition back in June, we're giving away over £150's worth of motorbike care and cleaning products... but this time you stand more chance of a prize because there's the added bonus of 3 runners up prizes, each of a £50 voucher to spend at Opie Oils.
Free to enter, no purchase necessary.

Read enough? Scroll down for the link to the competition entry form, or read on for more details...

It's a great prize, containing everything you need to get your bike looking the business!

There's a wheel cleaning kit, polish, visor cleaner and a selection of aerosols - 16 items in all from Muc-off, Motul, Meguiars and Castrol.


How to win it?

Easy! Just let us know the make of the (or car, if you feel so inclined) and fill in the competition entry form with your answer. If you like,
you can tell us what model it is too, but you don't need to... and there are no bonus prizes!

It's free to enter and there's no commitment to buy anything, so give it a go!
You've nothing to lose... except, as we said before, a whole weekend spent cleaning and polishing your car if you're the winner

The competition's not open for long, so get your skates on... last entries by close of play this coming Sunday, 4th October 2009, please.
