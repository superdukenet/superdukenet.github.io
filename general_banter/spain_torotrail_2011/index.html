Ducati Pete

2011-05-15 21:19:00 UTC
One year for our (at the time annual) pyrenees road riding fest. and Catalan GP trip, 3 of us decided to give the Portsmouth / Bilboa ferry a miss and ride down through France.

We had a route that not only avoided any M way but only used a 60 mile stretch of dual carriageway, the rest was pretty small roads, some of them D roads. It was one of the best "getting somewhere" rides I've ever done.

2 fireblades and my GSXR -fuel stops were quick and we made good time. Off the ferry at Le Harve early, first night stop in Periguex and over the border and in Huesca at the end of day 2.

The weather was awesome, and that's the key.
I'd done similar trips before and if it rains through France it's shite.

Off course southern Spain adds another big chunk to the journey.
