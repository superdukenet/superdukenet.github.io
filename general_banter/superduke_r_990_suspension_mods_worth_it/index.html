The OE White Power rear shock and forks are good quality kit. KTM own White Power and it's quality stuff really with good adjustment.

But I just fitted a Wilburs rear shock and had it set up at HMRacing and it is quite noticeably better than what I've been used to. It's still firm with a 180 spring like the SDR shock I had on but it's way better in the compression and rebound settings and makes bumpy roads feel smooth instead of kicking you in the spine - which makes for a more controlled feel. I'm no expert but it just feels better quality but it should 'cos it was a load of money, even second hand.

HM charge abour Â£96 to set up your front and rear to match the type of riding you do and your weight in full kit. Best use of Â£100 I ever spent. I get it done every few years and always ride away and feel the improvement. I don't know what they do, it's all a bit of black magic really. (Or maybe only psychological but even that's worth it IMO )
