Sorry to hear about your bike. Sounds like you won't be seeing it again.
If you can, always chain it to something immovable, at least it can't be picked up or dragged. I had a bike stolen that just had a disc lock on it but they had a van. I guess disc locks are deterrents to the casual thief but If they want it they'll have it though.
With regard to insurance - I agree with Klinck - never accept the first offer. If you get into negotiation with the insurance company, send them examples of ads in MCN, Autotrader etc, preferably dealer rather than private sales.
It takes a while to get over the paranoia - its like being burgled.
