Finally. Been planning to do this for over two years but only got round to finishing today.

There's very few of these single seats available second hand or through e-bay or anywhere else so ended up buying new from KTM. Carbon seat unit through a German company. Quyality good but it wasn't the best fit and I had to do a lot of work to make the tail light fit, dremmelling, sanding, reshaping. This was made worse because I was fitting a bike monkey light unit and this was also slightly different from OE parts. So I got an after market carbon unit trying to mate up to an after market light unit. Double trouble, took a good few hours to sort this out. Added to that was the bike was dirty so all the bits that were transfered across needed to be cleaned up.

Anyway, now looks great I think.
