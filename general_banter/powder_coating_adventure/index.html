Powder coating adventure

bic_bicknell

2013-08-28 19:50:00 UTC

I've got a spare subframe and engine hangers that I keep meaning to get powder coated satin black but since I moved out of London I wanted to find a local place that was good and haven't had the time. Looked up a few possible names on google and found somewhere called Colour Coatings in Edenbridge which is only a ten mile blast down country lanes from me.
http://www.colour-coatings.co.uk/

Had the time today so set off with parts in a rucksack and high hopes. When I got there it looked a bit dubious, a few guys came over from a neighbouring unit and started looking at the SD and I wondered if it would have wheels when I returned. But when I walked into the office the first thing I saw pinned to the wall was a signed letter from Kevin Schwantz's GP team back in the day personally thanking this company for sterling work done for them! That's all the references I need!

When I said I wanted satin black finish they hmmed and haaad and came out to check the bike over, only to tell me I didn't want 'satin' finish, what I wanted was a 30% up from matt towards full gloss if I wanted to match most of the SD frame. So, satisfied that they will be doing a good job I left the parts and will be picking them up in a week.

I'll post up what they look like when I get them back.
