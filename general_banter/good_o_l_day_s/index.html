The old 500 was a stand out bike back then every were I would go there would be people hanging around looking at it made me a bit nervous worried some one would steal the thing. One time I even had salesmen in a Yamaha dealer ship come out of the shop and just stare at the thing asking me questions about it. Yes it was like being a GP star LOL!! The main reason that the RG500 didn't do real well in racing was that the top expansion chambers were way to close to the rear tyre there was about a 1/4" gap on each side so this restricted the size of the tyre you could stick in there.
On my bike I had 1/4" on one side and on the other side it was nearly touching so I packet the expansion chamber out with some washers. Suzuki quality control
