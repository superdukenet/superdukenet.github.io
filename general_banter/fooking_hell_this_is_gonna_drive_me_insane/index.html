dude, dude, dude... they usually tell you to ALWAYS check that any modification to bars, clipons, risers, ride height adjustment, does not foul and cables. always check by turning the bars lock to lock with the engine running before a ride. even if you take stock bars off and put them back the same way. take the time to do this. being human and trying to rush can make us forget this so always recheck!!! (obviously this applies to brakes as well as wiring etc)

as far as the tank goes. it is the little gas charged lift that keeps the tank elevated for servicing that is the culprit, it is very strong and still tries to do its job when bolting the tank back on. good idea, bad design. there is a thread on here (do a search, id do it for you but am strapped for time today) that addresses this. some guys just remove the thing and prop the tank up with a home made prop as necessary. i plan on doing the same next time i am under there.
